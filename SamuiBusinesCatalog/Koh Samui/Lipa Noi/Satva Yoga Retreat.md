### Top Reviews
- **Winter Ray** (5/5): "I didnt stay in their accomodation, but I lived near here, I went to join their communities where they throw Kirtan night every Sunday evening, there is free vegan light dinner afterwards, and Krishna Mantra Chanting music, and a little cute little dance when the live music gets groovy. You can sit, stand, dance, or not, all is welcome. I will miss living near Sattva! They also throw amazing Yoga+sound healing classes in the morning and evening by the beach every weekday except Wed; the evening classes especially are wonderful since it is by the beach where you see sunsetting. Beautiful, welcoming and peaceful. Their food is also very lovely and yummy, I had breakfast buffet right after the yoga class in the morning. Great experience! Thank you for everything."
- **Neer Komm (neeros)** (4/5): "A stunning resort right by the beach in Lipa Noi... The rooms need some renovation, but steel we loved the place. There are several kind of treatments there, together with the neighbor resort. Very recommended to try one of the panchakarma treatment."
- **Thilo Roh** (4/5): "The Resort is really beautiful and the beach is awesome! Great offerings for yoga, sport and healthy live style. The rooms could use a little love but you have everything you need. Do not expect luxurious interior. The environment is full of beautiful plants and the 2 pools are great. They offer shuttle service and private tours to see some places on the island. Only the breakfast is a little bit disappointing. Eggs and things like Cappuccino are being charged extra. In general the restaurant is very good with a mix of local and western oriented food. We went there every day. Location and beach are stunning especially the sun set due to it being located in the west of the island. Definitely check out the yoga courses!!! In total we felt welcome and the stay was very pleasant!"
