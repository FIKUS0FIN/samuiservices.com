### Top Reviews
- **Paulius Jakelis** (5/5): "We enjoyed our 3-day stay with the family. The resort is very quiet, and the grounds are beautiful and well-maintained. A few lovely cats live on the property. The beach is calm and beautiful, with two great hammocks and enough sun loungers for guests. There is also a nice pool, a beach bar, and a restaurant. It’s a perfect spot for a peaceful, relaxing holiday. There are only 11 houses on the property, which adds to the private and quiet atmosphere. The staff are friendly, always smiling, and very welcoming. The villa was very spacious – we loved it. Bicycles are available free of charge. The closest 7/11 store is around 900 m away. You can order Grab food or use Grab for transport. Breakfast decent just takes some time as you have to fill the paper and wait for the main dish.  On the downside, the property is old. Although it is clean, the shower and bathtub were not working properly. Hot water also kept disappearing during our stay, which was a big issue when traveling with a baby. The staff came and solved the problem quickly, but unfortunately, it kept repeating in the evenings. I also had one beer at the bar, and it was the most expensive beer we encountered during our 1-month trip in Thailand. Still, despite these small issues, we enjoyed our stay. If you’re looking for a spacious place for quiet holidays, this is a good choice. It feels like real, authentic Thailand."
- **Stu just travelling** (5/5): "The hotel and staff were amazing, room was absolutely massive…gardens well maintained. The only downside for me was location…being solo traveller it was a long walk to anywhere to get food or have a beer…and it was low season so many places had closed..a great stay for couples or families tho…"
- **Eskeatnaf Mulugeta** (4/5): "This place is located right next to the beach, offering a breathtaking and unforgettable sunset every evening. In the morning, the tide recedes and you can walk further out into the sea, while in the evening the water returns. Unfortunately, the sea here is not the most inviting for swimming.  I would hesitate to call this a true resort, but it is definitely a very nice place. It is well suited for families and couples looking for a peaceful holiday. However, if you are here to party, this might not be the best choice.  The rooms: In general, you get a spacious house with everything you need. The furnishings are a bit old and the property would benefit from a renovation.  The swimming pool: The pool is beautiful, large, clean, and well maintained, with a fantastic view of the sea.  Getting around: If you want to go into the city or anywhere else, you can easily book a taxi (via Grab or through reception). Bicycles are also available if you prefer to explore on your own.  The breakfast: Simple but sufficient.  The restaurant: The beachfront restaurant is fantastic. You can enjoy drinks, lunch, or dinner here. I highly recommend booking a table for a sunset dinner—it’s an amazing and memorable experience.  Although the property shows its age, it offers great value for money. The highlights are definitely the swimming pool and the stunning sunsets."
