### Top Reviews
- **Mister Benz** (5/5): "Amazing spa experience. The place is beautiful, calm, and very well designed, with a relaxing atmosphere from the moment you arrive. The facilities are clean and luxurious, and everything is perfectly organized. It’s the ideal spot to fully relax and unwind. Highly recommended."
- **V VV** (2/5): "I understand the concept that it is like a Bali massage/workout place. The facilities are very westernized - made for foreigners in Thailand. As a local Thai, it was really “weird” to experience a spa where the staff was mediocre for the price that they charge. The vibes are filled with gym bros and instagram girls. The massage itself was odd, there was only a fan turned on. The therapists (if you can call them that) weren’t experienced in any way. As a local Thai, this was really just a place catered to foreigners visiting Thailand."
- **Wunda Stone** (5/5): "Great experience at this relaxing spa. Neat, clean and cutting edge setup with lush greenery that adds to the island vibe. The cold and hot plunge pools are ample and thoughtfully designed, and well cared for with cleanliness and maintenance. The 2 sauna and steam rooms - as well as the ice baths and spacious bathrooms with lockers - are spanking new with serious, invigorating temperatures. The facility includes a bar serving healthy refreshments and more; a mezzanine level with extensive lounging areas and treatment rooms. An expansive space - great place to relax and recharge and like-minded healthy groups to bond."
