### Top Reviews
- **Ruslan I.** (2/5): "Don’t be fooled: the food in the photo wasn’t taken at KFC, but at Escape by Nikki Beach — a fancy, expensive place. Yet somehow the wings tasted worse than KFC’s. As for the squid in the foreground, it felt like it died of old age, got washed ashore by a wave, and only then ended up on the grill and on my plate. Completely inedible. The quality doesn’t come close to what the prices suggest. I was genuinely concerned about getting food poisoning and was lucky not to get sick.  The vibe and cocktails were decent, which is why this place earns two stars. Other than that, it’s absolutely terrible."
- **Thanchanok Ch** (5/5): "This restaurant is truly excellent — both in terms of food quality and service. The staff are polite, friendly, and very attentive to detail. I’ve been here many times and have been impressed every time. They consistently maintain a high standard. Highly commendable!"
- **Cuisine Quest** (5/5): "Very good food, prices are on the higher end, but very much not bad. This particular visit we had to pay cash because the credit machine was down, no big deal. Nikki Beach is very hip had great music, an actual DJ on duty, beautiful sunset view, worth a visit."
