### Top Reviews
- **Pernilla** (5/5): "Food is so so good! We came here both for lunch and dinner several times. All the dishes were amazing. We especially liked the noodle soup and the papaya salad. We also ordered quite a few coconut milkshakes and banana lassis. Great prices and very friendly staff. Views are nice too!"
- **Georgia Eaton-Rennie** (5/5): "We came for a drink after a day at the beach and had a really lovely experience! It was 4 minutes before the end of happy hour and the staff quickly whipped up our cocktails and did them even cheaper than advertised!! We had piña coladas and a strawberry daiquiri and they were all delicious!"
- **Nick Edwards** (3/5): "Stopped by for some food and drinks on a quiet Monday evening. Quick and attentive to take our order, provide mosquito repellent, and serve our drinks. 30 mins later, we asked where our food was - it turned out they’d forgotten to do it. They told us while laughing. Food came soon after, and was decent, but nothing special. When we paid with cash, they didn’t give us any change. Given that they forgot our order, I’d have expected them to give us free drinks, not just take the change we expected as a tip. Will not be visiting again."
