### Top Reviews
- **Leena Chanvirach** (5/5): "The most authentic french menu I can find in Samui. Set in a very very "very" quiet and peaceful cove on the beach with beautiful evening vibe before the sun complete set. We stop here every time we come around to Samui. Philip and Nit are great hosts in additional to their excellent dishes!"
- **Mike Schoers** (5/5): "A beautiful place, a hidden gem with a selection of European and Thai dishes. I found this place by looking for restaurants near me.. seen the photo of the fish dish and thought I have to try this, let’s go! Phillip let me know that the fish may take a little while as it requires preparation and I was happy with that as we were there for sunset and not in a hurry - we are on holiday right.. it was worth the wait indeed! My husband and I shared the dish and the crumbed pork also. Everything was fresh and cooked aldente. I tried Phillips selection of French wine and bought a bottle of Chandon which was also a reasonable price and his chocolate desert - a perfect balance of ingredients. This place really felt homely.. was lovely to spend the time here, we walked out on the waterfront and chatted with Phillip. Food is really 5 stars, we’re looking forward to having another meal here! I’d come back to Samui to spend time here again. Thank you Phillip, Nit and Daisy for your beautiful food and hospitality xo"
- **Lee Soon Guan** (5/5): "Discovered by chance. Such a charming restaurant in a very simple idyllic setting by the sea and rustic road to get to. The owner is French together with his Thai wife. She does the cooking and the food is superb and authentic."
