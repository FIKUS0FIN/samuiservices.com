### Top Reviews
- **Johanna Leitner** (5/5): "I love this place sooo much! I came here almost every day for two months The drinks are really good, same as the food. The view is amazing and they have lots of different options to sit, also options with shade. But the most amazing part is the staff. Everyone is soo friendly, they make such a good job every day. Especially Toom welcomed me within the first 3 seconds and made every day a little bit better with her smile🥰"
- **Aleksandra Grajczyk** (5/5): "An absolute gem in Koh Samui!" This is hands-down one of our absolute favorite spots on the island! We kept coming back during our trip because everything about it is exceptional. The staff and the ladies here are incredibly kind, helpful, and unbelievably good with kids—they made us feel like family every single time we walked through the door. What blew us away was their hospitality; they genuinely listen to what you like and are happy to slightly customize menu items to perfectly match your taste and expectations. On top of the amazing service, the coffee is exceptionally good, and they have a huge choice of drinks to choose from. Literally everything we ordered from the menu was delicious. If you are in Koh Samui, do yourself a favor and visit this great place!"
- **Julie Sarafov** (5/5): "Great modern and clean little cafe alongside the beach. We had a coffee and smoothie and both were 👍 great. We placed ourselves outside overlooking the beach with the boats in view and a bent coconut tree. The staff were very friendly and efficient. The food coming out of the kitchen looked amazing. We will be back again to try a meal."
