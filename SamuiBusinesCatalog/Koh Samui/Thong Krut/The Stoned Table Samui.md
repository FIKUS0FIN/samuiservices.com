### Top Reviews
- **gal tza** (5/5): "Authentic Thai Vibe, Amazing Food & Chill Atmosphere ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ If you're looking for a genuine, laid-back local experience rather than a fancy restaurant, this is the place! The atmosphere is unbeatable – sitting on bean bags right by the water, just soaking in the island vibe. The food is high quality and delicious. While they offer great Western options (like pizza and burgers) for those who prefer them, I highly recommend trying their Thai dishes to get the real authentic taste – the flavors are just on point! Another huge plus is their flexibility: the menu states that any order can have vegan substitutions, which was great for us. The staff is super friendly and the portions are generous. Highly recommended for a perfect, chill meal!"
- **duangkamon thongmark** (4/5): "It's very nice atmosphere. The staff was great. The food was ok. My crispy chicken burger was good but tuna salad was way too sweet for me. There will be a few dog walking around near the table with the bean bag seating which could be irritating for me. By the way, I had the table with chairs so no problem for me 😆"
- **David En plus** (5/5): "A nest in Koh Samui. A secret place. Between Nathon and Lamai, this little restaurant is well worth leaving the hustle and bustle of Lamai behind. Located right on the water, it offers a spectacular sunset. The simple yet elegant and thoughtfully designed decor invites you to relax and escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life. The food is delicious, prepared using family recipes. And everything is fresh. I highly recommend it."
