### Top Reviews
- **Annette B** (5/5): "The breakfast buffet line pretty decent. The food really tasty. I especially love their noodle soup section and their local Thai herb rice salad.  The room is really big, comfy and beautiful with Thai style decoration. Very unique and fascinating.  The staffs are supper friendly and helpful. Literally friendly and nice. The people here makes the stay more pleasant."
- **Bea Haysman** (5/5): "Amazing food and great choice Barbecue was incredible with so much variety and great customer service the whole time. The fire show was impressive too A real relaxed atmosphere dining on the beach with amazing views of the sea ."
- **Steffen Rademacher** (5/5): "We were guests at the Royal Muang and dined twice @ their Beach Restaurant "Spice Zone". The evening setup is very nice, directly on the beach in front of the hotels premises.  We had at the fresh sea food display and ordered crab, tiger prawns and squid, all of them plainly grilled. As sides the classic thai rice dishes with fried egg on top of the rice and vegetables plus more seafood or chicken. Also the Wagyu burger, which was very good as well, albeit being a bite too small but medium grilled as ordered.  The food is pricy but very good. The service was also very good, but as guests we were known to the service staff from our beach days and those smaller beverage and soft drink orders. So maybe either of us were biased ;-)  Anyways, clear recommendation.  Additional note: the 10% service fee and 7% tax are clearly pointed out in the menu. People should read the entire thing, tbh."
