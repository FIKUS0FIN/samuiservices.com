- **Alexandra Becková** (3/5): "I have mixed feelings about this salon because some services were excellent, while others were disappointing.  First, the pedicure was outstanding. The technician was very thorough, took great care of my skin and cuticles, and applied the gel polish beautifully. It has been almost two weeks since my pedicure and it still looks great.  The salon itself is beautiful, clean, and very professional. The hospitality is exceptional – guests are offered drinks and small snacks before and after the treatment. The overall atmosphere feels comparable to a high-quality European salon.  My husband also had a Thai massage and was very satisfied. According to him, it was above average compared to many other Thai massages he has experienced.  Unfortunately, my manicure was a major disappointment.  My cuticles were not properly treated, and the manicure already looked grown out on the same day because the product was applied too far from the cuticle area. The nail shapes and lengths were inconsistent, and even after I asked for corrections, the final result was still uneven.  The biggest issue was the nail preparation. I only had gel polish previously, but the technician treated my nails as if they had hard gel and over-filed them significantly. During the procedure I repeatedly indicated that it was painful, but the filing continued. My natural nails are now extremely thin, sensitive, and feel almost like paper. Even simple daily activities are uncomfortable.  The gel polish application itself was also below expectations. The product is uneven in several places, with visible bumps and thickness inconsistencies. Some edges were not properly finished, and the polish was applied untidily around the sides of the nails. Overall, the manicure does not look like a freshly completed professional service.  It is a shame because the salon, pedicure, hospitality, and massage were all excellent. However, due to the poor manicure quality and the damage caused to my natural nails, I cannot recommend the manicure service and would not choose it again."
