### Top Reviews
- **Cinderella Indie** (5/5): "An Unforgettable Dining Experience by the Sea  One of the most memorable parts of dining at Fish House Restaurant—besides the beautiful seaside location and stunning atmosphere—was the exceptional service.  Special thanks to Khun Raven and Khun Apple, whose care and attention truly stood out. Their service was detail-oriented, attentive, professional, warm, and genuinely friendly. They explained the menu clearly, answered every question thoughtfully, and made us feel truly welcomed. Their dedication turned our meal into an unforgettable memory and a wonderful dining experience. The team here truly deserves recognition.  As for the food, we ordered a whole seabass (500 grams), which is perfect for 1–2 people. Guests can choose whether the fish is grilled dry or juicy, and the chef prepared it using a butterfly fillet technique, making it very easy and enjoyable to eat. The fish was fresh and delicious, served with a side of mixed vegetables that complemented it perfectly.  The homemade ice cream was another highlight — rich, well-balanced, and available in several interesting flavors. A lovely touch was the complimentary basket of warm baked bread, which immediately won our hearts.  The restaurant’s atmosphere is beautiful both during the day and in the evening, making it a perfect spot for a relaxed yet refined seaside meal.  Overall, Fish House is a place where great food meets outstanding service. The team truly makes the experience special, and they absolutely deserve all the appreciation for their hard work and passion."
- **Kimberly Smith** (5/5): "We booked a table here as we were staying at the Melia hotel just down the beach. I was celebrating my birthday and the staff were so attentive and friendly. The beach setting was beautiful especially around Sunset. The menu had lots of options but we were recommended the steak. We also enjoyed some starters of oysters and seafood. It is very pricy but the quality and taste of the food definitely made up for this. We also got a tour of the hotel afterwards, would recommend."
- **Yaochang Kuo** (5/5): "I'm from Taiwan. This restaurant is fantastic, with a great atmosphere, beautiful decor, and very comfortable—it has a real resort feel, and the food is delicious. Ms. Pan's hospitality, in particular, made me feel very comfortable."
