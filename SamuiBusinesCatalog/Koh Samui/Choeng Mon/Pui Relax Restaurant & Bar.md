### Top Reviews
- **Riddhi L** (5/5): "Flavourful, freshly cooked Thai cuisine. We were in Samui for 5 days and we probably ate here 3 to 4 times. Incredible food each time, lovely service, and plenty of vegetarian options. I especially loved the crab salad and the stir fried morning glory. 10/10 recommend."
- **Jane Lee** (5/5): "My friend and I ate here twice while staying nearby in Koh Samui. Highly recommend everything we had: papaya salad, broad noodles (drunken noodle), pineapple fried rice, steamed fish with soy/ginger. They give you a little fruit dessert plate at the end of the meal. CASH ONLY"
- **Zimi Brown** (5/5): "Visited here for the first time and need to tell you that the customer service was brilliant, not only friendly staff but quick and professional service and super tasty food.
