### Top Reviews
- **William** (5/5): "Such a great stay. Spent two nights here and had a chill time. The hostel is calm and right by the beach, and its a great place to start conversations with other travelers. Its not super fancy, but thats part of the charm. GG (the owner) is super kind and helpful, and she even offers cooking classes. The food was great too. There are also cats here, which are super friendly. Locationwise, its a walking distance to Nathon, however, I felt like there was not much to see in Nathon. My only negative about this hostel is that the matresses were a little bit too hard in my opinion. All in all, if you’re in the area, consider staying here."
