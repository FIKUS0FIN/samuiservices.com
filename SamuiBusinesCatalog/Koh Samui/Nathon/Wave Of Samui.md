### Top Reviews
- **Philip Smith** (4/5): "Very spacious cafe. Lovely staff. Coffee is decent but there are better options around for the same price. My girlfriend really liked her matcha, which I think might be their specialty."
- **Björn Wildung** (4/5): "We came all the way from Chaweng specifically for the matcha, but unfortunately it was sold out. So we ordered something else instead, which turned out to be really delicious.  We came back the next day to finally try the matcha — but sadly, still no luck. On our third visit, we finally managed to get one — a strawberry matcha — which we enjoyed יחד with a croissant and some cake.  The cakes were absolutely amazing! Unfortunately, the matcha itself wasn’t really our taste. But just because we didn’t like it doesn’t mean it’s bad — it’s just not our thing. We’ve simply had better ones before.  That being said, the atmosphere is great and the service is excellent. Definitely still worth a visit!"
- **Dmitrii D** (5/5): "We accidentally found this café while walking during sunset in Nathon. We were pleasantly surprised by this place — it has a very nice atmosphere. There’s air conditioning, everything is new, and the interior is pleasant.  The staff and the owner warmly welcome you. You can order any drink you like — there’s a wide selection of delicious coffee, including premium matcha, tea, various croissants, sweets, and cold beverages. They also offer crocodile jerky and beer.  Later, I came here several times to work on my laptop — it’s a comfortable place to get some work done."
