### Top Reviews
- **Marek Horyl** (5/5): "Great indoor playpark and restaurant close to big C. Park is nicely designed with slides and trampolines. Staff is supportive and taking care about safety of your kids. Restaurant has wide menu with drinks and snacks. Possibility to organize bday parties in the facility. All is good."
- **aurelien mery** (5/5): "very good playground for various ages. mine was 16 months old and had a lot of fun. prices are very reasonable and the staff is very friendly"
- **Stephen B** (5/5): "Great place, took my young girls for a couple of hours whilst I had an iced coffee and some snacks. Very reasonably priced at 100baht an hour per child. Staff are very friendly and seem engaged with the kids - playing with them and helping them with the slide, pushing them on the little trikes, etc. The iced latte was well presented and tasted good, as did the sliced green mango with Thai sauce, and the side of fries I got. Toilets didn’t have any toilet paper which could be improved and I didn’t see any water freely available for the kids but I may not have looked hard enough. Overall a nice break for kids and adults to do their own thing for a while."
