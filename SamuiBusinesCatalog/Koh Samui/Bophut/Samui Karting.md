### Top Reviews
- **Mathilde Wanderlust** (5/5): "Samui Karting opened its new location few days ago. It was our 12 yo daughter first time driving a karting and she absolutely loved it. The owner & the team were extremely friendly and made our daughter fell very comfortable. For those who hygiene matters, a disposable hair cap to wear under the helmets is provided and shoes (with new socks) are available for rent (extra cost).  Highly recommended !"
- **Frequent Traveller** (3/5): "Fun level: high! 🚦 Kart quality: pure lottery. You don’t race your friends — you race against which kart you randomly get. Some fly, others feel like they’re powered by hope and good memories. Only five karts on track at once, so expect short rounds and long waits. The place could use a little love (and maybe a wrench or two), but overall still a good laugh and bit of adrenaline on Samui."
- **Giorgio Carlo Roberto Albieri** (5/5): "Best Go-Karting Race ever seen! Great race, great karts, wonderful environment with palms everywhere, very friendly staff! Top!"
