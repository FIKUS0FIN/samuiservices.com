### Top Reviews
- **Souraya Gyves** (5/5): "Such a gem!!! They have absolutely nailed it… the food is so good, not complicated, not pretentious, just good!! We had lunch and dinner few times and each time we were amazed by the quality and flavours. The service is very very good. Super attentive, knowledgeable and friendly. We really liked it and kept on coming back. Well done team for creating something truly special."
- **Ward Jones** (5/5): "This place was a great vibe! My wife and I (mid 30s) are on our honeymoon and wanted a quick break from Thai Food. We had a great dinner (Pizza and Caprese). Service was great and so was the ambiance. It felt like we were in the Mediterranean for a bit."
- **Julius Lussenburg** (5/5): "Superb place for seafood! We spent several hours here enjoying a selection of seafood platters. Although all dishes were great, I particularly enjoyed the rainbow fish and the spicy calamari. On a more practical note, between 17:00 and 19:00 they offer a happy hour, with - among other things - promotions on some of their wines by the glass."
