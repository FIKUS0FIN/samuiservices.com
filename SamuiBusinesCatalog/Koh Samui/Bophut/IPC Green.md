### Top Reviews
- **Siamak Davoudi** (2/5): "We visited IPC Green Samui to explore options for our two young children.  The facilities look nice at first glance, but the overall experience raised several concerns.  Before even properly touring the place, we were immediately presented with a price list. The system is very rigid: full-week attendance (Monday to Friday, full day only), with no flexibility for shorter days or fewer days per week.  Enrollment requires committing to a full semester (around 4 months), even if you only need a short-term solution. This creates a significant financial risk — if you realize after a few days that it is not the right environment for your child, you are still locked in and have already paid a substantial amount.  We also asked about trial days and were told they would follow up, but we never received any response.  Overall, this might suit families looking for a strict, long-term program, but we would not recommend it for families who value flexibility or want to ease their children into a new environment first."
