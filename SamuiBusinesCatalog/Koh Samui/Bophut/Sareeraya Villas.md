### Top Reviews
- **sorawit wongtamat** (5/5): "Fabulous boutique hotel, staff very friendly and attentive. Location is perfect with a lovely part of the beach at your disposal. We were there for my 70 th birthday and they made a lovely decoration on our bed and brought a cake to my room. My only regret was bothering to pre hire a car as they provide courtesy pick up from the airport return and Chewang is a nightmare to drive and parking is nigh impossible, there are regular baht buses that pick up from outside the hotel for a cheap cost. The room was luxurious and spotlessly clean. The balcony had great views of the beach. Breakfast was very good. All in all a great holiday, we will be back."

- **Tom Tom** (3/5): "I recently stayed at Sareeraya Villas & Suites and want to give an honest review focused on one key issue: the room size and location are not as advertised. •	The so-called 70 m² suites start directly along the main road. We had no idea these rooms were roadside. If we had known, we wouldn’t have booked them — at least not at that price. •	Once inside the room, it was immediately clear that the space was nowhere near 70 m². After raising the issue, the hotel sent two engineers to measure it. Their result confirmed my own quick measurement: 49 m². •	I was told that all rooms sold as 70 m² are in fact this size. So anyone booking these suites should expect roughly 50 m², not 70. •	I asked for a price adjustment to match the real size. Instead, I received a bottle of wine. Nice gesture, but it doesn’t fix the fact that I paid for a larger room than I got. •	To be clear: the staff is amazing. The ladies at the check-in were exceptionally kind, helpful, and welcoming. My issue is only with the misleading room description and the reluctance of management to correct the pricing.  Bottom line: Great people, but the advertised room sizes are simply not accurate. If you expect a true 70 m² suite, you won’t get it — and the rooms along the road are far from the quiet experience you might expect at this price point."
