Despite clear pre-booking communication and confirmation, this experience felt like a bait-and-switch and cash-grab, not a patient-first medical consultation. I would strongly advise others to clarify in writing whether: •	The booked treatment will actually be honoured •	A doctor consultation is required to proceed •	And whether additional fees apply simply to receive an explanation"
