Prices of food are on the medium $$ end, beverages are a little expensive mostly with the alcoholic drinks and wines are too expensive.  Lovely vegetarian massaman curry, vegan Pad-Thai and smashed potatoes with tofu 🤤💛🙏🏽 oh and a few days a week they have live music and fire shows, watch out for those, they are really good!
