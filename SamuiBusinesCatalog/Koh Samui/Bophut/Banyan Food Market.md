### Top Reviews
- **Natalie Kamolwattanasoontorn** (5/5): "We have been here about five times, tonight Khun Pui was playing Jazz with his band. We ate from the Grill and Sushi place and drinks for the bar. It is such a lovely setting there, great staff, so much food choice. Amazing host Gregory. Aways a happy space day or night."
- **Sören Dittrich** (4/5): "A bit hidden but worth a visit. You get to choose from 6 to 8 different food stalls, sandwiches, burgers, Thai, Russian, veggie/ vegan  All catered to the farang customer mostly. Prices are fair but above average. Atmosphere is nice, especially after sunset.  Good place for a quick lunch / dinner or some drinks to start the night.  Recommended, will come back"
- **i Travel** (5/5): "A wonderful small food market with a few types of tastey foods and dessert options. We had the Vegan sweetheart shake and the Eggoist egg sandwich and they were both delicious! Nice atmosphere 👌"
