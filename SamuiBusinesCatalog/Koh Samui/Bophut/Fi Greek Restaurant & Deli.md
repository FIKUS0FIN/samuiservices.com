### Top Reviews
- **Simon** (5/5): "I had a really great experience here! For being in Thailand, the food tastes surprisingly authentic Greek – very fresh, flavorful, and simply delicious. The portions are quite big, so you definitely leave the restaurant full and satisfied. Prices are a little higher but considering it’s a specialty cuisine, it’s absolutely fair and worth it.  The staff is very kind and welcoming, which makes the whole experience even better. The menu is clear and easy to understand, so ordering is simple  The only small downside is the atmosphere. It’s located on the busy main road. The place inside feels a bit empty and not really decorated with much detail. It comes across cold and plain. However, for me this wasn’t a big deal because the food itself was excellent and that’s what truly matters.  Highly recommended if you are craving authentic Greek food in Thailand!"
- **Gle Gle** (5/5): "Delicious food, excellent service and friendly people. Myself and my family eating often in this restaurant, in the center of koh Samui. Lamb oven, tzatziki, feta oven, pita bread, greek salad some of our favourite. They are makers of greek yogurt as well! This is the best yogurt I ever have. Thank you Fi Greek! See you on Friday!"
- **Ekaterina Artigas** (2/5): "We went here for dinner with kids and parents. Cafe looks very simple; food is so so, portions small, very small for the price. We ordered 4 mains and 4 starters, and we were still hungry. In the end we ordered one more starter shrimps and this was the only good sized plate. Ipita bread not cooked well, when I informed waiter about it he just said, that it is a recipe. We are international family: Russian, Turkish and Greek and we never try such pita bread. There is no manager, just burmese staff working, so, don’t expect much from this place."
