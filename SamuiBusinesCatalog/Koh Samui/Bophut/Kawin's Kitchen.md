### Top Reviews
- **Reagan O’Dea** (5/5): "We are visiting on holidays from Canada and we were out for a walk looking for a cute dinner restaurant that was between our hotel and not in fisherman’s village. At first glance it reminded me of a small bistro chain restaurant but I was pleasantly surprised to find out it was small beautiful, relaxed and delicious!!  There was chicken strips and fries for our picky children, delicious drinks and my husband and I both had meals from the specials board.  The attention to detail and presentation of the plates was gorgeous and full of flavour! Don’t overlook this place it’s definitely a must visit in Koh Samui! We stayed at Zazen boutique and it was about a 7-10 min walk to get there.  5 Stars!!"
- **Danielle Ruan** (5/5): "This was the best tasting morish food I've had for my whole stay in my Thailand trip in Koh Samui Bo Phut area. Sadly only found on my last night. My mum and me shared Khao Soi (creamy gorgeous sauce with noodles and chicken) and Budda Bowl. We finished every last bit. Then we're treated to complimentry coconut icecream. The Mai Tai cocktails were great to, would definitely recommend to anyone."
- **Lizzie T** (5/5): "What a wonderful restaurant :-)  Amazing food!  Such good value! (with plenty of gluten free options for me - knowledgeable and kind staff, including Kawin the owner who was so helpful.  A genuine warm welcome and true hospitality and caring - excellent fresh food, served with such care and attention to detail.  Thank you very much - we will be back :-)"
