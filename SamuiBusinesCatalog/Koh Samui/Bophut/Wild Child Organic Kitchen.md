- **Habs No** (5/5): "I booked the Day Pass with pool access and was truly impressed from the moment I arrived. The location is stunning, and the food exceeded all expectations – and that's saying something, considering the 1,500 THB minimum spend is included in the package. You get incredible value: the menu is delicious, fresh, and more than satisfying. What makes it even more special is that at least part of the ingredients are grown organically in their own on-site garden – you can really taste the difference.  What makes this place truly unique, though, is being able to watch the elephants directly from the pool. I've never experienced anything like it. The enclosure is spacious and thoughtfully designed – plenty of room for the two elephants to roam freely and simply be themselves.  Once a day, guests get the chance to feed them melons for around 30 to 60 minutes, which is a wonderful and memorable experience. What I appreciated most is the ethical approach: the elephants are never forced to interact. They do their own thing, move at their own pace, and if they disappear for an hour, that's completely fine – and honestly, that's exactly how it should be. No riding, no bathing sessions, no touching. Just elephants living as elephants.  If you want to be close to elephants in a responsible, ethical way – without exploiting them – this is the place to be. Highly recommended!"
