### Top Reviews
- **Sebastian Wagner** (5/5): "I spent five lovely days at the Us Hostel and would highly recommend it. It's certainly not a party hostel, but it's well located and quiet. It's also quick and easy to get to lively places. I would also recommend hiring a scooter (if you feel confident enough). As a guest, you can even rent one without a deposit for 250 baht per day. The return was also very straightforward."
- **Lauren Pascolutti** (4/5): "Enjoyed my stay in Koh Samui! The common areas and pool are brilliant and the food is great. I didn’t find it the most social hostel but I may have just been unlucky, I did still make a couple of friends but people didn’t interact with each other much and vibes were very chill. The rooms are small and the beds are a little creaky with no curtains and limited storage space. During my stay the water went off one night but it was fixed by the morning. The staff were really friendly and helpful! I’d stay again."
- **Mia Ellahee** (5/5): "We originally only booked for 1 night but extended without hesitation! The hostel has such a warm and welcoming atmosphere and the staff are so friendly! There is plenty of social space but is big enough if you do just want to sit by yourself to chill too. We rented a bike from the hostel (super easy) and had breakfast here both mornings. A great menu with lots of choices of different things to eat, and so tasty! A great location too, a 10 minute walk to the food market and walking street!Would 100% recommend! ❤️🙏🏼"
