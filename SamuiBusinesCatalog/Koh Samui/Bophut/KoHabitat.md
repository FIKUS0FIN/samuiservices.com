### Top Reviews
- **Verity Wilkinson** (5/5): "i LOVED staying here. the vibe is leafy greens and dark woods and it’s just chef’s kiss. the bed is genuinely the softest i’ve had since being in Thailand and the dorm was really quiet at night. The owner is really friendly and helpful, she’ll wash, dry, fold and return your washing to you for 150thb (correct at time of writing) which is so convenient. I also had a nightmare of a journey so arrived quite late and frazzled but she was wonderful and chilled out about it which was so nice 😭. the hostel is about 20 min walk from fisherman’s village and there’s the big C super centre next door and 7-11’s also super close. Everything was clean and felt safe. Also omg the shower was brilliant, I almost cried."
- **Oskar** (4/5): "Rooms are very nice but there is not much space or things to do with the other people staying at the Hostel. The Lady owning the hostel is super friendly, speaks fantastic english and is very helpful. Location is not really good, as it is directly at the main road and the windows dont keep the sound out. Also you need the drive a while to get to the places you want to see at the Island, which results in very few visitors at the hostel during rainy season especially. Overall i enjoyed the stay."
- **Maria Frias** (5/5): "Could have stayed longer, if I had the time. Very aesthetically pleasing inside and cozy. The bed was comfortable to which very seldom in hostels in Thailand. I love that there’s a basic equipped kitchen. They are located in the main road so everything is in a walking distance. The owner made my visit very easy with their knowledge of the island. She can hook you up with almost everything you need, may it be tours, motorbikes rental, taxi services, etc., It was a wonderful experience to find this hostel gem! Will stay again when I get back!"
