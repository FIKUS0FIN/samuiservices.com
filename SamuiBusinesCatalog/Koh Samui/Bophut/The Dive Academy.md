### Top Reviews
- **Marlene Hansen** (5/5): "My son and I had our first ever dive today with The Dive Academy. From the very beginning we felt safe and in good hands. Our instructor Bjørn made sure that we understood everything thoroughly before we got in the water and took his time to make us comfortable — both before we went under the surface and during the actual diving part. It was such an amazing experience for both my son and me. I would definitely recommend The Dive Academy to anyone who wants to experience the joy of diving while on Koh Samui. They are great for both adults and kids — I mean, my son would have us come back every year if it were up to him, because he had such a wonderful time thanks to the incredible team! 😊  Edited on October 14: The attached photos were taken by Leia while we were underwater. These are just a small selection of the many amazing pictures and videos she captured. She did an incredible job, and it’s so cool to have our trip immortalized this way!"
- **florian Stöckli** (5/5): "I went diving with the Dive Academy in Koh Samui at Sail Rock, and it was an absolutely fantastic experience.  Everything was perfectly organized and very well prepared. The briefing and debriefing were clear, structured, and helpful. The equipment was in excellent condition.  A special thanks to Dive Master José – with his calm and relaxed attitude, he made the dive truly enjoyable and ensured we felt safe at all times.  The dive itself was incredible – huge schools of fish like I had never seen before. Truly impressive!  Highly recommended – I would definitely dive with you again!"
- **Ramita Mint** (1/5): "Very disappointing experience and highly unprofessional service.  Three days before my trip, I specifically asked whether Nitrox would be available and was assured that it would. On the actual dive day, however, I was told by the manager that the Nitrox machine was broken and that nobody was using Nitrox, except for me on one dive.  Once I boarded the boat, I discovered that this was not true. Almost everyone else was using Nitrox tanks for two of their dives, while I was the only diver who did not receive the Nitrox that had been promised during booking. Being given misleading information was extremely frustrating.  As a result, I had to end one of my dives after only 28 minutes due to air consumption limitations, which significantly impacted my diving experience.  The boat itself was dirty and poorly maintained. In addition, some of the guides appeared inexperienced. I was also picked up by a Myanmar driver who told me he was not allowed to drive me, which raised concerns about whether proper licensing and operational procedures were being followed.  Overall, the service was disorganized, misleading, and far below the standard I would expect from a professional dive operator. I would not recommend this company based on my experience."
