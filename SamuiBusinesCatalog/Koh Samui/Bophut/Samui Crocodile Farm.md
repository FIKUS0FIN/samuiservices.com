### Top Reviews
- **Stefan Hosemann** (5/5): "Pretty interesting, the show is actually pretty scary and intense. Of course: do not compare it to a western standard zoo - this is Thailand. Nevertheless it's worth visiting! There are no monkeys and cobras (at least not when we went there in July 2025)  Shows are at specific times... I remember 14:00h and 16:00h"
- **Hertta Siekkeli** (1/5): "Stumbled by accident. The enclosures were in very bad shape and the crocodiles looked miserable. Also snakes in their concrete enclosures with no water or greenery whatsoever. The crododile show was a hard watch and completely unneccesary."
- **BA M** (3/5): "Despite reading the reviews we went. Many crocodiles to observe. Pretty run down place.Total stay around 25 minutes. Paid 1200 baht (2 adults and 2 kids). Not worth the money I think but they do have a lot to maintain. Did not see a show. Could come back another day to see one. We were the only visitors. Bring repellent. Would not recommend going again. But the kids liked the crocodiles. Do think the owners are good people and do love the crocodiles."
