### Top Reviews
- **anup thakur** (5/5): "Located in Bo Phut. 5mins walk to the beach and 10mins walk to fisherman’s village night market and the wharf. You can find coco tams there too. Maria was extremely friendly. Helped me out by booking the cab for us from the pier. Response was prompt. Helped us in booking a day tour to Koh tao and assisted in renting us a bike.Property is located inside a lane and was cosy. Although there’s no breakfast available, there are various places where you can eat here. Will definetly be coming back to stay at this place again. 🙂"
- **Paul Campbell** (5/5): "A nice small hotel located just off the main road in a quiet area, walkable to the Fisherman’s village night market. The rooms were spacious and clean, small hob and microwave were provided if you want to cook in your room. The manager Maria and her 2 staff were on site most of the day and were frequently asking if every thing was OK. There is a small clean swimming pool and sunbeds with shade in a well kept garden area. All rooms have a decent size balcony. An added bonus was the on site washing machines with detergent supplied, this was free of charge.  I feel the  need to comment on a recent post that stated;  "What really brings down the rating for us is that it is only cleaned every four days. It got dirty very quickly as we are 2 adults and 2 small children in a small room. It said "daily cleaning" when booking so to be told on site that it is only cleaned every four days shocked us. Sand, dust, hair and crumbs collected everywhere and very quickly. It felt disgusting to sleep when you could feel all the sand in the bed."  This comment is simply not true, the information book in each room clearly states that rooms are cleaned every 4 days and if you require daily cleaning just ask the staff. My advice to this reviewer is if you feel it was disgusting to sleep with sand on your bed don't put it there, you could use the foot wash hose at the entrance or use the shower by the pool, or indeed take advantage of the offer to have daily room cleans. Worst case scenario remove the sheets and shake the sand off outside. We booked 6 nights and leave tomorrow, I would happily stay longer."
- **teresa lukianiec** (5/5): "Lovely spot. Rooms are all looking at the pool the pool is lovely treat partly shady areas to sit. My room had a kitchen not sure about the rest of the units but it had everything I needed and I was happy here. 10 minute walk to the beach several restaurants close by.
