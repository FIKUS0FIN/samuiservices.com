### Top Reviews
- **Radu Mustata** (5/5): "Awesome for western-type breakfasts, bread eggs etc. The variety is fantastic and the food is really good, especially for the price. Ive shopped around for breakfast / brunch places in Bophut and I find KOB to draw the best balance between menu variety, price, and quality. My go-to is the breakfast hash with cheese, but I also really enjoy all of their sandwiches and burgers. Staff are really friendly, and another extra bonus is that you can pay with card, so you can save your cash for when you really need it."
- **Serene Chua** (5/5): "Nice and cozy breakfast spot! The food was very delicious and well prepared, and the atmosphere made it a great place. Friendly and welcoming crew too, which made the experience even better. Would definitely come back again!"
- **Monica’s Taste** (4/5): "It may be a bit overhyped, but I still thought it was good. It’s a breakfast and brunch spot with a wide variety of food and drinks to choose from, offering both Thai and Western options a nice fusion overall. It’s also a great place to work or spend time casually. Overall, it was a good experience."
