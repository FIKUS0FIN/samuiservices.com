### Top Reviews
- **SICILIAN-AMERICAN** (4/5): "Came here to chill on the beach and lay out on their sun lounger which are free to use with drink/food purchase. My friend and I chilled there for several hours drinking. Prices are a bit high but the place is nice and right on the beach. Happy hour we got 3 ice cold beers in an ice bucket for 250b. Staff were friendly and attentive.didnt try their food menu but maybe next visit. Recommended"
- **Paul Exeter** (4/5): "Dined here most days while staying at the weekender. The service was exceptional. They make a mean cocktail, the food presentation is amazing and tastes good. Thank you Lak for your wonderful care and service!"
- **Daniel DeRosha** (5/5): "We stumbled upon this beachside restaurant in Lamai Beach - best dining choice of our trip. Good variety of tradition Koh Samui Thai and a few western comforts too. I had the yellow snapper and pineapple curry and it was delicious. Highly recommend!"
