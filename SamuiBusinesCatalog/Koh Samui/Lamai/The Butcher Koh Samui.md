### Top Reviews
- **Kyrio Azure** (5/5): "The Butcher is easily one of the best Western restaurants in Lamai. The steaks were cooked properly, the meat quality was noticeably better than most places on the island, and the portions were generous. The atmosphere feels busy and lively without being too loud or pretentious, and the staff were friendly and efficient even when the restaurant was packed. Prices are higher than average for Lamai, but for imported beef and the overall quality, it feels justified. Definitely a great spot if you want a proper steak, burger, or comfort food meal in Koh Samui."
- **Kylian PONTE** (5/5): "Amazing restaurant in Koh Samui with a great atmosphere, delicious food, and outstanding service. Our steak dinner was cooked perfectly. tender, juicy, and full of flavor. The staff were very friendly and attentive, making the whole experience even better. Definitely one of the best dining experiences we’ve had in Koh Samui. Highly recommended!"
- **Salvador Calderón A.** (4/5): "A place with a very good appearance. The service is excellent. The food has a very good presentation, and the size is perfect. The taste is normal, we tried the two burgers and the meat tasted good but their cooking was a bit overdone. The prices are slightly high, but fair for the attention and the dishes you are receiving.  Excellent option to eat food different from Thai."
