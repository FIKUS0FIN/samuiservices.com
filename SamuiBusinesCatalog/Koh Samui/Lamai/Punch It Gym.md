### Top Reviews

- **נטלי קדיש** (5/5): "I started Muay Thai training with Coach Four, and I had no previous experience or knowledge at all. From day one, he has been incredibly patient, professional, and supportive. What I appreciate most is how attentive he is—he quickly identifies weaknesses and knows exactly how to help strengthen them. I’m very happy with my training and the progress I’ve made with him. It’s nothing against the gym/coaches itself, because everything is fine but I prefer a smaller, less crowded, more family vibes kind of Muay Thai training. I was at a group class with around 20 people. And usually I had max 6 other people joining a class. But the gym is clean, price is also super okay 400 baht for a group session of 1,5 h. They even have a little shop and a cafe there. It’s nice just not for me."
