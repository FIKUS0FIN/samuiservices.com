### Top Reviews
- **Elvira** (5/5): "🩷🩷🩷 It felt like one of those places people truly fall in love with 🥹🌿 so green, so calm… like a little escape  You can really feel why everyone loves it such a peaceful, gentle atmosphere  We had falafel, Japanese soba with vegetables, Indian samosa, and a beans roll and Cadadilia 😋 simple, fresh, and made with care  And the kombucha with lemongrass… so refreshing ✨  The girls there are so kind and sweet you feel it right away 🤍"
- **Magdalena Ros** (5/5): "The best food!!!! SO fresh. SO delicious. YUMM. I think the best we had in Thailand. Had the wrap it up with guacamole, the quesadillas in vegan, the pink passion roles with tofu and the spicy mushroom tofu salad. think the favourite were the quesadillas but the wrap it up were SO fresh 😍 100% recommend. Also free drinks a plus as it’s quite pricey but worth it."
- **Charlotte Owen** (5/5): "10/10 for the food and drinks here. It was all really tasty. I'd recommend it all - I had the Viva Burrito which was a really excellent vegan burrito even compared to London standards. I loved that it had beans, tofu, peppers, lettuce, rice AND guac!  I also had the herby salad - I didn't care for the dressing so I asked for lime and olive oil which went brilliantly.  And then finally I had the tofu satay skewers which came with a satay sauce and pickled veg.  We also a decaf iced latte which was excellent coffee.  The staff are nice and speak good English. Portions and prices are good."
