### Top Reviews
- **Anna Kálmán** (5/5): "I honestly can’t recommend NO STRESS enough — this place completely lives up to its name 🌴✨  From the moment we arrived, the owner (such a genuinely kind and welcoming lady!) made us feel right at home. The whole team is incredibly friendly and attentive, which just adds to the relaxed, happy vibe of the place.  The food is delicious — fresh, flavorful, and exactly what you want when you’re by the beach. And don’t even get me started on the coconut… SO good 🥥💛  What really makes this place special are all the little extras: free WiFi, free sunbeds, and even free stand-up paddle boards, which made the whole experience feel extra fun and carefree.  The beach itself is stunning — soft white sand, crystal-clear water, and a beautiful view that makes you want to stay all day (which we basically did 😌).  If you’re looking for a place to relax, eat well, and just enjoy life by the sea… No Stress is absolutely it. Can’t wait to come back!"
- **Anislav Atanasov** (3/5): "I wouldn't recommend the Tom Yum. It was too clear and sour, and I didn't enjoy the flavor at all. It’s the worst version of this soup I've tried. We also tried the Pad Thai, which was nothing special, and the coconut drink was disappointing as well. Interestingly, all dishes were a flat price of 180 baht, which is quite unusual for this type of food."
- **K** (5/5): "The place lives up to its name. The staff is very friendly and attentive, making you feel welcome right away. The food is delicious, and there’s a great selection of drinks. One of the best perks is that you can use the sunbeds for free as long as you order something. All of this is set on a beautiful beach, which makes the experience even better. We would definitely come back!"
