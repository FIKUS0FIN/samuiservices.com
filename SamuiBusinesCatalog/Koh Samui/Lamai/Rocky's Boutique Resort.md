### Top Reviews
- **Ioana Dit** (5/5): "We had a perfect stay at Rocky’s. The staff was very helpful and willing to help us with everything. The room left us speechless, as they gave us an upgrade. We stayed in a sea view room, with shared private pool, with all the necessities. We were lucky to be there in a Saturday and we booked the dinner by the sea activity. The food was very good, varied, and the evening included a fire show that amazed us. The massage was very relaxing and the ladies took care of us, to be an unforgettable experience. Overall, choosing Rocky’s boutique resort was the best ideea. Definitely recommend to book some nights there when in Samui."
- **Claire H** (5/5): "This is a fantastic resort, do yourself a favour and book your holiday here! We brought our 19 month old daughter and had an amazing time. The pools are great, the staff are friendly, our room was cool and spacious, the resort’s beach is great for adults and children. We liked that the beach and pools had shade to sit in. The staff are happy to organise anything you need such as a hire car, transfer, tour, activity. Our food was included with our package and was delicious. We also enjoyed the cocktail night and beach fire show. We loved that the resort was big enough that it had everything we needed, but small enough that it really was boutique and not overcrowded. Thank you Rocky’s for a truly memorable and enjoyable trip. We wish we had stayed longer than a week!!!"
- **Bec Kilpatrick** (5/5): "We were lucky enough to book one of the beach view rooms with plunge pool and it was incredible! We loved the small private beach for relaxing and enjoying the beautiful sunrise and sunsets. The rocky scenery is so unique and beautiful. We read some reviews saying you couldn't swim but we swam every day and it was beautiful. We also took out the kayaks they have for free and really enjoyed exploring the coastline around the area. The food at the restaurant is great and we also liked exploring outside the hotel around the grandfather rocks area which is walkable. Would absolutely stay again!"
