### Top Reviews
- **Joe DeHart** (5/5): "Absolutely great dining experience. We ate here twice and did a bit of the fixed menu the first night and changed it up for our final night. Chicken satay starter is extremely flavorful and perfectly prepared, we spooned the rest of the peanut and chili sauce when we finished. Beef massaman was fantastic, fried local greens and cashew chicken were a big hit also. Really fits the venue well and the executive chef went above and beyond both times we dined in. Going to miss this spot"
- **Serhat Sozen** (5/5): "We had an incredible experience at The Height. The traditional Thai cuisine was a delight, and Chef Manee took the time to provide insightful explanations about the dishes. The staff was friendly, and the service was of high quality. We found the best Thai food here, and the prices are reasonable compared to other places on the island. I highly recommend this restaurant for those seeking to savor traditional Thai cuisine with a culinary touch."
- **J T** (5/5): "Perfect Thai food!  Top: fried rice with crab, Gang Poo Bai Cha Ploo, the soups and Massaman Gai / Nuea (it comes with roti)."
