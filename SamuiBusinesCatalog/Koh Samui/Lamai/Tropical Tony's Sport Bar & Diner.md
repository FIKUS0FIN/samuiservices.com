### Top Reviews
- **Alrike Coetzee** (5/5): "Officially one of my favourite places. We came for dinner and had the fish and chips, and the beef and mash. It was delicious. The staff was friendly and lively and the setting was nice and relaxed. Massive portions with great prices. 10/10"
- **Becca James** (5/5): "The best home comfort British food I’ve had since travelling. I had steak and onion pie and it was amazing. The mash potato was some of the best mash I’ve ever had. Was even better than most pie and mash I’ve had in England. The service was great and the staff are so friendly. We will definitely be coming back before we leave. Thank you so much"
- **Steve Foote** (5/5): "I was recommended this by a friend who lives in Koh Samui. I have to say it was the best value meal I have ever had. Such good food at such a good price. The ribs are slow cooked for 12 hours, the pies are homemade and that was just what we had. I have been told by other locals in the bar that all the food is just as good. My only disappointment is that I live in Australia so can’t come here every week. If you are in Koh Samui, you HAVE to visit Tropical Tony’s !!"
