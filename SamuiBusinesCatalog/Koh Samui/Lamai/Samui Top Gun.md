### Top Reviews
- **Rachad Koaik** (4/5): "Everybody agrees the prices were expensive, however we had a pleasant experience though very short (5 mins for me and my wife). We shot the glock and a smith and wesson gun 10 rounds each. The service was basic with smiling faces and helpful staff. They even allowed me to setup my gopro and film with ease! 5% plus if you want to pay by card."
- **Adam D** (4/5): "We had a really fun day at Ko Samui Top Range Gun. The experience of shooting at the range was exciting and well-organised, and we never felt rushed at any point. The staff were friendly, professional, and even kindly took photos of us posing with the guns, which made the day even more memorable.  One thing that did surprise us was the pricing. We later found out that the actual cost of the bullets is around £3 which is quite expensive. That felt quite expensive so it’s something to be aware of before booking.  Overall though, it’s a good spot for shooting, especially if you’re looking for a fun and different experience while in Ko Samui"
- **Gabriel** (5/5): "Listen, this place is not cheap, but for shotgun and medium calibre rounds it’s probably as cheap as you’ll get here.  Good little range. Decent friendly staff.  Really good for beginners  But if you’re a shooter like myself peeper to rack up the $$$  Other than that, good selection of weapons. They let you handle most of them and have a look at the them."
