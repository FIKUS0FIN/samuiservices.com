### Top Reviews
- **Philipp Gärtner-Uszkai** (5/5): "An absolute highlight on Koh Samui. The Oyster Bar is a true paradise for oyster lovers. The oysters are incredibly fresh, perfectly chilled, and full of flavor. You can really taste the quality with every bite. What makes the experience even better is the large selection of different toppings, from classic options to more creative combinations, which makes tasting the oysters especially fun.  The atmosphere is relaxed and welcoming, and the quality of the food speaks for itself. Without a doubt, the Oyster Bar is a must place to visit when you are on Koh Samui, especially if you love fresh seafood."
- **Massimo Gobbi** (5/5): "I am used to go Samui for more than 20 years but this Oyster Bar is really amazing. Fresh Oyster, delicious escalope, a very good selection of wines and the staff is always kind and ready to assist you with any info you need regarding their excellent food. Even the Octopus was superlative 😍😍😍"
- **Jerry Fung** (5/5): "Had a really enjoyable dinner here during our Samui trip 😊  The atmosphere was relaxing, staff were friendly and attentive, and the overall dining experience was very comfortable. Food quality was solid and nicely presented, especially the seafood dishes which were fresh and tasty.  A great place to chill with good food and nice vibes in Koh Samui. Would definitely consider coming back again next time!  Highly recommended 👍"
