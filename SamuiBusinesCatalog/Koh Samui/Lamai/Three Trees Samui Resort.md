### Top Reviews
- **Kate Matthews** (5/5): "Amazing place to stay! Beautiful pools and ocean views. Great location. Breakfast buffet on site was amazing! Good variety of western and Thai foods. The fire show on Friday is amazing! Wished we could have stayed here longer. The most luxurious stay we had in Thailand and for a really great price. The service and staff were also very attentive and kind.  The only problem we had was with hot water going to our room. Staff was very quick to fix the issue though. One small recommendation would be the shower head. The placement made it a bit difficult to stand under as it shot straight out into the far wall, so it was a bit annoying to have to hold the handle in your hand to get proper placement. Minor annoyance."
- **Prakasam Mathaiyan** (4/5): "My partner and I had a wonderful stay at Three Trees Samui Resort – Sarocha Villa. The villa was cozy, clean, and well-maintained, with a beautiful tropical atmosphere that made it perfect for a romantic escape. The staff were incredibly warm and welcoming, making us feel right at home throughout our stay. Lamai Beach is just steps away, and the peaceful surroundings made it ideal for unwinding together. A minor scope for improvement in a few amenities keeps it at four stars, but overall it was a delightful experience and we would love to come back!"
- **Guy Basile** (5/5): "Just spent 4 days here in a garden pool Villa Absolutely loved it and was sad to leave. Staff were fantastic facilities awesome. Great food in the restaurant.  The room was spotless and the lounge pool was awesome after a hard  day of exploring.  If I had any complaint it would be that access to the pool is a little awkward for the elderly. Perhaps a step would be helpful  Minor complaint. This  place was awesome."
