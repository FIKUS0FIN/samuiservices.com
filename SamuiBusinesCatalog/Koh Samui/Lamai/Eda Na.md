### Top Reviews
- **Daria Kirbai** (5/5): "The best place with Russian food in Thailand! The restaurant is owned by a friendly man who enjoys his job and gives a warm welcome. All the dishes we tried tasted great and reminded of homemade food from my gran in Russia. Divine herring salad!Also, the ambience is lovely and the space was decorated for NY’s with good taste. Don’t miss this cafe if you are in Samui."
- **Sara Fischer** (5/5): "So good! They have vegetarian borscht on request and a lot of veggie options. Free bread is served to the table. Tea is delicious. Potatoes are great along with blinchiki with mushrooms and cheburek with cheese."
- **Z** (5/5): "Wow, never had such good Russian food. I went here with a Russian friend and he agrees that the food is delicious. Some real Russian favourites like borscht, this noodle dish with minced meat, and the dumplings are amazing."
