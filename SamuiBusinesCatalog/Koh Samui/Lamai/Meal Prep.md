### Top Reviews
- **Mathieu Chaumont** (5/5): "One of the best healthy spots I’ve tried in Bo put. The concept is really well done: every meal includes the calories and macros (protein, carbs, fats), which is super convenient if you train or simply want to eat clean without guessing everything.  What I liked most is that the food is actually very healthy BUT still tastes really good, often “healthy food” is bland, but not here. Portions are also generous and filling, while still feeling light and balanced afterwards.  The wraps and bowls were fresh, high in protein, and very satisfying. Service was fast.  Another big plus: they’re open almost all day, including mornings from 9am, which is perfect for breakfast, post-workout meals, or just a healthy lunch during the day.  Prices are fair and not overpriced considering the quality, ingredients, and portion sizes.  You can tell this place is made for people who care about fitness, health, or simply eating better without sacrificing taste. I’ll definitely come back 👍"
