There were many small details which really made a difference, for example complimentary breadsticks, pretty lamp, ashtray and toothpick holder on the table. A special mention to our waiter Niko who was very helpful and friendly. We came on our last night in Koh Samui which is a shame as we would have definitely come back if we had found it sooner!"
