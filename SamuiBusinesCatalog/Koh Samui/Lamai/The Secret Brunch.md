### Top Reviews
- **Jonny Harding** (5/5): "Wow!  With only 2 days left of our trip we found this place and are already wanting to come back!  I had the egg and cheese jalapeño bagel and it was incredible perfectly toasted with melty cheese and just the right spicy kick. The mango smoothie was fresh, thick, and bursting with tropical flavor, and the coffee was honestly fantastic!! rich, smooth, and expertly brewed.  Great vibes, amazing food, and some of the best coffee on the island. A must-visit brunch spot!"
- **Simon** (5/5): "Managed to find a lovely coffee shop in Lamai, and I'm gradually working my way through their pastries and baked goods ... It's a hard life, but someone has to do it! Not only is the coffee great - top marks for the latte art - but the entire Secret Brunch team here are friendly and super-efficient. There's  always a lovely energy about this place every time I visit - pretty much every day for the past couple of weeks!  Worth mentioning too, that the facilities are spotless and the wi-fi is fast and reliable.  More importantly though, today was the turn of the cinnamon bun ... the verdict, 10 out of 10!"
- **Edvin Kibus** (5/5): "Great little brunch spot! We tried their energy balls — super tasty and perfect as a snack. They also have a nice selection of local Thai kombuchas, which we really enjoyed. Friendly service, great atmosphere, and overall a lovely place. Highly recommend!"
