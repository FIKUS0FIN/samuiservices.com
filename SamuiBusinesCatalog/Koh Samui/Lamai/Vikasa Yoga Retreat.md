### Top Reviews
- **P T** (5/5): "Stunning location with top notch healthy food and service. Some of the best ocean views I’ve ever seen. It’s worth the trip to the island just to come here. Highly recommended for yoga but also relaxing. The spa was very good. Rooms are very comfortable with healthy snacks inside. The only downside are the stairs but using the buggy service really helps."
- **Renato Rottmann** (5/5): "We stayed at Vikasa in Koh Samui for three nights, and without any doubt, it was a truly special experience. This place radiates a unique, deep, and authentic energy that you can feel from the very first moment.  The staff was always incredibly kind, welcoming, and helpful, making us feel at home at all times. The yoga teachers were outstanding: an exceptionally high level of professionalism, experience, and communication, with classes suitable for every level, from beginners to more advanced practitioners.  I had already been here two years ago for a longer stay and had a wonderful experience, but this year, together with Francesca, we truly fell in love with this place all over again. It managed to surprise us once more, exceeding even our best memories.  The location is simply perfect: beachfront, surrounded by nature, and at the same time strategically positioned to explore Koh Samui and the nearby islands. An ideal balance between practice, relaxation, and discovery.  We leave with deep joy and heartfelt gratitude for such an intense and rejuvenating experience. We will definitely return and can only highly recommend Vikasa to anyone who wishes to practice high-quality yoga and enjoy Koh Samui in a truly magical place.  Thank you from the heart ❤️  Renato & Francesca"
- **Felicity Lathrop** (5/5): "I came to Vikasa for the Recharge personal program in October. From the moment I arrived, the staff were welcoming, helpful, and went above and beyond to make sure my stay was everything I could have imagined. The program itself includes all the yoga classes on offer at the White Prana Sala, with the addition of daily private sessions to work through the Recharge program material. The content was just what I needed, and Kara was a beautifully wise and safe guide through it all. I was also supported by Eva, who made sure that I always felt comfortable at Vikasa and was there to answer any questions I might have. My prana cabana room was beautiful, the balcony looked over the beach and I spent hours just sitting outside listening to the birds, watching fireflies, and meditating to the sound of the waves. The food here is next level, so incredibly delicious, and the vegan ice cream is something I could (and did!) happily eat every day. Yes, there are a lot of stairs, but they are a great warmup for your practice, and the infinity pool is always there to cool off in. I often had the whole pool to myself, which was gorgeous. I would highly recommend a Vikasa personal program if you want to spend a week in a beautiful location, working on your practice and yourself. Thank you to everyone there for such a beautiful stay! Oh, and I can't forget Sally's massages, they were HEAVEN!"
