### Top Reviews
- **Emery Ellis** (4/5): "Beautiful atmosphere, right by the water and aesthetic decor. I ordered to the fried rice with pineapple and chicken + a passion shake. The presentation of the meal was beautiful and the rice was quite tasty with good flavour, however I found the chicken to be a bit dry. The passion shake was incredibly, super refreshing and juicy to keep cool because there is no fan/air conditioning in the cafe. Overall I and a positive experience and would recommend."
- **Tristan Genest** (5/5): "This restaurant exceeded my girlfriend and I expectations. First off as soon as we got to the restaurant’s lobby, smiles and greetings. Pulled chairs when we got to the table and towels on the lap to avoid a mess. Drinks were Happy Hour so 2 for 1 and tasted very good. Food was cheap money wise yet so good. I had the fish burger which was absolutely fenomenal and my girlfriend had a rice dish with pork, which she loved as well. The place is beautiful and kept clean. Big plus is i have a peanut allergy and it was taken care of very smoothly. All around a great restaurant. Very recommended."
- **AHMED.A** (5/5): "Great place with amazing food, excellent service, and a stunning view directly on the beach. Highly recommended. We loved coming here for lunch—try their combos, they are huge and delicious."
