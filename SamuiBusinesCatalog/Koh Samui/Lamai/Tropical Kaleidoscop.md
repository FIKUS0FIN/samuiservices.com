- **Svetlana Katson** (5/5): "Finally, a normal summer camp has appeared on the island that helps to raise children, and not a club with the task of entertaining children for a few hours.  Very close to what we had in childhood: a camp with an overnight stay, the need to build long-term communication and relationships, team sports activities, hikes, normal nutrition, exercise and discipline.  The camp is run by experienced adult women with the best combination of education: a leader and a teacher.  This allows them to lead the children, and not merge under their destructive injections. Children monitor the cleanliness of their rooms, their own hygiene, work, develop independence, learn to be friends, communicate freshly, build a team, overcome difficulties, help each other in close contact.  The camp is based on a travel agency, which gives it good capacity: children regularly go on excursions and sports events by minibus, go to the sea, spend the night in a hotel, eat well. My children, 10 and 7 years old, spent 8 days in the camp with overnight stays, returned tanned, physically stronger, happy and most importantly they became calmer, more polite in their conversations, more friendly with each other, more independent and more organized. They have developed a desire to take responsibility and grow up. The children want to repeat this experience, I am also ready because I see that they return as a better version of themselves than when they left - for me this is the most important indicator.  We will meet on October holidays, but for now, to maintain the effect and see the friends we have made, we periodically use the weekends."
