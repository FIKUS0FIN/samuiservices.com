### Top Reviews
- **Zaza Meerveld** (5/5): "House of Suzy in Lamai is easily one of our favorite spots in Samui. This speakeasy-style bar has such a unique vibe, and every visit feels like a great night out waiting to happen.  The tapas are delicious, the cocktails are creative and perfectly crafted, and the overall atmosphere just pulls you in. We love coming here to eat, drink, and dance—it’s the kind of place where you can spend the whole evening without realizing how fast time flies.  The Chinese Deco interior is stunning and adds so much character to the space. The music is always on point, and they often have live performances that make the experience even more special.  If you’re in Samui and looking for somewhere with amazing food, drinks, and energy, this place is a must-visit. We’ll definitely keep coming back!"
