### Top Reviews
- **Kirk MacDonald** (5/5): "Jasmine Rice Restaurant is a popular beachfront dining spot located at the Samui Jasmine Resort in Lamai Beach, Koh Samui, known for serving authentic Thai, seafood, and international cuisine. The restaurant offers a romantic, laid-back atmosphere with tables on the sand, live music, and a focus on fresh, local ingredients. Not a better way to finish a beautiful day on the beach than to have a great meal on the beach. We ordered Khao Soi and Tom Yum with noodle and a couple glasses of wine...fantastic. Congratulations Chef"
- **Bernhard R.** (5/5): "Jasmine Rice is a wonderful beachfront restaurant with a super friendly service and an unbeatable location right on the beach. The relaxed atmosphere is made even better by live music, which creates the perfect setting for a laid-back dinner by the sea.  The dishes are excellent, especially the Pad Thai, the refreshing Papaya Salad, and the flavorful Beef Curry. While the prices are higher compared to nearby food markets or smaller local places, the quality, setting, and overall experience make it absolutely worth it.  A great spot for anyone looking to enjoy delicious Thai food with stunning beach views and warm hospitality."
- **lofgren1992** (5/5): "Absolutely wonderful food! Staff is very friendly and if you are looking for tasty Thai food this is the place! Highly recommend the seafood barbecue menu!  So fresh and cooked to perfection."
