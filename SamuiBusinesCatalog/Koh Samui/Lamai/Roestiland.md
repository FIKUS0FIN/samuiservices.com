- **Steve Dempsey** (3/5): "The food at Rostiland was pleasing to the palette.  However, we were taken back by the attitude of the owner when we were asking questions and ordering food.  My partner asked if we could share the raclette and the owner was flabbergasted that we would even suggest that we would share a meal.  We have been travelling for well over two months and we typically share a meal as we find the portions are generally too large.  He went on about  a portion is a portion and that is how we should order it, a portion for my partner and a portion for myself, or a meal apiece.  My partner than asked about sharing a different meal, the meat loaf, and would that be suitable to share.  At that point the owner asked if the sharing of meals was a financial issue.  What a rude, unacceptable thing to say to a paying customer.  The cost of the meal has never been the problem but rather portion size.  We felt extremely insulted by his accusation of it being the cost of the meal, not the fact that too large a portion is a waste of food.  The meat loaf, which we did share was very tasty, but I believe the owner should be careful with his accusatory statements."
