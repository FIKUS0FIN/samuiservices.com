### Top Reviews
- **Ieva Vilcāne** (4/5): "Overall our stay in this hotel was nice. Location is perfect for those who like peace and quiet. The owner of the hotel is very friendly and helpful, and really gives you the impression that you can ask him anything. Also all hotel staff is very nice, polite and makes you feel welcomed in this hotel. There was very intense smell of the air freshener in the 2nd floor room when we moved in and since I am very sensitive to the smells the hotel owner kindly moved us to another room on the first floor. Room on the first floor was much bigger and also looked much better. Only room minus was that during the heavy rain whole bathroom was wet, because in a place of large window area is a fine mesh. There were also lot of ants, but they don't do any harm to the hotel guests, so it is ok to share the room with these neighbors. Rooms were clean, but I would recommend to the hotel staff to change the towels for the guests each day, because especially during the rainy days there is no option for guests to dry them and by the end of the second day those towels already start to have bad smell. Breakfast offer is very tasty, but each day the same. Pool was very clean and we liked the fact that it was private. By private I mean, that we were almost the only ones who used it. And this hotel is definitely a very family friendly place."
- **Emre San** (5/5): "I was here with my son and my mom and we all loved it. The pool is very nice to relax and the restaurant has good food too. The breakfast is good and healthy. The Location is not perfect but by motorbike or foot you can go to the nice spots within 10-20 min. (Lamai Beach/night market/ grandfather Rock etc.)  We will come again !"
- **Josie Holmgren** (5/5): "This place is fabulous. Great size rooms, both family and doubles. Really relaxed environment and the owners are truly amazing with guests. Would recommend if you are travelling with kids. Only minus is the location, takes about 15-20 min to walk to the beach. For us it was worth it since we felt very welcomed and taken care of. Highly recommend"
