How can you not offer fresh sea food on a beachfront location on a popular Thai island?  Chicken skewer plate 300 THB served without salad pretty strange. To be honest, for a French owner, you would expect a higher standard.  Besides that, the pizza was good. Great taste.  Anyhow, it's a nice place to have a drink and perhaps to have a snack or 2 but nothing more.  Would love to see the kitchen improving!  Cheers."
