### Top Reviews
- **Engva** (5/5): "I only stayed for three nights, but it truly felt like I got to experience a real piece of paradise. The rooms are spacious and comfortable, and the entire property is beautifully maintained and set in stunning surroundings, with easy access to the beach - which was dreamy to walk along 💕  The service was excellent across the board - from reception to the kitchen staff and housekeeping. I really enjoyed the food and cocktails, and appreciated the convenience of being able to communicate via WhatsApp and order room service easily 👌  If I had to mention a couple of downsides, it would be the Wi-Fi (since I unfortunately had to work during my stay), and the occasional tiny ant in the room that seemed quite fond of my keyboard. There were also quite a few families around, which sometimes made it harder to fully relax by the pool 🫣  Even so, the place absolutely deserves full marks, and I would happily stay here again 🤗"
- **Amornrut Ruangvilaisup** (5/5): "We received a complimentary room upgrade, which was a lovely surprise. The room was very clean, spacious, and the bed was extremely comfortable. The sea view was beautiful and made our stay even more relaxing. The staff were friendly, helpful, and welcoming throughout our stay. Overall, a great experience and I would happily stay here again."
- **Kiera Willis** (4/5): "Nice place. The room we had was very spacious and had comfy pillows. It was very clean and well presented. The on-site restaurant had lots of options for food and drinks. The staff were friendly and helpful. Only downsides were that the aircon in the room was rather loud even on the low fan setting, and the room let in quite a lot of light at night through cracks in the door etc."
