### Top Reviews
- **City of Angels** (5/5): "Chill casual beach bar and restaurant on Lamai beach. You can sit on the beach in the comfortable bean bags or inside at the upstairs restaurant. Food was good and reasonable prices. There’s also a swimming pool and towels available at no extra charge. Friendly staff and quick service. Enjoyed it."
- **Richard Lycett** (5/5): "Great service on the beach. Matthew is a star. WiFi. Free pool. Beach bean bags. Service to your bean bag. Often a DJ there. Great tunes. Sometimes fire shows as well."
- **Babie L M** (5/5): "This place has it all! From the moment you walk through the big front doors you are greeted by a beautiful ocean view, ocean breeze and some perfect tunes. The food and beverages were delicious. I don’t drink alcohol but everything I ordered was affordable and freshly made. The staff is so cool. You have options of inside or outside dinning. I couldn’t choose because there were both gorgeous ❤️. I sat on the comfy bean chairs by the beach. You don’t like the sand, NO Problem! They have a pool and a wash off station which is perfect for cleaning up before you hit the road. Be sure to check them out if you’re in Lamai, Koh Samui . Check out the full video on Tik Tok ❤️ Always tip your servers, they work so hard to please you. Can’t wait to return next year!"
