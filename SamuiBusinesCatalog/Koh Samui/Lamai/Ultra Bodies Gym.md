### Top Reviews
- **Jaan R9** (4/5): "It’s a really great gym with very friendly staff who keep things organized and make sure everyone follows the rules. The gym has a wide variety of good equipment, though the selection is understandably limited given the size of the facility. As a result, it can get a bit crowded in the mornings. Overall, it’s very clean and tidy. Towels are included. For a daily rate of 300 THB, it’s a great deal."
- **Tazio Lai** (4/5): "Great gym located in an easy to find area and with lots of scooter parking.  Definitely on the smaller side size wise, but packed with everything you need for a great workout. Day pass was 250baht which was very fair for the facilities.  Gym was spotless and machines well maintained, 2 floors with cardio upstairs and weights downstairs, and fully air conditioned!  Worth a visit if you want to workout on holidays."
- **Michael Wolff** (4/5): "Small community older well used gym with a good selection of machines and freeweights. Dumbbells to 50kg. Overall, I feel lucky this gym is here and use it almost every day. So this isn't complaining... it's merely suggesting.  The equipment needs some serious help. Cut my leg just barely brushing against the leg curl squeezing by. Judging by the electrical tape... I wasn't the first victim. The lateral raise for delts, as well as the abductor and adductor machines are all seriously misaligned. The standing calf looks about to have an "event" judging by the wear on the upper bolt.  The bearings in the Smith are done. The ezbar for biceps is worn out like I've never seen.. for just a bar. There is no way to clean any of the equipment before or after use. And its usually 28ish Celcius in the gym. 🥵 to their credit, I’ve seen the a/c guys in there twice trying to remedy that."
