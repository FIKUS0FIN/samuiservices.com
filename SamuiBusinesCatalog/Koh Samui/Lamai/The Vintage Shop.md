A large variety of polos, t shirts, long sleeves, rugbys, sweatshirts and button shirts in all sizes and colours.  Prices all hovering around at least market value, closer towards premium  - but some really nice bits in here and a good mix of modern era and true vintage Polo.  Some Polo denim, vintage Levis, Lacoste, Tommy Bahama and band shirts thrown in the mix also.  Dropped in on two separate occasions this week, both times the staff were super friendly and welcoming.  A dangerous place to visit if you happen to have plenty of space leftover in your suitcase!"
