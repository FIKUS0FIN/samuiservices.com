### Top Reviews
- **Elvira** (5/5): "🌿✨ One of the best vegetarian restaurants on Koh Samui!  The food was absolutely incredible. The Smokey BBQ Bowl with Jackfruit was packed with flavor and perfectly balanced, and the Gluten Free Veggie Wrap was fresh, delicious, and surprisingly satisfying. And Cutest pancakes 🥞🥰💚  Everything tasted healthy without sacrificing flavor. The atmosphere is peaceful and cozy, and the staff are genuinely kind and welcoming.  A true vegetarian gem that even non-vegetarians will love. We can’t wait to come back! 🥹🌱🙏🏼"
- **Guy Braverman** (4/5): "Part of a chain with locations across Thailand. I’ve eaten at one of their other locations previously and the taste and presentation were very consistent. The offering is mostly western, bowls and sandwiches and some dishes with an American Mexican flair. The food feels clean, healthy and tasty, although really not overwhelming in any way. Extra points for being 100% vegan. Definitely recommended if you are around Lamai, but I wouldn’t make a special trip if elsewhere. Serving sizes are very generous, and prices although a little high, feel average for Koh Samui."
- **Timon Koths** (5/5): "Pure Vegan Heaven is an absolute must-visit! We had an incredible meal where every dish felt like a curated experience. The Food: The Burmese salad and tacos were standout stars—each bite was a complex, rich explosion of flavor. Also the waffles were divine. The Drinks: Their Kefir was refreshing and tangy, hitting just the right spot, and the coffee is excellent. Between the creative menu and the quality of the ingredients, this spot is a total gem. Already our second time here"
