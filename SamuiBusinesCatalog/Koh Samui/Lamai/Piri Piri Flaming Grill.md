### Top Reviews
- **Glasgow girl** (4/5): "Came in for dinner. Service was excellent, friendly staff. Husband ordered half chicken snd ordered Piri Piri wrap, both came either chips. Food didn’t come quick but that’s ok as you can see them making everything fresh to order. Drinks a little more expensive than other places but food makes up for it. Our food was delicious and the extensive selection of sauces on the table was brilliant. Note they add 7% vat to the bill which we didn’t notice on the menu. Would recommend."
- **Kimberley Ramsay** (4/5): "Service was good, immediately shown to a table and our server took the order but forgot to bring both drinks.  Food came quickly which was piping hot, and tasty! Very similar concept to Nando’s, with different sauces available too.  Would recommend a visit if you’re craving some peri peri chicken!"
- **Megan Norris** (1/5): "The staff here were very attentive and helpful I cannot fault any of the staff here apart from the chef who neglected to cook my chicken. As you can see from the pictures it is still clucking. Not a happy customer considering this was the most expensive meal of my three weeks travelling."
