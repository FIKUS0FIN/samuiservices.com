### Top Reviews
- **Barry Musgrave** (5/5): "A really cool place. I didn't try the food but enjoyed a cold beer and the relaxing atmosphere. Music was great. Lots of seating and at different levels, each turn revealed a surprise. The staff were a bit too cool for school and seemed very indifferent as to whether you stayed or left. This was the only downside."
- **Modern Nomads** (5/5): "The Rock Bar in Koh Samui offers a charming seaside experience with great hospitality and a relaxed atmosphere. The fried rice was well-prepared, and the banana with coconut milk made for a delightful local dessert. Combined with the scenic ocean views and calm setting, it’s an excellent spot to unwind and enjoy authentic Thai flavors. Highly recommended for travelers seeking a serene dining experience by the beach.  Rastafari Vibes!!"
- **Tiffany Ponsot** (5/5): "The best bar in Koh Samui! ☀️ What can we ask for more? You have the view, the wonderful drinks and food, the good music and the kindness of the owners 🥰 You can only feel relaxed when you’re in there, so natural and beautiful"
