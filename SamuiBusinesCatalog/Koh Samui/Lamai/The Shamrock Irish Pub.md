### Top Reviews
- **Taisiya Lyubimova** (5/5): "Amazing musicians play here almost every night. The best! And for that reason alone, it's a favorite bar on Lamai. The bland cocktails and the long queue for the pool table are secondary. The music is amazing!"
- **Carl Greenall** (5/5): "Only went there for drinks, good music with a live band 6 nights/week starting at 9:30. The night off varied. Service generally good but obviously a bit slower on very busy nights, a trainee started the last night we were there so even this is being addressed. Happy hours between 3 and 9. Great place for a few drinks and a dance, we went there for both my and my wife's birthday."
- **Friendly Customer** (3/5): "No food. They have happy hour for cocktails at 99B. I ordered a Mojito and a Pina Colada, watery and tasteless. Left before the live band started and will not be back."
