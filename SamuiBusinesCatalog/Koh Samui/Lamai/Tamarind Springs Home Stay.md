### Top Reviews
- **Ghada Mallah** (4/5): "Extremely friendly and welcoming staff — truly some of the nicest people. I loved every part of the premises; it’s beautiful, peaceful, and very well designed in nature. The massage itself was very good and relaxing.  We booked a couple’s massage and also experienced the caves, natural spring areas, and body scrub treatments, which made the whole experience feel very complete and unique.  The only area I feel could be improved is hygiene standards in certain parts of the facility, which would make the experience perfect.  Overall, a wonderful experience and I would still recommend it for anyone looking for a relaxing spa day in nature."
- **Zaza Meerveld** (5/5): "We visited Tamarind Springs Samui as a wedding gift from our friends and chose the “Stoned on Samui” package — and it was absolutely worth it.  From start to finish, the experience was incredibly relaxing and thoughtfully curated. We really appreciated the no-phone policy, which allowed us to fully disconnect and enjoy the peaceful surroundings. It made the whole spa day feel even more special and immersive.  The treatments were amazing, and everything was beautifully set in nature. One thing to keep in mind is that there’s quite a bit of walking and stairs involved, so you do need to be reasonably fit. But honestly, it just adds to the charm of the place.  We loved every moment and would highly recommend it for a special occasion or a proper treat!"
- **Renato Rottmann** (5/5): "We had an absolutely wonderful experience at Tamarind Springs. The entire setting is unique, serene, and incredibly well integrated into the natural surroundings.  The spa itself is simply amazing and unlike anything we had ever seen before. The sauna carved directly into the rocks is something truly special – a one-of-a-kind experience that you won’t easily find elsewhere. It feels authentic, original, and perfectly in harmony with the forest. Without a doubt, the experience is worth every penny and absolutely justifies the price.  The massage was exceptional: deeply relaxing, professional, and perfectly executed. On top of that, all the staff were incredibly kind, welcoming, and attentive in every aspect, making us feel comfortable and well taken care of from start to finish.
