### Top Reviews
- **Mark Meshe** (1/5): "Just avoid this place – not worth it  I live 2 minutes away from Samui Teerak and still decided to stop going. That says a lot.  First of all, there’s no A/C. It gets ridiculously hot inside, especially during the day. You’ll be dripping with sweat before you even start your workout.  Second, the equipment is super old and barely works. Machines are often broken or just feel unsafe to use. It honestly felt like they haven’t updated anything in years.  But the worst part? The lady at the front desk. I’ve never felt so unwelcome at a gym before. She’s constantly giving people bad looks and acting like you’re a problem just for walking in. One time she told me I hadn’t paid when I absolutely had — then asked me to pay again. Total scam vibes. It was uncomfortable every single morning.  There are way better gyms in Koh Samui with decent equipment, A/C, and staff who actually treat you like a human being.  I wouldn’t recommend this place to anyone."
- **Pipsa Ervasti** (4/5): "Gym with great equipment to have a good workout. Due to not having proper AC, I'm giving four stars. Fans helped a bit in between the sets but it was still a very sweaty workout. 😁"
- **Anna Bell** (5/5): "A great gym with all the equipment you need. The space is quite small but it never felt overcrowded, and I always managed to get the equipment I needed. Clean, loads of fans (although on a hot day they won’t help like an AC), and I also appreciate that the floor is hard and not covered with a gym mat-like material. No changing rooms though, I changed in the shower, plus nowhere to put a big bag"
