### Top Reviews
- **Clayton** (5/5): "This restaurant was one of the most memorable dining experiences I had in Koh Samui. My driver told me I had to check this place out and wow… what an absolute hidden gem. Tucked away in the jungle right on the beach, this spot has one of the most peaceful and magical atmospheres I’ve experienced in Koh Samui.  The ambience here is unreal. Beautiful decor, incredible beachfront setting, calm tranquil waters, and the most amazing ocean breeze that honestly made me never want to leave. It feels completely off the beaten path in the best possible way… quiet, relaxing, authentic, and surrounded by nature. At one point I swear I heard at least 15 different languages being spoken around me, which somehow made the whole experience feel even more special and connected.  The food was absolutely amazing too. Everything tasted fresh and full of flavor, and the entire experience just felt special from start to finish.  Leo loved it as much as I did 🐾 The staff were welcoming, the vibe was calm and cozy, and it’s the kind of place you accidentally spend hours at because you simply don’t want to leave.  One of my favorite discoveries on the island. If you’re looking for a peaceful beachfront escape away from the crowds, this place is a must visit."
- **Katherine Tar** (5/5): "We stumbled here for supper from our nearby hotel and what a beautiful surprise. Gorgeous seaside setting, fun design and smiling staff. I recommend reserving ahead of time if you’re coming for supper since it was quite busy, and we didnt get a table outside by the sea because we didn't have one. We will most definitely be back again before we leave."
- **Martin Alexander** (2/5): "Restaurant review: The food and drinks are good here, but definitely don't order the children's spaghetti with butter and a little cheese. For 120 baht, you get such a small portion that no child will be satisfied. I spent a total of 2,700 baht here and consulted with the manager, who refused to give me a 20 baht discount for the insufficient portion. The highlight was that they ended up charging 200 baht for this portion. No willingness to satisfy the customer, on the contrary, they will laugh at you by trying to charge you even more."
