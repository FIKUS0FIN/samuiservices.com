### Top Reviews
- **Laura Williams** (5/5): "We stayed here for a week and are looking forward to going back for another. The rooms are spacious with comfortable beds. The AC is excellent and so is the staff. Outstanding value and amazing location on the beach!  Marie is a laid back hostess and if you need anything all you have to do is ask. This has been our home away from home. The cocktails are made by a brilliant bartender, they are strong and taste lovely."
- **Johana C. S.** (1/5): "Then only good thing is the location. We walked in and out, payed 1 night but didn't stay. Dirty, dusty, cat walking everywhere even the kitchen. I was glad there is a nice place to stay next door. The owner might be a nice lady (as told by some poeple)  but we didn't get to meet her, the other ones were not very welcoming and they forgot about our booking. Total mess. I hope they improve the place and service because here is a lot of potential."
- **Mags Hammill** (2/5): "We didn't stay here this is solely based on the restaurant. I'll start off by saying at least the beers were good because they were cold. We ordered shrimp with garlic and pepper and penang seafood. Both dishes weren't even close to that. The shrimp was battered in way I can't even describe, actually it wasn't even supposed to be battered so I'm not sure what happened there. But even if it was the batter was disgusting; soggy, cold and obviously not fried properly. The penang wasn't penang. Maybe a sweet coconut soup at best. Didn't taste awful but shouldn't have been labelled penang. Buuut all of this makes sense because we found out later the chef was a foreigner. Can't expect amazing local food from a non native. Too bad the menu is only Thai food. The staff were a lot of fun though cause they were pretty much blackout drunk. Maybe a fun place to party but don't eat here."
