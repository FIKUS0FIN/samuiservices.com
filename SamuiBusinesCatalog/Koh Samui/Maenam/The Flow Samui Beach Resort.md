### Top Reviews
- **Sofia Maksimova** (5/5): "We really loved this place — honestly had such a great stay ❤️ The hotel is small, quiet, and right by the sea, which was a huge plus for us. We stayed for 9 days and had a really good, relaxing time.  To be honest, we didn’t expect much from a 3-star hotel, but the service was surprisingly great — that was a very nice surprise. The overall atmosphere and staff made it even better.  The food was tasty, and even though breakfast was quite simple, we genuinely enjoyed it — it had everything we needed.  The beach was good too: right in front of the hotel the entry is a bit rocky, but if you walk a little to the right, there’s a much nicer spot to get into the water. The water isn’t super clear because of the sandy/muddy bottom, but there were almost no waves, so it was still really enjoyable to swim.  Since the hotel is small, the level of quiet really depends on the guests. Most of the time it was very peaceful, but on our last day a noisy group arrived, which was a bit annoying — luckily, it was already our last day.  So just a small note for future guests: please be mindful of others and respect the quiet atmosphere — this isn’t a party hotel.  Overall, definitely a place we’d come back to 🌿"
- **Supaporn Jamlek** (5/5): "The sea view here is absolutely beautiful, with a full panoramic view that feels incredibly relaxing. It’s the perfect place to sit back, unwind, and enjoy the atmosphere. The food is delicious and of great quality. The pasta, in particular, was rich and flavorful, with fresh, well-cooked shrimp. Paired with crispy garlic bread, it was a perfect combination. The drinks were refreshing and easy to enjoy, making them ideal for a laid-back afternoon by the sea. What truly stood out was the service. Special thanks to Khun Korn and the entire team for the warm welcome from the moment we arrived. Everyone was polite, friendly, and attentive, which made the whole dining experience even more enjoyable. This is definitely one of the must-visit spots in Koh Samui, whether for the view, the food, or the overall ambiance. I’ll definitely be coming back next time to enjoy the day pass by the pool and spend more time relaxing here. 🌊✨"
- **Claire England** (5/5): "Just wow. I’ve been visiting maenam beach for 10years and always enviously looked into the place!! Anyway fast forward to my first stay there. Beautiful rooms, all backing onto a slick pool. Really good food, happy hour cocktails. The highlight is the infinity pool looking out over the vast blue calm sea. Oh and at night the pool has beautiful sparkling lights in it! Will be back."
