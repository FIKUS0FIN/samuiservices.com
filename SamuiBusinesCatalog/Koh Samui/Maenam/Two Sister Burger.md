### Top Reviews
- **Stefan Pichler** (4/5): "⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐☆  I go into this review wanting to be fair — there were good moments, but some things didn’t live up to the expectations set by earlier reviews. The service was friendly and the ambiance pleasant, and that’s worth credit.  However, the fried rice was nothing special — just average, not really memorable. And the acai bowl I ordered also fell short; I was hoping it would be exceptional given the glowing reviews I had seen, but it was simply okay.  Overall, I’d still give it 4 stars — it’s a good place, and there are definite positives, but I can’t say it wowed me in every dish."
- **Hannah whittington** (5/5): "such a sweet little spot with the best vegan food. The chili basil with Tempe was delicious - make sure to add the soy sauce with ginger and fresh chillies 🌶️ and the vegan carrot cake sooooo yum"
- **Eddy** (5/5): "Best place to eat in all of MaeNam. I had breakfast and lunch here a couple times. Amazing selection of food.  With some Thai and western options.  Def check out their breakfasts. I’m not vegan but I do love good vegan food.  And they have a great selection of it."
