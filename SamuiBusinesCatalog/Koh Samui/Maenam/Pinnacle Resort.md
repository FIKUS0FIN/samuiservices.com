### Top Reviews
- **AJAY BEHERA** (4/5): "Rooms were really nice. Had a garden view. But the electrical sockets were very loose. Not a single socket was working properly for plugging the charger. Initially we were given 2 rooms on the 3rd floor and in one of the rooms water from AC was leaking. After informing the reception they gave us 2 rooms on the ground floor. Same type rooms. We had to carry our  luggages up and down the stairs. No staff were there to help us carry our lugguages.  There was shortage of staff as informed by the lady at the reception. The food at the restaurant was good( we had lunch there on the last day of our trip) . It's the pool and the beach of this property that is awesome and makes it worthwhile. Overall the staff were courteous and quite responsive. FYI Coco palms is the adjacent property."
- **D Lee** (4/5): "Had a  really wonderful stay at Pinnacle. It’s right on the beach, and although at the end of the bay and quite far from most things, would be good if you’re looking for a calmer place to stay. The room was spacious with a comfortable bed and had everything you would need. The bathroom had 2 different shower heads (fixed and handheld). They provide beach towels upon arrival in your room, or you can also ask the restaurant on the beach. They also provided toiletries (shower cap, qtips, toothbrushes and shaver) and water bottles daily. Breakfast was also great with a decent variety of options including fruits and fresh eggs that can be made to order. The grounds were quite beautiful and well vegetated which created a nice atmosphere. There were plenty of lounge chairs along the beach as well as around the pool, and I didn’t normally have a hard time finding an empty chair. There is also a spa where you can get a massage for a very comparable price to the massage parlours along the road. The massages are done along the beach on a 2nd floor open air room which was nice.  The only thing I would give feedback on was to the receptionist upon my check in. Upon giving me my room key she loudly announced my room number, and the exact location of the room for others in the lobby to overhear, and as a solo traveller this made me a little bit uncomfortable.  Overall, I felt safe and comfortable at this resort, but hope that they can avoid doing this to future solo travellers."
- **Ben Waart** (5/5): "We have just stayed 6 days at this resort. We had an amazing time here. From the moment you step inside it feels like walking in a botanic garden. The bungalow was also great. Spacious, good air conditioning. Only the shower doesnt get so hot. It was hot, cold, hot, cold. But its so hot outside that a cold shower didnt bother us that much. The view from the swimming pool is amazing. Direct view at the sea. The staff was also very friendly and helpfull. They cleaned the bungalow every day. And put new towels every day. The breakfast was also good. Not a five star restaurant. But nothing to complain about.  We didnt understand why there are so many reviews that its so remotely located. That there is nothing around. There is everything around. Just outside there are lots of other food places. There is even a 7-11. On 1200 meters. About 15 min walk. Also places where you can rent a scooter just couple of meters to walk. I didnt had a international licence with me. So could not rent a scooter. But you can just get a Grab taxi with the Grab app. And taxi is there in 2/3 min. And can take you to lots of places.  Also the matrass was in the beginning very hard. But in the days after they put new Topper matresses in all the rooms. Wich made it softer to sleep.  If you want a quiet relaxing place to stay, With natural environments, And still easy acces to all the highlights. I can definetely recommend to stay at this place."
