### Top Reviews
- **Michael O'Hagan** (5/5): "Super fun. Good mix of technical and flat terrain. Even more fun if you've driven a quad bike before. We paid £62 approx for 1 hour (2 people on one quad) through Get Your Guide, which included pick up and drop off from our hotel.  We ended up having one guide take just us around, so private tour essentially.  Bit of advice: if there's a dip, try and straddle it, so both wheels are either side of the dip, and that way the quad stays mostly flat"
- **Marco B.** (5/5): "I booked the full two hours for like 70$ and it was absolutely worth it. It was a real experience and a lot of fun. Through the jungle and across rivers – it was a true adventure! It's important to note that the route is very steep and bumpy in places. This makes the ride challenging. I wouldn't necessarily recommend it for inexperienced riders or children."
- **Ian W.** (5/5): "In 2023 we booked a 2-hour Jungle ATV tour and we had the best time of our lives on Koh Samui! We are coming back in 2025! The ATVs were fun and easy to operate, the jungle was amazing, we saw snakes, and went through rivers! This company is professional and good!"
