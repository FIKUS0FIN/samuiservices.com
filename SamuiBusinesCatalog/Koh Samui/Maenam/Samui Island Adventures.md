### Top Reviews
- **Sterling LaPratt** (5/5): "I had a great time. This is good for all levels of riders. But you must notify them of your skill level.  There are easy paths, medium, and hard paths.  There are plenty of stops along the way in the mountains if you need a break.  My bike ran good and was set up good."
- **Philipp Andermatt** (5/5): "We had a blast on our Enduro tour! We were equipped with great protective gear and bikes. The route (we chose the medium-level route) contained a good mix of challenging and fun dirt roads and back country roads. The guide did a great job as well, always making sure that we were fine and explaining how to get through tough sections."
- **Ashley Jackson** (5/5): "Absolute blast!!!! Highly recommended for anyone who wants that adrenaline kick. Did the full day(7 hours) tour around the mountains, ps- the routes change from narrow tracks to rocky hills in a blink of an eye. The guide was amazing, so enthusiastic towards everything and brought only good vibes which really made my day being a solo traveller as it was only me and the guide. He took me to temples, did the samui zip lines, took me to a waterfall for swimming which isn't open to the public, and had food on top of the mountain in an amazing restaurant with a breath taking view. He took a lot of photos and videos for me and you can also rent a GoPro for your helmet for an extra 1000bht. I can't say enough about this place and the staff..."
