### Top Reviews
- **Alberto Centioni** (4/5): "Pros: - Equipped with all the machines you need, surpassing the typical hotel gym. - Cool vibes and affordable prices, plus it's not crowded.  Cons: - The heat can be intense; best to train in the morning. - Uneven flooring can be noticeable during heavy squat or deadlift sessions. - Machines and weights often have grease on them; a warning sign or notification would be helpful. While greasing machines is understandable for an open-air gym, a heads-up would have been appreciated."
- **Marjo Heikka** (5/5): "Lovely, authentic and special gym with open walls and fans. Friendly personnel, you get a towel and price is only 100 baht. Applied a little and was able to do all the moves in my training program. I definitely recommend this place. Must experience."
- **Vladimir Miletic** (5/5): "Very nice gym, affordable (100 baht per day), with good equipment and great vibes. I definitely recommend it.  Keep in mind that it’s an outdoor gym, so you should definitely wear insect repellent."
