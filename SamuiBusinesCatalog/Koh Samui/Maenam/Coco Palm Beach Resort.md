### Top Reviews
- **adam šedovič** (5/5): "We stayed in a cute little beach house #212 right by the sea and were really happy with it. Breakfast was included and it was perfectly fine. Just a short walk away is the pier for boats to Koh Phangan and Koh Tao – super convenient, as we spent a few days on Koh Tao. The boats were barely noticeable, so no issue at all. Honestly, this turned out to be the best beach on the whole island! We didn’t expect that, but there were almost no people, the water gets deep immediately (no rocks, no water shoes needed), and right next to the beach there’s a bar/restaurant with clean toilets. Perfect! We explored about half of Koh Samui, but this beach was easily our favorite. The area is nice and quiet – not many options around, but everything you need is within a 10–15 minute walk. Highly recommended! :)"
- **Andrej Michal** (5/5): "Nice accommodation offering a comfortable family vacation and great value for money. The location is excellent: right on a calm and clean beach with pristine waters, in a quiet area with plenty of good and inexpensive restaurants and bars nearby, as well as minimarts, massage parlors, and a laundry service. The beach overlooks the Pralarn Pier, but we did not find the ferry traffic bothersome for our enjoyment of the beach.  The property consists of many bungalows of different sizes and amenities. We stayed in the Bungalow Deluxe and although it was simple and not very large, it was sufficient for our purposes. Most importantly, the bungalow was clean, well appointed, and quite cozy. Complimentary coffee was a nice addition, and we also liked the spacious veranda.  The bungalows are surrounded by beautiful, well-maintained gardens and greenery. The property overall is styled in a pleasant traditional oriental setting. The pool was clean and sufficiently large, and there were plenty of sun loungers available both by the pool and on the beach.  Breakfast was served buffet-style and was plentiful and varied, with many traditional as well as local dishes on offer, including fruit salad, filtered coffee, and the option to order various styles of eggs.  Everything during our stay was perfect and we really enjoyed our time at this family-oriented hotel."
- **Eric Bionova** (4/5): "I have stayed here for 10 nights. Location is very quiet, nice, but there is nothing to do in the vicinity. Except for a number of reasonable restaurants. Had a villa with private pool which is very spacious and good, but it needs a renovation. Houses are clean on the other side. Atmosphere is nice and personnel is friendly and nice. Breakfast is very simple and every day the same. Anyhow, interesting place for a reasonable price."
