### Top Reviews
- **Рааррр** (5/5): "I visited this coffee shop and tried several different coffees and dishes, and I absolutely loved everything. The quality of the coffee is truly high-grade, you can really taste the care and professionalism in every cup. The food was also excellent — especially the pumpkin soup with bacon, which was absolutely amazing and stood out the most for me. The service was outstanding: friendly, attentive, and welcoming. Thank you for such a great experience, I’ll definitely be happy to come back again!"
- **Epolina** (4/5): "I enjoyed the syrniki and buckwheat porridge. The honey cake was just okay. However, some of the prices felt unreasonable. A cappuccino costs 150 baht, which is far more expensive than at other places like Flo, To Be Sweet, and many others. The guacamole was also disappointing: 110 baht for a very small scoop that wasn’t particularly tasty."
- **Евгений Боровиков** (5/5): "The Basque cheesecake was absolutely amazing — rich, creamy, and incredibly smooth. You can really tell that high-quality ingredients are used, and it tasted like it was made with premium Philadelphia cream cheese.  The Americano was excellent as well. It was served hot and had a great flavor. As it cooled down, more of its pleasant acidity came through, making the coffee even more interesting and enjoyable.  Everything was delicious. Highly recommended!"
