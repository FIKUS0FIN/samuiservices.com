### Top Reviews
- **Dave** (3/5): "We came to the W Hotel's Woobar and Namu Restaurant for my partner's birthday as they looked rather special.  We started, at the Woobar bar, up on the hillside, overlooking the sea - a wonderfully situated bar, with stunning views.  The cocktail menu had a good selection of classics and signatures. I tried a classic Margarita and one of their specials Thai Mai. Both very well done.  This was a really nice start to the evening apart from the atmosphere-wrecking music! Awful.  We absolutely hate this trend of taking a breathtaking setting—like the Woobar’s spectacular view—and drowning it in the same mind‑numbing, over‑amped beat for hours. Lucky us! We even had a DJ… though I’m not sure why, since absolutely nothing changed in the hour we were there. I thought a DJ's was to match music to the atmosphere and audience. He was absolutely useless. The place was almost deserted. Maybe it was this awful, ambience-wrecking cocophony.  Restaurants and bars, please: look at your surroundings, look at your guests, and maybe—just maybe—pick music that reflects your special place, and doesn't sound like a car alarm having an existential crisis.  We wouldn't sit here again being assaulted by this terrible, mindless cocophony, despite the fabulous view."
- **Jet_Set_Gypsea** (4/5): "A gorgeous bar with a stunning view.  Fabulous great quality cocktails and the best service.  The bar itself and view from the rooftop are gorgeous a very cool venue.  Very empty which meant it lacked a bit of atmosphere but definitely worth a visit none the less it would he great for sunset."
- **M Brix** (3/5): "A super beautiful bar that we visited one evening, and were able to enjoy a drink under an open starry sky. The bar is located on top of a small mountain, with a view of the sea. The staff was super super nice, and did a lot to make us feel welcome. We started with a seat close to the pool, but these seats were uncomfortable to say the least, as they consist of metal chairs without cushions. When one of the seats islands became available, we were fortunately offered this one. Our drink was one of the place's signature drinks, and therefore we expected a lot. Unfortunately, we expected more than we got, because it didn't taste very good, to say the least. When a drink has a high price, in my world there is also an expectation of a particularly good taste. And yes, it is probably a question of taste, but this experience did not give us the courage to spend more money, so we left the place again."
