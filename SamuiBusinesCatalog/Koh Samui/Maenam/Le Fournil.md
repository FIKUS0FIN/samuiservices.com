### Top Reviews
- **Denis Yumatov** (5/5): "Le Fournil French Bakery — A Little Piece of France in Koh Samui!  If you’re looking for an authentic French bakery experience on the island, look no further than Le Fournil. This charming gem in Maenam has been delighting locals and travelers alike since 2011 — and it’s easy to see why!  The coffee here is absolutely divine. Whether you go for a classic Espresso, a velvety Cappuccino, or a refreshing Iced Americano, every cup is crafted with care and passion. The flat white and macchiato are simply perfection — smooth, rich, and full of character.  The menu is unpretentious yet thoughtfully curated — great quality at very reasonable prices. You get real French bakery vibes without the Paris price tag!  The atmosphere is warm and welcoming — a perfect spot to start your morning, catch up with friends, or simply slow down and enjoy life island-style. ☕🥐  Le Fournil is not just a bakery — it’s a daily ritual for anyone lucky enough to discover it. A must-visit on Koh Samui — don’t leave the island without stopping by! 🇫✨"
- **Cynthia Spilman** (5/5): "French bakery in Thailand. We had the English breakfast which was huge & delicious! The bacon was personally smoked by chief/owner with coconut wood. The croissants were lovely! The orange juice was freshly squeezed and the coffee latte was perfect. Oh the cat was an added bonus."
- **Frank Taravella** (5/5): "Perfect breakfast in Maenam. French bakery concept with a limited variety of options to select from an each is excellent.  Most remarkable is the sweetest owner/manager of the restaurant who personally takes your order and brings an elegant sweetness to your morning to start the day.  Relax with a coffee and a croissant or have a full English breakfast.  Whatever best fuels your day.  See you again soon."
