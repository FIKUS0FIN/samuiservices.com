### Top Reviews

- **Carter Haggerty** (5/5): "Definitely my favorite restaurant on Koh Samui. A big menu selection, authentic Thai food and very kind staff. Always locals around enjoying themselves and having a great time. Very calm beachside tables for relaxation, romance or getting some work done. I have never been disappointed by the food during the countless times I have eaten here. Strong and cheap drinks, fantastic food and beautiful views. I’m not sure what more you could ask for!"
