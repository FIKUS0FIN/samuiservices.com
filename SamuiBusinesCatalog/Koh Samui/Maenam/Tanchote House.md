### Top Reviews
- **Neon S.** (2/5): "I am extremely disappointed with this establishment. Despite having a direct path to a public beach, the management refuses to let anyone use it. In an area where public access points are non-existent, this feels like an intentional attempt to "privatize" a public resource that belongs to everyone. It is frustrating and, in my opinion, legally questionable to block access to public land just to maintain an exclusive vibe or force people to walk miles around. A business that profits from its location near the coast should show more respect for the community and public rights. I will not be returning until they change this unfair policy."
- **Samui Guitar Lessons** (5/5): "The location of place 'LaOr Tanchote Samui House' is quite charming. The hostess of this small lakeside complex is a wonderful woman, Or, who is ready to help you whenever you need help. Although the price is low, the lodges are clean and cosy, air-conditioning and a fan are working.  The place is on the one hand quiet and peaceful and on the other hand it is not very far from the main road. The beach is a 10-minute walk away, and a 2-3-minute walk to the fresh fruit stand and the Family Mart 24-hour shop.  Tanchote lodges are home to relaxed and friendly visitors from different countries, who are often 'house buddies'.  Despite the water problem on the island, Or's tap water is clean enough, and for cooking and drinking we used the water stand nearby, where we can fill 10 litres of clean drinking water for 10 baht.  For a cheaper and better taxi service, you can ask Poo (the car, in contrast to Thailand's typical unpunctuality, arrives in advance).  My heartfelt thanks to Or for the hospitality provided and hopefully this review will help you choose a quality place to live on the paradise island of Samui.  Address: Mae Nam, Ko Samui District, Surat Thani, Tanchote Samui House (nearby the Clever Kids Development Centre)."
- **Paul May** (4/5): "Cheap clean affordable place to stay."
