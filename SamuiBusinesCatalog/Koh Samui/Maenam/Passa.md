### Top Reviews
- **Adi Mimran** (5/5): "The best dinner I’ve had in Thailand. The service was outstanding, the sea bass and the wagyu were exceptional, the calamari starter and the crab ravioli were absolutely perfect, and paired with the Italian wine recommended by the restaurant, it was an unforgettable experience."
- **Anastasija Terebej** (4/5): "Atmosphere is awesome! Cozy place! But the food, not sure. I ordered a lasagna. It was tasteless. Like they forgot spices in general?? Not sure if that's how they do it here (my first time). But it's definitely not how I'm used to. And I've eaten a lot of lasagnas in my life. Maybe some of the other dishes are better. I donno. Would recommend at own risk. Food looks good in pictures tho."
- **Hlwann Moe** (5/5): "I had a wonderful dining experience at this Italian restaurant. The steak was perfectly cooked,tender, juicy, and full of flavor,served with crispy fries and a rich, well-balanced sauce. The vegetables were fresh and beautifully prepared, adding great color and taste to the plate. Everything was presented thoughtfully and tasted even better than it looked. The quality of ingredients and attention to detail really stood out. Highly recommended for anyone looking for a satisfying and well-prepared Italian meal!😚"
