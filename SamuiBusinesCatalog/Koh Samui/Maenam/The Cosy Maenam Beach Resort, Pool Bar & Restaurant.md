### Top Reviews
- **Natalia Percival** (5/5): "Quiet place tucked away close to a few local food markets and many massage places. It consists of approx. 10 small detached bungalows, one per family. Our stay included breakfast (a choice of Thai, continental and English i think) served at the restaurant overlooking the sea. There is a small swimming pool which may be suitable for kids rather than adults. Beach beds with parasols are provided. Rooms are cleaned every day, all have ceiling fans, tv and air-conditioning, veranda. Bathrooms are modern. Make sure you close doors in the evening as there are some mosquitos especially towards evening hours. The service is great and accommodating.  There are 3/4 parking spaces which was plenty as we were the only guests with a car."
- **howard smith** (5/5): "What a wonderful resort. Great location, real standout is the amazing people. Special mention to King and Zaw who made me feel so welcome. Note the pool is small and not really for swimming - lovely however. Place seems French owned with a lot of French guests. As a Brit, what a pleasure :), met some great people here who I will visit in France. This place is so charming it could even be a viable alternative to the vastly more expensive 4S, RC etc. Bravo to this place. Staff culture world class."
- **Ole Connor** (5/5): "Nice quite place to stay. Clean rooms, great pool on the beach, good food and quiet beach 🏖️. Good reception service- but I recommend every guest to check the TAXI prices before. Don’t pay 700 BHT !!! when Grab is 400 BHT."
