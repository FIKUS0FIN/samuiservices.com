### Top Reviews

- **Ruth Brock** (5/5): "This place is amazing! Great food, service and more! We live nearby and eat here regularly."
- **Nick Tessalee** (5/5): "We have breakfast here twice and we like the food here. The parking was big enough we did not have any problem finding parking spot. My other favorites includes Chinese donut (Pro Tong Ko) and hot Thai Tea with condense milk! The only con for us were no air condition room so I need to have my towel with me to wipe my sweat. The service from the waiters were excellent! Overall, I would recommend this place for breakfast."
