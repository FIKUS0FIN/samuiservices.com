### Top Reviews
- **Lee Soon Guan** (5/5): "Introduced to me by my friend Tony. This place is incredibly nice and pleasant. The setting is relaxing with a bohemian lifestyle. We were greeted warmly by the owner Fanny. She introduced me to their own made Rum. It was very flavourful. Highly recommended! We ordered the mixed plate, pizza Regina and ending with Ba Ba au Rhum, a desert. The pictures below says it all...delicious! Don't miss this place when in Samui."
- **Alrike Coetzee** (5/5): "Great hidden gem. The rum and home-made syrup was really good. Lovely location, with friendly staff. Recommend the pizza"
- **Chrys Fernandez** (5/5): "Rhum and Islands -- a good fit! This is a great stop if you're in the south end of this island. The pizza was excellent, and the staff were lovely folks who walked us through the whole rum tasting thing :) If it's a hot day and you've ridden all the way from the North end, I believe you can also take a dip in their pool."
