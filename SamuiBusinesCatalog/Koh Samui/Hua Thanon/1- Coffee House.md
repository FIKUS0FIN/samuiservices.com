### Top Reviews
- **kiddie garden** (5/5): "Cute spot for a good coffee. Great for a coffee break and a little croissant snack. Would be nice if they had a breakfast, but this place is mostly coffee lovers oriented. Fresh good pastry though 🤗"
- **Philip Smith** (5/5): "Really excellent coffee. If you like americano try the medium light roast. It is very flavorful. Nice acidity, the blueberry note really comes through.  The staff are very nice and obviously take pride in how they roast the coffee and prepare each drink.  The indoor area is very small and sunny even by mid-morning. The outside area has some shade but still a bit hot."
- **Sven Fischer** (5/5): "Great coffee with a good service and a very friendly young owner.  Coffee is roasted by the company itself. You have a few choices of coffeebeans and roasting levels.  Futher you can choose a sweet piece or a ham cheese filled croissant. Very big garden outside. This is a place were you dont need to hurry. Arrive and enjoy the good taste."
