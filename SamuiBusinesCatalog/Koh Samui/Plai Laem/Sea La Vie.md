### Top Reviews
- **Nora Vale** (5/5): "A beautiful seaside restaurant located within a villa resort — perfect for a romantic dinner.  We ordered a large seafood platter with fish, prawns, mussels, squid and baby octopus, and everything was fresh and delicious.  The staff were very attentive, and the manager was especially helpful — answering all questions and making sure we had a great experience.  I visited in the evening, and after 8pm it gets quite busy, so I recommend booking in advance.  Loved everything — the food, the atmosphere and the service. Highly recommended."
- **Julie N** (5/5): "We had such a wonderful evening here. The food was a 10/10 – everything was absolutely delicious. The manager and the staff were incredibly kind, attentive, and made us feel very welcome throughout the whole evening. The location is also perfect for watching the sunset, which made the experience even more special. I would definitely recommend this place and would love to come back again!"
- **Matthew Groves** (5/5): "Went there this evening randomly and what a find. Right on the beach great atmosphere music good not too loud. Meet the owner moved us to a nicer table once one opened up. We had sangria & cocktails with 10 Japanese oysters 10/10 would recommend to anyone 😎"
