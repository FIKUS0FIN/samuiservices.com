### Top Reviews
- **Vanessa Bruce** (5/5): "I surprised my wonderful partner with a belated Valentine's romantic getaway. The resort is absolutely stunning, with lush tropical gardens, and we took lots of memorable photos during our short 5-minute walk to the beach. After waiting a few minutes, our beachfront romantic dinner was ready, and it was an incredible experience to watch the sunset. The team members were friendly, attentive, and even took lovely photos of us. The meal was delicious—a beautifully arranged 7-course set menu with many other options. The variety of food was tempting, and the wine paired perfectly with the dishes! The candles, flowers, and open pergola created a truly breathtaking setting. We loved every moment of this special evening, and it’s a memory we will cherish forever. Thank you again for such a wonderful experience!"
- **Sarah Reinhoudt** (5/5): "We stayed at Melati Resort for 4 nights with our 2 kids and we absolutely loved it. From the moment we arrived, it felt like a little tropical escape. The resort is set on a quiet stretch of beach that feels almost private, which made it incredibly peaceful and relaxing. The whole place is beautifully designed, surrounded by lush gardens and with a real sense of space and calm. We stayed in a spacious villa with a private pool which is perfect for families and such a luxury to have your own space to unwind. One of the highlights was the option for a private dinner on the beach, which felt really special. The spa was also amazing, we had one of the best massages there. What really stood out to us was how genuinely kind and warm everyone is, the staff made us feel so welcome throughout our stay. There’s a small but lovely kids club right by the pool, which made it easy to combine relaxing and keeping the kids entertained. The pool area at he beach is beautiful, with a swim-up bar, and we really enjoyed the different dinner themes in the evenings. If you feel like exploring, Choeng Mon Beach is just a short scooter or taxi ride away with some great beach restaurants. Right next to the resort there’s also a small local spot on the beach where you can enjoy simple Thai dishes, grab an ice cream, or even get a massage. Overall, a beautiful, luxurious and very family-friendly place, perfect if you’re looking for a peaceful stay with a bit of everything."
- **Florencia Macsad** (5/5): "What a wonderful experience from stepping out of the taxi until checking out. As a person who has traveled in different countries around the world I would highly recommend this hotel if you visiting Koh Samui. All staff are so helpful and nice. You feel more like a part of the family than just a guest with a room number. The breakfast were really good, they had something for everyone, they also changed some of the courses from day to day which was fun. The caramel sauce and bread and much more were homemade which gave that “little extra”. Excellent food, from Thai cuisine to English fish and chips. The rooms had everything you can ask for. Spacious environment, bathroom facilities, board games and wardrobes and cabinets. A lovely experience. A bit pricier than other hotels, but you don’t mind while you get to enjoy everything I wrote above. After a couple of days after checking out of this hotel we came back to enjoy a stunning dinner and the staff remembers us and was very welcoming. Thank you!"
