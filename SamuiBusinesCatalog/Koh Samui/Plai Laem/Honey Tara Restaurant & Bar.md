### Top Reviews
- **M. L. STARK** (5/5): "Absolutely and amazing and quite place to enjoy your dinner. Most vegetables are organic from their own garden. Huge portion of food, tasty and well prepared. Staff really friendly and the atmosphere is like a family."
- **Batty On Tour** (5/5): "Tara Bistro is a real little gem on Koh Samui. The atmosphere is relaxed and welcoming – the kind of place where you immediately feel like you’re exactly where you’re supposed to be.  The prawns were absolutely delicious: fresh, juicy, and full of flavor without any unnecessary fuss. You can really taste the quality. It’s simple food done exactly right – which, in my opinion, is much harder to find than fancy dishes. The rest of the menu also looked amazing, but honestly… after those prawns, it was hard to think about anything else!  The service was friendly and professional, with just the right balance between being attentive and giving you space to enjoy your evening. I had a great time and will definitely be coming back. If you’re looking for a place to enjoy great seafood in a relaxed setting, Tara Bistro should be high on your list.  And a little tip: order the prawns. Trust me – your future self will thank you!"
- **Timothy Murtland** (4/5): "Beautiful location in the hills. The grounds are quiet and peaceful. Great spot to have a lunch and cocktail."
