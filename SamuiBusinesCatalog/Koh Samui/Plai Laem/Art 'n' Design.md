### Top Reviews
- **Julia Kadłubska** (5/5): "I’ve had a lovely experience here! The lady that sews the dresses is really kind and helpful, she picked just the perfect dress for me that I would have missed. She helps with the colours, shape and size. She can also change the clothes a bit to fit you better! Definitely recommend. My dress was 950 THB which is not cheap, but I think it’s a fair price for an unique pair of clothing. PS: The dress on the picture is not the one I got, but it was really cute too so maybe it’ll your new dress :)"
- **Sappie** (5/5): "Lovely shop with incredibly kind owners. I came for the great selection of second-hand English books, and you can also donate your books here. The owner also creates beautiful, unique handmade clothes, accessories, and art — a truly talented lady! Definitely worth a visit."
- **Eleni Kypridis** (5/5): "This shop is a little haven to step into out of the heat. We had a lovely experience chatting with couple who own this shop. The very charming female owner is from generations of seamstresses and she makes beautiful clothing for the store. The owners offered us a refreshing herbal drink while we shopped. The inner sanctum of the store is a bookshop with many good English and German books so if you run out of reading material this is the place. The Couple both also produce artwork for the store. A great experience highly recommend!"
