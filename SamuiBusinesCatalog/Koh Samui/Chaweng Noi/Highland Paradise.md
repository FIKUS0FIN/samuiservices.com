### Top Reviews
- **Not Sure** (5/5): "It's an amazing place with friendly staff and the best view we've come across on the entire island. The food was very tasty and there is a lot of dishes to choose from. You can also veganize some dishes if you ask (e.g. replace meat with tofu). The price performance ratio is also very good. We ordered like half a dozen dishes so of course that adds up :-)"
- **Falina Stack** (5/5): "This is the best dinner we had on Koh Samui, hands down. The food was so so delicious and at typical local prices. But on top of that, the service was so attentive and the view was spectacular. There's also a giant swing onsite, chickens, parrots. So much to see! Treat yourself to the delight of dining here."
- **Nicolai Olesen** (5/5): "Wow, what a view. Food was amazing and so was the cocktails. The restaurant wasn’t over crowded but not empty either. Very good place if you want to eat and enjoy a beautiful view without it being over priced and over crowded."
