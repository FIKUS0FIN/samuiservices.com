### Top Reviews
- **Chelsea Polaschek** (5/5): "Just spent 9 nights at the Sarann, and had the best stay! We booked through a travel agent so had an all inclusive package. We stayed in a pool villa (102)  and it was spacious, had  2 separate rooms in one villa-one living room with tv, couch, bar fridge) and one huge bed/bath room- and a huge deck with bean bags, our private pool, and a table setting. It was so lovely and private. The staff were absolutely amazing the whole stay, so helpful! If you want an amazing Thai massage- the spa is the place to get it! I walked out feeling like I was floating! The restaurant is good! Although limited with choices, if you are on the inclusive packaging. I feel like they may dumb down some Thai food, I had to ask for salt a few times to get some flavour. I read through some reviews before I left and would love to clear some things up- Yes there are no footpaths- but you can walk along the road safely- literally the traffic will move out of the way for you, and the 7/11 is like 10 mins walk up the road- we never felt unsafe walking Yes it may seem like you are in the middle of nowhere- but they have an amazing shuttle for free , can get a ride back for 400 baht- after 4-5 hours out and about - that was worth it for us . Also the stray dogs- they are so freaking polite- they just crave some interaction- I wanted to bring them home. They won’t bother you unless you want them to . There is absolutely no way they enter the resort We had a beautiful stay at the sarann. I could not praise the staff enough, they were helpful, friendly and attentive"
