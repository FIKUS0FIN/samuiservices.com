### Top Reviews
- **Sadshadow0399 Shadow** (2/5): "We booked 2 rooms for our family stay from 31/5/2026 to 4/6/2026, but unfortunately we had to check out after just one night.  The photos shown on Google and booking platforms were very different from the actual condition of the rooms. The rooms appeared old, poorly maintained, and had a strong musty smell. The bathtub in the bathroom was dirty, and the wooden panel above the bathtub was covered with visible mold.  Most concerning, all of us woke up feeling extremely itchy after sleeping in the beds. We also noticed small insect-like debris and dirt on the bedding, which raised concerns about cleanliness and hygiene.  Due to these issues, we decided not to continue our stay and had to spend extra money to book accommodation elsewhere for the remaining nights.  We hope the management will take these concerns seriously and improve the cleanliness, maintenance, and overall condition of the rooms."
- **Eleanor Dore** (5/5): "We only stayed for one night here but immediately wished we had booked more when we arrived. We were a group of 6, and they put our rooms next door to each other (something hotels seem rarely to do when you book as a group!) which was so appreciated. The rooms were very modern and clean, with comfy beds, a safe, pool towels, and hairdryers provided. The pool and communal areas are lovey and well maintained. You definitely feel like you get a lot for your money and this hotel is very reasonably priced for the quality of service and room. We would definitely stay here again!  The only thing I would say is a slight downside is the location, if you do not ride a scooter then you are limited on where you would be able to get to without getting a grab or a baht bus. You can walk to Chaweng Noi beach, and there is a 7/11 and shops not too far away to walk to, however you have to walk alongside a busy dual carriageway with no path which feels very dangerous"
- **Keith McNamara** (4/5): "Not sure how this place is a 4star resort, but it is. The place looks tired and so does the staff. Not that we had an issue with our stay, just trying to give an honest opinion.  I will however say that if we had an issue, the staff were on to it immediately.  The location is only good if you want to lie around the pool or cross the road for the beach. There is not a great lot nearby.  The breakfast leaves a lot to be desired. Not a lot was actually hot, except the eggs that you order.  One more thing, which really isn't the hotels fault, but the smell of marijuana throughout the resort is horrendous."
