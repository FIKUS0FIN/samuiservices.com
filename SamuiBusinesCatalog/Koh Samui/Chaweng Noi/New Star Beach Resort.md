### Top Reviews
- **Мария Бурнашова** (5/5): "A hidden gem!  Would recommend for sure. The room was spacious with very high celling. And there was quite a creative bathroom with open shower.  The area is quiet, there weren't many guests when we stayed. There are a lot of greens, from lobby to beach bar goes a stream. The hotel has a good access to the beach.  As always I am pleasantly surprised with amazing staff, they know what they do and they do it well."
- **Gabija Mostvilaitė** (5/5): "if you want peace and rest just in the beach,this place is perfect for it! they have wonderful people working in the hotel,thank you all for such a hospitality! definitely worth to book place including breakfast,it was one of the best breakfast in all island so far. hotel located in peaceful area,far away from crowds,not so much things to do around,but 7/11 and pharmacy is just across the street.In general this hotel is  for couples and families,no party vibe,so just take your book and enjoy your holiday! looking forward to come back !p.s. i stayed in ocean view villa,highly recommended!"
- **Benny Kwong** (4/5): "The Room I have has high ceiling and large bathroom with extra outdoor shower.  The price was about US$350 per night.  Very big infinity pool by the beach front.  Breakfast restaurant is not very big and has few patio tables.  The food doesn't match up to the price I paid for the stay.  The USM airport lounge has much better food than here.  Services also need to be improved as their staffs were not well trained in the hospitality industry."
