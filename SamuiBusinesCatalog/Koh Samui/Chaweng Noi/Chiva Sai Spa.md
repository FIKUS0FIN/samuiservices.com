### Top Reviews

The massage itself felt more like gentle stroking. We ended up leaving after 25 minutes because of the noise. Disappointing."

They were very kind and sweet while getting our nails done (aside from her causing me to bleed on several fingers)… But afterwards we experienced terrible customer service. We had gel nails done on Friday, and by Sunday my nail had already started chipping off and the polish on my partners nails was bubbling up, probably due to not being cured properly. We went back in and politely showed them what was wrong and requested that they remedy the problem and they refused to give us any sort of compensation. We spoke with the manager Noy over the phone and she was very rude and ended up hanging up on us."
