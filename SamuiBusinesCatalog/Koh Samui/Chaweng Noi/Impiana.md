- **Tarek Sinjer** (3/5): "The beachfront location is beautiful and great for relaxing by the sea. The breakfast was amazing and definitely one of the highlights of our stay.  However, the rooms are not very luxurious and don’t really feel like a 4-star standard. We booked two rooms, and unfortunately one of them had a smelly bathroom. We politely asked to change the room, but were told the hotel was fully booked and it wasn’t possible.  The location is good for the beach, but not ideal for shops or getting around. Outside the hotel you’re directly on the road, the area isn’t walkable, and the nearest shops are about 1–2 km away, so a motorbike or transport is needed.  For the price we paid, we would probably choose a different place to stay next time."
