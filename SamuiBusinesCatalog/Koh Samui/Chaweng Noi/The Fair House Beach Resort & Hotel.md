### Top Reviews
- **Tomer Levi** (1/5): "Not recommended at all. For this price you can find much better and more luxurious hotels. The rooms are indeed large but old and a little smelly. - **Мария Бурнашова** (5/5): "Good location, picturesque scenery. Landscape designer really did a great job, such a pleasure to look at.  The beach is wonderful, clear water and soft sand. Staff is nice and friendly. I would like to mention an amazing desk manager Nunna - you made our stay so much better ☺️ (sorry if i remembered your name wrong)  Would recommend for family vacation, there are a lot of amenities for kids and a lot of place to rest for adults."
- **Sevket Sefer** (5/5): "Amazing staff. I thank all the staff that served us, not even one person bring the staff quality less than 5 star. Everything is very cool in this hotel, but bring ear plugs for the night. We stayed in a bungalow and if the birds decide to chirp at 5 in the morning, you will hear it as it is near your bed. Unfortunately bungalows don’t have the best sound isolation."
