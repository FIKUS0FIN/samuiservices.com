### Top Reviews
- **Hendricks** (5/5): "The breakfast options are quite varied ranging from rice and noodle dishes. You have the option to choose your style of eggs to customise to your preference. They also do English options like bakes beans, hash browns and sausages. The fruits are of course delicious, which include dragon fruit, water melon, papaya, pineapple etc.overall very satisfactory."
- **K.G. C-W** (3/5): "We didn't stay at the resort; we just stopped here for breakfast after reading great Google reviews. However, the place didn't live up to the hype. The sautéed tomato was just one small cherry tomato cut in half, and the apple pancakes had no apple in them. The side of spinach was overly creamy. Three of us ordered the Ur Breakfast dish, but it was confusing to understand what was included. It mainly consisted of eggs (any style) on some bread, with the rest being optional and needing to be ordered as an add-on. The poached eggs were served cold and on the hard-boiled side. The highlight of the meal was the fresh coconut. Our total bill for five people, including some drinks, was 2,118 Baht."
- **Riddhima Shamji** (4/5): "We had a little family reunion of 18 people at Culture! Siri, Pom, Daw, Tee, Fluke & Warn were so fantastic! Rooms are great, pool is wonderful. The food is okay. The hotel is located in a very central area which is great! I will say - the pool villas are beautiful but the bathroom is WIDE open! No privacy at all! Do consider when booking!"
