- **Josh Sampays** (5/5): "An absolutely fabulous day on the sail ship with Pko and crew. Very attentive staff. Boat not too crowded. The lunchtime meal was not only ample but very well cooked. The masaman curry was my favourite. As far as value for money goes I would highly recommend as you get a quality experience. Seeing the sail ship and experiencing the sails out was a truly wonderful time that my wife and I will always remember especially as it's our 40th wedding anniversary. Very impressed."
