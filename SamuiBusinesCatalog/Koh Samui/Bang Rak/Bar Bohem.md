### Top Reviews
- **PRISMA Consulting** (5/5): "Authentic and original place with real character. Carson was there and welcomed us with open arms; genuine hospitality at its best. His Negroni is outstanding, perfectly balanced and truly unforgettable. A must-visit spot in Koh Samui for cocktail lovers. Cheers 🍸"
- **Gal Toronto** (5/5): "A very cool bar that we happened to hear about through friends who live on the island — they took us there, and we’re so glad they did!  Honestly, I have no idea why this place doesn’t show up more in recommendations, because it’s definitely a must-visit if you enjoy cocktails.  We were two couples and ordered several cocktails — everyone loved everything we tried.  The atmosphere is fantastic, the bar is beautifully designed, and the lighting creates a really special vibe.  The staff were super professional, incredibly friendly, and made us feel genuinely welcome and comfortable throughout the evening.  Highly recommended — a great experience overall! 🍸✨"
- **Lita Sekarnphicha** (4/5): "A cozy little bar with a great approach to cocktails. They don’t rely on a fixed menu—just tell them what you like, whether it’s sweet, sour, fruity, smoky, or even with a bit of spice, and they’ll create something for you. I asked for something fruity, fresh, and not too sweet, bitter, or smoky.  What they made was spot on—a beautifully balanced cocktail with a subtle hint of ginger that elevated the whole drink. Absolutely delicious. I’d definitely go back again."
