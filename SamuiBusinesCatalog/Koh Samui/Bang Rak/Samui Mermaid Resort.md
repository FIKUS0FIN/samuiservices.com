### Top Reviews
- **Fabiana P** (3/5): "We recently stayed at Mermaid Resort in Koh Samui. The bed was comfortable and clean, the pool area was lovely, and the staff provided great service.  However, there were several issues that affected our stay. The room had a lingering odor, the chairs and small couch in front of the bed had visible stains, and the bathroom sinks were not very clean. There was no laundry service, and the beach towels provided still had some spots despite being washed. The resort’s layout gives the feeling of being surrounded, like a fish in an aquarium, so for privacy, you need to keep the blinds closed, which makes the room quite dark. The location is convenient for the ferry and the airport, but not much else.  We booked last minute during a change of plans and paid €100 for the junior sea view room. For what the resort offers, this felt too expensive. A rate around €50-60 per night would feel more reasonable.  Overall, it wasn’t a bad stay, and my boyfriend enjoyed it more than I did. While we appreciated the friendly staff and pleasant pool, we wouldn’t return due to the privacy issues, room cleanliness, and the price."
- **Laura Vajdíková** (1/5): "The hotel was generally okay, but unfortunately our stay was affected by several unpleasant issues.  On the first day, the bathroom drain was not working properly. We reported it at the reception, where we were told that it had been fixed and that our room would be changed the next day. However, when we went to the reception the following day, they refused to change our room unless we paid an extra fee. This seemed completely unreasonable, as the problem was caused by a technical issue, not by us requesting a room change without reason.  Even after the drain started to work, there was a strong unpleasant smell in the bathroom, making it almost impossible to breathe comfortably. The drain kept getting clogged repeatedly throughout our stay.  Another disappointment was that towels for the pool or the sea required an extra charge, which was not mentioned anywhere in advance.  The hotel staff was often very unfriendly and unhelpful. In many situations they were unwilling to accommodate us. Several times they refused to change our towels because they believed we had extra ones, which was not true.  In the evenings, we returned to our room multiple times to find it either not cleaned at all, or missing basic supplies such as toilet paper or fresh towels.  This negative experience was very disappointing and truly affected our overall impression of the hotel."
- **Matt** (5/5): "Great place to stay, staff are very friendly. Love the location, afternoon sunsets on the beach/pool or room. Restaurants has amazing food and drinks at good prices. Highly recommended for anyone, especially couples, chill out in resort. Beach club a couple of doors down if thats your thing, great tunes and Fire show. Or rent a bike or car and explore the island."
