### Top Reviews
- **Noam Akselbant** (5/5): "Great place to chill with cute pugs running about. The owners really care about the pugs, it feels like it’s her own dogs, and not for show/business at all.  Entry price is 100 per person but you get to stay as much as you like and they have really great drinks as well you can buy (you are not required to)  The dogs seem very happy and clean and chill, and always happy to have guests to pet them.  We’ve been here twice and I would be every day if I could."
- **Witek Ustijanowski** (5/5): "⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️ Amazing experience for dog lovers!  The Pug House Samui is such a lovely place! The dogs are incredibly friendly, well cared for, and clearly loved.
