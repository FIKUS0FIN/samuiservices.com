### Top Reviews
- **Kraemer Lovelace** (5/5): "We had a fantastic experience at MK Ultra Pottery doing a wheel throwing class as a family with kids ages 8–12. It was a great couple of hours learning the basics of throwing in a relaxed, welcoming environment. Morgan was an excellent instructor — patient, personable, and very encouraging with both adults and kids. She explained everything clearly, gave hands-on help when needed, and made the experience genuinely fun for everyone. Highly recommend this as a family activity or for anyone curious about trying pottery for the first time."
- **natalie heitman** (5/5): "We had the best time being creative as a family. We enjoyed focused and gentle, easy to follow, instruction on how to throw clay on a wheel. Perfect for visitors to Koh Samui and folks that are lucky enough to live here."
- **Tal Sharon** (5/5): "What a wonderful experience! My husband and I came for a practice wheel throwing class and experienced pottery for the very first time.
