### Top Reviews
- **Edward Mellor** (5/5): "Vibration is an absolute gem and easily one of the best music bars on Koh Samui.  The attention to detail in the décor and overall finish of the venue is outstanding. Every corner of the bar has been thoughtfully designed, creating a stylish and immersive atmosphere that feels both intimate and inspiring.  The drinks menu is extensive, with a great selection to suit all tastes, and the prices are incredibly fair for the quality on offer.  The live musicians were exceptionally talented, and the sound quality was excellent — crystal clear and perfectly balanced, allowing you to truly appreciate the performances.  What really makes Vibration special is the ambience. It strikes the perfect balance between chilled, welcoming, and vibrant. Whether you're there to relax with a cocktail or enjoy some top-quality live music, the atmosphere makes you feel instantly at home."
- **STORMSON Music** (5/5): "A beautiful place with crazy, boundless jam sessions and an atmosphere of love and freedom. The home of live music 🎶 on the island 🏝️of Koh Samui! ✨🤍✨"
- **Luke Pol-in** (5/5): "Vibration Bar is one of those places you don’t really plan too much… you just end up there and it turns into a solid night. It’s not trying to be better than everywhere else, it just has its own thing going on and people seem to feel that.  The open mic and jam nights are probably the best part. It’s not polished or overthought, people just jump in, play, sometimes a bit rough, sometimes seriously good, and somehow it all works. You get proper moments where the whole place just locks in with whoever’s playing. Feels real, not like a show being put on.  The vibe’s easy as well. You can sit back and just watch, or end up chatting with random people, or somehow get pulled into the music without even planning it. No pressure, no weird energy.  Drinks are decent across the board, but yeah the coconut rum stood out for me. Kind of like Malibu but not as flat, got a bit more depth to it, slightly dangerous because you don’t really notice how fast you’re drinking it.  Overall just a really nice balance. Fits in with the rest of the scene but gives you something a bit more relaxed, a bit more honest. I had a great time there, definitely one of those spots I’d go back to without thinking twice."
