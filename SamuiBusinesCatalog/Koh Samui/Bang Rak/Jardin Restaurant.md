- **Leo Rodriguez** (5/5): "We didn’t expect the restaurant to be this wonderful. For the main course, I had the lamb and my wife ordered the beef, both expertly prepared and cooked to perfection. We finished the evening with a lovely dessert that completed the meal perfectly. The food was truly outstanding, and you can really feel the passion and dedication of the chef in every dish. A special thanks as well to the chef’s wife and the staff for their warm hospitality and welcoming service throughout the evening. Overall, it was an incredible and memorable experience, and we would highly recommend this place to anyone looking for high level French cuisine. We will definitely make time to come back again."
