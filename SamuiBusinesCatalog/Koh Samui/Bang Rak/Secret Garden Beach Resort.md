### Top Reviews
- **Dana Frid** (3/5): "The hotel is located on a nice beach and is an easy access to the pier and airport. One thing I didn’t know about when we booked and just wanted to call out is that the bathrooms have some small openings to the outside that can’t be closed. It became a bit of a problem for me since lizards/bugs were coming in to the bathroom. The room itself was okay, so if you’re not afraid like me it would be worth the price."
- **Shirden** (5/5): "We had dinner. There was live entertainment and dancing. The all you can eat ribs were delicious and at a great price. The wagyu burger was delicious too. There’s a resort here with nice rooms. The atmosphere next to the beach was magical."
- **Charlie** (4/5): "We came here for dinner whilst staying in the area.  The service was good and the food was decent.  There was live music on during our visit which added to the nice vibe.  Would recommend visiting here."
