### Top Reviews
- **Tycho Klein Severt** (5/5): "Restaurant with great food and a nice view.  Booking the reservation through WhatsApp went smooth. When we arrived they gave us a nice table inside but soon moved us all the way to the front with a better view over the water. We didn’t asked for it but the proposed this themselves which we found attentive.  The staff is really alert and professional, they asked if we wanted to have a photo taken, were quick with refilling the wine and took the plates away at the perfect moment.  Food was quick and more importantly; delicious. The portions are not too big so I recommend ordering more than usual."
- **Ben Pearson** (5/5): "We had a stunning meal here, was my favourite in Koh samui. Highly recommend, amazing food and the view and atmosphere is lovely! Staff very attentive and polite too. Highly recommend!"
- **Erika Gudnason** (5/5): "We went to Nahm for dinner and would highly recommend arriving before sunset so you can sit by the water and enjoy the view.  The food was excellent, and the drinks were just as good. Overall, it was a really lovely experience. I went with one of my girlfriends for a night out, and we left having a great, memorable time.  Definitely a spot I’d recommend."
