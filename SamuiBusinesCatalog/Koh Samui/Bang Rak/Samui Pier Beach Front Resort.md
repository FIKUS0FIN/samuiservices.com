### Top Reviews
- **Ed Jones** (5/5): "Myself and 2 friends just stayed in 3 beachfront rooms at this resort. We found everything absolutely perfect - especially for the price you are paying.  Super clean rooms, right on the beach, excellent affordable and very budget friendly menu, plus excellent staff in the reception and restaurant.  We especially loved the facilities for the children and will be returning again with a friend’s family and children in a couple of weeks.  Excellent value for money, super clean and very budget friendly. Directly on the Beach ! Highly Recommended"
- **Bridie Blakeston** (3/5): "The hotel is nice. Good location. Clean, big rooms. On the beach. Clean pool. Sand pit for children.  Unfortunately for us (I know it's not the hotels fault and the staff try hard to clean it up) but the large amount of trash in the sea and on the beach is very off putting and sad to see.  Also, we paid an extra 500 baht per person for the ninja water park in the sea. I believe if you are a hotel guest the water park should be free to use or atleast come at a discounted price to everyone else.  We found the food and drink at the hotel restaurant very expensive. But there is a microwave, fridge and cutlery in the room to cook something yourself."
- **Richard Price** (1/5): "Staff are friendly and service around food and drinks is ok. Its a budget hotel  2 star as described by hotel on here its a 3 star. so not expecting too much. We arrived from flight early in morning. Was told room  will not be ready until 2pm will try to get it ready before. Ended up being ready at 2.30p  workmen drilling and sawing until 9.00pm when I went to complain at reception owner was talking to a builder and explained he did not know it was still going on! He did agree work would stop. Work started at 8am"
