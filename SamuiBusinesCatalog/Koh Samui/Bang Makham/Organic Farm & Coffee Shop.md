### Top Reviews
- **Oliver Kremers** (5/5): "Organic Farm & Coffee Shop in Koh Samui is a wonderful place to visit. Located in the hills, it offers a beautiful view overlooking the sea, creating a very relaxing atmosphere.  The menu has a great selection of Thai dishes, all fresh and very tasty. The service is also very friendly and welcoming.  A lovely spot to enjoy good food and one of the best views on the island. Highly recommended."
- **Netnapa Tanyongthong** (5/5): "The atmosphere is amazing, the food is delicious, and the prices are very reasonable. The staff are friendly and provide excellent service.  If you plan to visit, I highly recommend coming by car. It is not advisable to ride a motorcycle because the road is quite steep. Going uphill is fine, but going downhill can be very dangerous.  Only very experienced riders with good riding skills and high-quality motorcycles might manage safely. However, for safety reasons, it is best to avoid using a motorcycle altogether."
- **Shirden** (5/5): "The food is fresh and delicious. I know I’m eating healthy when I eat here. The views are amazing and the atmosphere is wonderful. The air is clean up there and I always enjoy the respite from the smoke filled streets of Samui. They have a variety of dishes, desserts, and drinks to choose from. They have places to relax and lay back while taking in the surrounding countryside and spectacular views"
