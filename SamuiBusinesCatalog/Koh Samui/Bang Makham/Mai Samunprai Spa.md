- **THXX KAEW** (2/5): "The hotel is located quite far from town and feels a bit isolated, so having your own car or transportation is necessary. I had to approach and inform them that I was checking in, and they seemed unsure about the process. I had to wait until another staff member came to assist.  The hotel itself is very beautiful, with a lovely landscape. The room is nicely decorated with a good layout, but it feels a bit outdated and could benefit from renovation in the future.
