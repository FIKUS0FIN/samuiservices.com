### Top Reviews
- **Antonio Graho** (5/5): "We had an absolutely wonderful stay at the Humble Villas in Koh Samui! We would especially like to highlight the incredibly friendly and attentive staff – both at the reception and during breakfast service. From the very first moment, you feel welcomed and well taken care of.  The rooms were very clean and well maintained. The small private pool was a real highlight – perfect for cooling off and simply relaxing.  The breakfast was also delicious and prepared with great care. Another big plus is the scooter rental service as well as the reliable taxi service, which makes exploring the island very convenient.  All in all, a clear recommendation – we would definitely come back anytime!"
- **Joel Gros** (4/5): "Everything was perfect expect the noise coming from the nearby road.  The people and service were  perfect. The room is perfect. The breakfast is perfect. It is very easy  to have a scooter delivered there.  But… the upper rooms are very noisy, we were in room 3 and we could hear the cars and scooters passing. I had to sleep with eat plugs all the time.  So my advice ask for any room but not rooms 1,2,3 and 4, as they are noisy."
- **Cuisine Quest** (5/5): "I cannot stress enough how utterly awesome this place is. Each bungalow has a private plunge pool, a sunset view, and is right on the beach for quick access. The breakfast is absolutely spectacular, your choice of everything from Thai to eggs Benedict. The beds are very soft and the rooms have air conditioning. Will definitely come back here again. Btw if you enjoy good cooking and travel videos, please subscribe to cuisinequest on YouTube, no regrets!"
