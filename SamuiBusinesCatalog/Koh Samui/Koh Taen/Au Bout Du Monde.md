### Top Reviews
- **Boris Lauser** (5/5): "Truly magical. The entire experience is one of the best things I have done on Koh Samui and I have lived here now for many years. Erik and his wife are just the born hosts. But not only do they know how to make you feel comfortable, they also know their craft. The food looks and tastes absolutely delicious and Erik's cocktails are truly a revelation. I was scared to get a juicy drink when I saw the amount of liquid. But it was an incredibly well balanced and very intense tasting cocktail and it looks fabulous on top. So much love for details. I cant wait to be back and bring more friends."
- **Maud van Aerts** (5/5): "My boyfriend and I went here in March and we absolutely loved it! The baths on the beach were amazing, as wel as the cocktails and the service. I would recommend everyone who is visiting Koh Samui to go here."
- **Joana Bataityte** (5/5): "That experience was one of a kind, everything from start to finish. Gladys, Eric and their team truly knows how to host and make this time special. The food and wine pairing was fantastic. Cocktails were so strong, that everyone was laughing at the end. Also they went beyond when finding and returning my lost glasses on the island, which I am very grateful for. Thank you and hope to see you one more time"
