### Top Reviews
- **Cukrfree** (5/5): "We visited Wanngram five times during our stay on Koh Samui and completely fell in love with this place. It’s truly something different from most restaurants on the island. The food is exceptional their duck with lychee sauce, caesar salad and the baked shellfish are absolutely phenomenal. Honestly, everything we tried was delicious and beautifully prepared.  On top of that, the service is very warm and friendly, which makes the whole experience even better. We will definitely come back next time we are on Koh Samui.♥️"
- **E Czar** (5/5): "We had heard great things about Wanngram and were not disappointed! To begin, the location was easily reached with parking readily available. The atmosphere was relaxed, quiet and elevated casual.  Each dish ordered was divine. We ordered the lobster gnocchi, kingfish, steak and fish. Each dish had their own vibrate and deep individual flavours which were thoroughly enjoyable and unique. This excellence was replicated in the service which was consistent, timely, and attentive throughout. The price, although on the higher end, was very much reflective of the quality of ingredients and overall dishes.  By far one of the best restaurants we have been to in Koh Samui!"
- **Pete Thompson** (5/5): "A fine dining experience in a remarkable room.  Filled with object d art and vintage furniture, this place is laid-back cool.  Wanngram has an excellent wine list and very attentive staff.  Food presents as a mix of modern Thai and classic European. Wonderfully presented and delicious.  The lobster gnocchi and the seared scallops with Japanese sweet potato are outstanding.  Worth dressing up for!"
