### Top Reviews
- **Olga Martí García** (4/5): "Solid gym for 140 THB per pass! Honestly, it’s not bad at all for that price on the island where everything is overpriced. you have everything you need for a full workout. It would be nice to see a few more kettlebells and dumbbells added to the functional area, but overall, it’s excellent value for money. Definitely a good spot if you’re looking for a budget-friendly session!"
- **medium_nice** (5/5): "One of the best local gyms you’ll find! OK Fitness Samui is a hidden gem for anyone who loves a no-nonsense, old-school gym vibe. No aircon – and that’s actually a big plus. You sweat it out properly, just the way it should be in Thailand. It’s raw, honest training in a tropical setting. The equipment covers all the basics, and everything you need for a solid workout is there. Best part? Only 120 baht per day – unbeatable value for money. Highly recommend for anyone looking to stay fit without the frills."
- **Curtis James Fountain** (5/5): "Decent gym with decent equipment! Has everything you could possibly need for a good workout.
