### Top Reviews
- **Htoo Lay** (5/5): "This French restaurant offers a truly unique dining experience right by the beach. Visiting during sunset is absolutely magical—watching the sun dip into the ocean while enjoying your meal creates a romantic and unforgettable atmosphere. 🌅✨ The interior reflects a classic French elegance, while the outdoor seating is the real highlight, allowing you to enjoy the cool ocean breeze and stunning sea views. The food is exceptional. Each dish is beautifully presented, with rich and well-balanced flavors that capture the essence of authentic French cuisine. Service is equally impressive, with friendly and attentive staff who make the entire experience even more enjoyable. Overall, this is the perfect spot for a date night, anniversary, or any special occasion. Great food, breathtaking sunset views, and a relaxing beach atmosphere—all in one place. Definitely a hidden gem worth visiting."
- **Daria Zhuravleva** (5/5): "We were craving something different from Thai food and decided to visit this place for dinner. Samui food scene keeps impressing me! Even the restaurant is not at a central location, it has a lot of customers, which is always a good sign. The menu is diverse with choices of meet, fish and pizzas.  I was absolutely impressed by truffle Camembert. I also tried Thai style tartar, which was also interesting twist on a traditional tartar dish, and the only downside is my husband ordered a steak to be medium but it came “well done”, he nevertheless thoroughly enjoyed it, especially with the mushroom sauce that was with it. You can see that people here like what they do and pay great attention to everything from service to the atmosphere. Great place to visit for a couple or family dinner."
