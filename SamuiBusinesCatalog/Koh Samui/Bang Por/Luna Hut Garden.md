### Top Reviews
- **Trine Nielsen** (4/5): "We really liked this place we went there 4 times in the 5 days we were there. Great for a lunch place. Can recommend the pad Thai! The last night we ordered the lobster (order one day in advance). We got really good service and got too see them fresh early in the day. The meal was amazing and the vibe really nice plus super service. A definitely recommendation."
- **Sara Gomboc** (5/5): "Got here randomly when bypassing and we were pleasantly surprised. The staff and the owner is very kind. Its a traditional thai restaurant that never disappoints. We had 2 drinks, 2 cocktails, 1 appetizer, 2 main dishes and 2 desserts and the receipt was only 820baht! The portion itself may be a bit small but that is a good thing so you can try many different things in one go. Every single dish and cocktail was delicious. Recommend!"
- **Nathanael Tan** (5/5): "Base on Google reviews decided to visit since it was a short drive from my hotel and Google was not wrong!
