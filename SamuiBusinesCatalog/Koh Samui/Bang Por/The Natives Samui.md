### Top Reviews
- **Alan Bond** (4/5): "Traditional villas set right next to the sea. Very spacious and high quality furnishings. Some minor points can be observed such as visible service areas around the grounds and a few screws and bolts could do with tightening up around the villa but over all an exceptional home from home in traditional Thai style. It’s like Samui probably was 30 years ago but with air conditioning, toilet roll, WiFi and Netflix. If you’re looking for a comfortable, spacious and private accommodation away from the busy areas then this is highly recommended."
- **Richard Král** (5/5): "Just a perfect and lovely place with full privacy. Thai style villa with own garden and entry to the sea plus shared pool (renovated). Amazing authentic breakfast. On top of that, they do their own sourdough bread. Nice and lovely people, fully recommended for couples as well as for the families. Thank you."
- **Nathan Johnson** (5/5): "So this place had a great view and location for a great price. The breakfast was amazing so I'd recommend opting for it. If you can, get the beach villa if you're like me and want to have a deck looking at the ocean and within walking distance. Almost perfect, but you should know it's not the best for swimming as there are rocks/(coral?) close to the beach, but it's doable for kids and adults, just can't go too far out. For a softer beach experience, you only need to walk about 50 meters to the left facing the ocean. I found a great "surf shack" vibe restaurant if you hang a right at the beach selling four basic Thai dishes, one cook out of one pan. If you want a more formal sit down hang a left and it's no too far (though I didn't love it there). You are remote keep in mind and I found taxis not the easiest to get. I think this would be ideal for a  honeymoon or someone that just wants to stare into the ocean and relax."
