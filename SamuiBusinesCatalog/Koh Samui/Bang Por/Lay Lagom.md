### Top Reviews
- **Shouvik Mukherjee** (5/5): "Wonderful beachside cafe in koh samui. Really loved the vibe & ambience. Went there to have breakfast, pancakes were amazing. Staffs were very friendly. A perfect place to spend quality time with family/friends/alone. Also ideal for remote working. Must visit😋❤️"
- **Doenpen Surapong** (4/5): "Really nice location and atmosphere. The café is pet-friendly and even provided a water bowl for our dog, which was a nice touch. However, there are three big dogs at the restaurant, so we had to keep an eye on our surroundings.  The coffee is good, and the pasta was really delicious. I finished the whole plate. The chicken cashew nut was a bit unusual though, as it mostly tasted like tomato sauce."
- **Cathy Nelson** (5/5): "Nothing beats the feeling of having meal overseeing the ocean, listening to the sea breeze....and accompanied by chillax acoustic songs.  Everything I had here was good. The staff are very nice and hospitable.  I came here after reading positive reviews and it didn't disappoint.  Come here if you are really hungry and want to unwind at the same time."
