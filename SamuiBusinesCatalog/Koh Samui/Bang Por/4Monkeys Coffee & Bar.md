- **andrew owen** (5/5): "We came here twice in April 2026…the first night of our holiday and the last…we wished it had been more.  An absolutely wonderful welcoming bar with incredibly relaxed beach vibes. The owner Nong is a lovely lady who cannot do enough for you…and she is an expert cocktail maker. Literally we had some of the best cocktails during our stay on the island.  4 Monkeys had an extensive cocktail list and my wife asked for one that was not on it. Nong checked but one of the ingredients  of the cocktail was not stocked (this was our fault for asking for something not listed!) however she got one of the team to check with a shop over the road for the ingredient..this way above and beyond what we expected!  The positioning the restaurant is incredible occupying an enviousn position overlooking the beach and sea…if you can try to get there for sunset and watch the fishing boat lights sparkle across the water as the waves lap against the sand…you cannot put a price on this!  Coming from the UK I cannot tell you how much we felt welcomed by Nong and her lovely staff..and if you appreciate a well crafted cocktail do not miss this place!"
