### Top Reviews
- **Oliver Kremers** (4/5): "Sassy Beach presents itself as a wine bar and upscale restaurant. The location right by the beach is beautiful and the atmosphere is pleasant.  The service is very attentive and friendly. The menu focuses heavily on meat and steak dishes, which are well prepared and enjoyable.  Prices are noticeably higher compared to many restaurants in the surrounding area, but the quality is overall solid. A nice spot if you’re looking for a beachside dinner with good service."
- **Stanislav Markov** (5/5): "My wife and I celebrated our wedding anniversary here! It was a fantastic evening with a great beachfront location. The food was superb—we had the steaks, tartare, and appetizers. By the way, they have a huge wine selection, which was our main criteria when choosing a restaurant for the occasion. There was a musician playing live music in the evening, which made for a great atmosphere. Huge thanks to the staff for the excellent service; everything was top-notch!"
- **David Moore (heynowdave47)** (5/5): "We had an amazing dining experience at Sassy Beach. From the moment we arrived until our departure everything was top notch. First thing to note is the comfort of the chairs. We were a group of 5 and we started with 2 appetizers (appertivos) and a very nice, reasonably priced, bottle of wine. 4 of us got pasta dishes and we all were very impressed with the quality, taste and portion size. Desserts of creme brulee and Panettone with gelato were the perfect end to a perfect meal. If you're looking for a restaurant for a special occasion or just a high end dining experience, this is the place!"
