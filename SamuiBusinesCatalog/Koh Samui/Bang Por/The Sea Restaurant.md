### Top Reviews
- **Derek** (5/5): "Three years ago my wife and I tried this amazing Thai restaurant....and three years lately I returned with family to enjoy what I would say is the best Thai restaurant in Koh Samui.  The owner Chaw is the main chef at the restaurant, the food is truly a delicious experience and the presentation of all the different dishes is so elegant.  It's so good we ate there twice in the 4 day trip.  The restaurant has a pretty seating area open air under the leaning bamboo trees which is so nice to eat in there.  Top picks are the Barracuda dish is expertly cooked and juicy, the king prawn dish has huge prawns and so tasty.  The sea bass with tamarind sauce is amazing, massaman curry is very rich and full of favor.  Out if the 10 or 12 dishes we tried, we would order them all again as each one is so unique and delicious.  Thank-you Chef Chaw for such an amazing dining experience.  You deserve a Michelin Star for the quality of food you make!  Amazing!"
- **Malikeh Salimi** (5/5): "One of the best restaurants I’ve been to. I ordered the green curry, and it was amazing. I’m not usually a fan of spicy food, but I absolutely loved it. The flavor was rich, balanced, and mildly spicy. The staff were so nice and friendly. Highly recommended."
- **Alyssa Victoria** (5/5): "Amazing food and the owner is so kind and friendly! We came here twice for dinner during our 4 night stay. If you have a bigger group, I would recommend making a reservation just to be safe!"
