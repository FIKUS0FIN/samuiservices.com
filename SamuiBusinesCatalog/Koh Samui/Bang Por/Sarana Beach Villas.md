### Top Reviews
- **Brad Weir** (5/5): "Simply amazing, right on the beach with minimal people. Ours to enjoy mostly on our own. Our only mistake was that we should have stayed longer. The villas were modern and new and very comfortable. The private plunge pool was a welcome relief returning at the end of the day. These villas are close to all amenities that we required including walking distance to 7 Eleven and fruit stores. Laundry service across the road and some nice restaurants nearby. Could not rate these properties highly enough and definitely plan to return."
- **Sharna and Katie** (5/5): "We stayed here in December 2023 and loved it. The location, decor, and facilities are amazing and for us this felt like a luxury stay. Easy check in and check out and very clean.  The view from the bedroom on the mezzanine floor in a morning was stunning. It is in a quieter area than some of the more tourist spots - plenty of beach restaurants and bars but if you want to be visit a busier area you can easily order a taxi.  We would definetely recommend and look forward to staying again."
- **Carol Horton** (5/5): "We were the fortunate folk to be the first ever “residents” at Sarana Beach Villa. The decor, the ON beach location,private pool and Deb and Ricky’s warm welcome made a very memorable holiday for us. Great variety of restaurants within easy walking distance, plus a pharmacy and a 7-Eleven. Khun Yips little restaurant directly across the road is superb - so cheap and delicious, plus she happily delivers your meal. The nearby laundry service shop added to the genuine Thai experience !"
