### Top Reviews
- **Beatrice** (5/5): "Jahn restaurant is simply divine and easily one of the best on the entire island. It is a grill restaurant, and while we mainly had fish and seafood, it was fantastic and prepared to an incredible standard. There is also a great choice of wines to accompany the meal. We almost want to keep it a secret from people outside the resort so it doesn't get too crowded, but absolutely hats off to all the chefs; the food is incredible and the experience was flawless. During my birthday, we had the most delicious chocolate mousse cake there which was the perfect end to the evening. It truly is a culinary masterpiece."
- **Dave Eri** (4/5): "Jahn might have one of best locations on the island. It has one of the most incredible views which sets the scene for a great dinner. The space itself is very intimate. There are only a couple of tables and booths in the restaurant. The design is very relaxing and smooth. The wagyu itself was actually underwhelming and I didn't enjoy it as much as I thought I would. The entrees and sides were fantastic though. The service was impeccable and I am annoyed I didnt remember our hostesses names as they did an amazing job."
- **Keaton Wlaz** (5/5): "Our dining experience was excellent from start to finish. Our server, Ming, and the chef, Fon, were outstanding. We chose the tasting menu with wine pairings, and every course was incredible. The food paired beautifully with the wines.  The staff truly personalized the experience and made us feel very well taken care of as hotel guests. The ambiance was unforgettable: being so high up and watching the sunset behind the five islands was absolutely stunning.  Highly recommend this restaurant for a special dining experience."
