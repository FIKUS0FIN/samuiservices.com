### Top Reviews
- **Silvia Mariani** (5/5): "Bungalows are small and basic, but it's all about the location: right on the beach, very peaceful. We had a wonderful stay, if you really want to appreciate it get a moped (owner is more than happy to arrange one for you). Plus points for the lovely black cat that sits by the till!"
- **Kiki Dee** (5/5): "We very much enjoyed our meal here. Great breeze on the beach with a beautiful view. Good food with a full page of vegetarian options available, and crispy bacon.  Excellent service.  We look forward to coming back."
- **Rob S** (5/5): "I don't know how I have missed going to this gem location before. Located on the south west coast of Samui, it's a stunning location for watching sunsets with wonderful views out to the nearby islands. At the entrance there's a wooden bridge that spans across a lake with colourful lotus flowers. The restaurant serves amazing food and cocktails to sip while watching sunset. The restaurant also has beach bungalows for hire. Certainly a highlight of this trip"
