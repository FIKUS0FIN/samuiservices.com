- **Елена Макарова** (5/5): "I’m definitely not a fisherman, but I really liked this place. My boyfriend and I came here for fishing. We were given a gazebo, and a Thai staff member sat with us and helped put bait on the fishing rods because we didn’t know how to do it ourselves. He also helped us remove the fish from the hook in the safest way possible, without harming the fish.  The lake is quite large, with more than 200 fish and over 20 different species. We managed to catch two types of fish — in total, we caught six huge fish. When you catch a fish, you’re allowed to go down and gently touch each one. It reminded me of a kind of interactive fish park.  I definitely recommend visiting this place if you have the chance.  There were a few downsides, though. Check-in took quite a long time — the staff needed about 15 minutes to find the correct key for our room. Also, in the morning they didn’t tell us that breakfast was available. When we came later, it turned out that breakfast had already ended. However, they made an exception for us and served breakfast anyway, which we really appreciated — thank you so much for that.  On the territory, there are also activities like darts, pool, billiards, and a café."
- **Glenn Nowosad** (5/5): "The accommodations and fishing experience were outstanding. We fished for an afternoon and morning of the next day catching 20+ carp and catfish species. The local fishing guides setup the rods and baiting for each fishing hut or accommodation hut located on the water front. The fishing time period was from 7:00 am to 6:00 pm. I would definitely return for the fishing experience if we ever return to Ko Samui in our future travels."
