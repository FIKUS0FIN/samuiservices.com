### Top Reviews
- **emma rose** (5/5): "We didn’t stay here, but visited for the day.  The beach is well maintained, soft sand, shallow waters and peaceful.  The bar serve delicious food & mouth watering fresh shakes.  It’s a little off the beaten track but definitely worth a visit.  And if your into caravan’s and tents, then you’ll be in paradise."
- **Patcharapa P (Autobot)** (5/5): "Love my stay This place is like you’re not in typical touristic Samui. It’s calm and relax. I have my weekend feels like vacation. The beach is not best to swim. We enjoyed cooking our seafood bbq for dinner with the ingredients and equipment provided by the place. They have mini projector with chill out vibe music. Bar has craft beer and easy drink( no cocktail). The staff are nice and welcoming. Really nice experience."
- **Nathan Jennings** (5/5): "Beautiful location, vibe and family! We spent 5 nights in our own tent, not far from the water!  Lovely Drinks (including craft beers) and food available whilst on the most quiet and relaxing environment you could imagine.  Smoking shops and restaurants a few minutes along to beach.  If you’re looking to get away from it all and enjoy the sound of silence, visit this beautifully maintained camp!"
