### Top Reviews
- **Andrzej B** (5/5): "Moorea Boutique is a lovely place to spend your time in Koh Samui. A little bit outside of main island, it’s perfect distance to get anywhere with a rented scooter.  The hotel itself is great, with a small but relaxing pool, attentive staff and a friendly owner, who is there to suggest interesting sights to see. What is more you can see that they are expanding of the campus, with new buildings being prepared to accomodate more facilities, I can only see the resort to be better with time.  The breakfast are huge and tasty, although if I were to give one small con - a bit on the pricier side. Nothing you cannot try once in a while.  All in all, great place to stay in order to cruise around Koh Samui!"
- **Gilly & Noga Nativ** (5/5): "Everything about this small boutique hotel is wonderful. The grounds are gorgeous, with lights in the trees that make the whole place feel almost enchanted at night. The pool is beautiful, and the rooms are peaceful and comfortable, with good AC, cozy bedding, and excellent water pressure. Fresh towels (including beach towels) and drinking water are provided daily. The restaurant is beautifully decorated, serving a good mix of Thai and Western food, and often features live music. The service throughout the hotel is impeccable, everyone is friendly, attentive, and speaks good English. It’s also just a short walk to a quiet, pristine beach with several restaurants and massage spots nearby. The only downside is occasional power outages in this part of the island, which can mean a few hours without AC or running water since the system runs on a pump. It happened a few times during our nine-night stay, though the candle-lit restaurant makes it surprisingly romantic while you wait."
- **Brian Melton** (5/5): "Wow wow wow!  We attended a wedding at this special gem, and it was one of the most special experiences I’ve had.  I highly recommend this property for private events, entertainment , and chef inspired cuisine.  The property is family owned and operated, and they’re incredibly friendly, engaging, and attractive.  The food was a big highlight.  Easy walk to the beach, massage/nail places, and cute beach bars.  Very clean, inspired decor, and attentive service.  I can’t wait to go back."
