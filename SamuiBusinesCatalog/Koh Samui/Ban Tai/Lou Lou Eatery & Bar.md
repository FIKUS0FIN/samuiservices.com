### Top Reviews
- **Dtown** (5/5): "Fantastic restaurant. I ate here multiple times and was never disappointed. Both the Thai and non-Thai dishes were very good. The cocktails were always very well made (and strong!) The service and staff are excellent, very attentive and helpful. I would highly recommend."
- **Jim Anthony** (5/5): "Lovely new restaurant on the way to BanTai beach.  Just opened a few days ago in the soft-opening stage, and I have been 3 times.  An excellent caesar and chopped salad,  chicken green curry and spring rolls.   Once they get up and running with a full menu and staff, I suspect this will be packed and difficult to get a seat. The food quality and taste was excellent with generous portion , the staff very caring , and the relaxing atmosphere felt very high quality for this neighborhood.  Definitely keep an eye on Lou Lou's as a great new spot.  The Boutique resort is looking fantastic as well.  You can tell the pride of new ownership here !"
- **The Misses** (5/5): "Sad we did not have the time to enjoy it more! What a lovely place, all the staff is nice, the food is delicious, we ate dinner 3 nights in a row and also breakfast. Great burger, the strips and the chopped salad, lovely mix! Good cocktails, Good cappuccino, we loved it. The music, the vibe, everything! Thank you!"
