### Top Reviews
- **April VanKirk** (5/5): "The servers are super friendly. The drinks are exquisite coming equipped with a golden spoon. We also have a stir rod. The bread was super fresh; it was cash money. The sandwiches were so yummy. There is even a garnish on the side of the sandwich. There is even a wonderful experience with a cute small cat. You can play with the kitchen while waiting for your food… if she chooses you."
- **Sady S** (5/5): "We made this our regular spot on the island, and it never disappointed. Everything we tried—from fresh smoothies and coffees to a variety of Thai dishes—was incredibly flavorful, beautifully presented, and the price-to-quality ratio is unbeatable. The small team working there is also very warm and welcoming.  The restaurant is located on a busy main road, so it can get a bit noisy at times, but it didn’t bother us in the slightest. Highly recommend this spot for anyone looking for a delicious, casual meal"
- **Keren Lipiec** (5/5): "Perfect Thai place with amazing food and smoothies! The family who owns the place are very welcoming and friendly, they cook very well and are very attentive to clients needs. The place is very cute. A bit noisy because it’s on the road but it’s common in the area."
