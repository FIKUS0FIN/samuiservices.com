- **Kathrin Grabher** (5/5): "Cape Away Beach Bar & Resort quickly became our favorite place. We ended up being there almost every day because it simply felt right. We stayed at the resort for five nights and were honestly overwhelmed by how kind and attentive the staff were. Every single person was warm, genuine, and incredibly caring. Some days we had freshly cooked food brought to the pool, other times cocktails were delivered straight to the beach. Nothing ever felt like too much trouble.  It was one of those holidays that stays with you. We didn’t want to leave and already know that we’ll come back. Cape Away feels special, and we’re very grateful for the time we spent there."
