### Top Reviews
- **Zaphy Christoforou** (5/5): "I came here on 3 different occasions, each trying a new pasta dish and I was soo happy with the food, 10/10, just how I expected and more!! The service is excellent and you are always greeted with smiling faces and good vibes. There is a lot to choose from and lovely desserts - you’re spoilt for choice but I recommend the pesto pasta or the seafood spaghetti!! I also loved the ham & mushroom tagliatelle - if you’re in fisherman village then I 1000% recommend!"
- **M Short** (5/5): "After having tried different Western restaurants on the island we were absolutely blown away by the quality of the food here. We had pizza (probably the best one on the island), lasagna and a salad and were more than happy with everything. Paul the owner is a really nice guy who talked to us for a while at the end and had lots of interesting stories to tell. We highly recommend this place to anyone! :)"
- **Sabai Glamping** (5/5): "Probably one the the best restaurants in the fisherman’s village ; avd for sure the cleanest ! All best ingredients are selected to provide an excellent food . Wooden oven pizzas are so good , Caesar salad , amazing ( not easy to find a good one in samui ) . Very nice wines too , and all at very decent prices . Will go again"
