### Top Reviews
- **Chawqi Dridi** (5/5): "Amazing fresh and healthy food! I honestly didn’t know what (not) to take while looking at the menu so tried different things. Had quite the surprise when saw it all arrive as the portions are very generous! It all went down very well though as the quality of the food is amazing and the products super fresh. Also, very decent prices! And the ambiance and location (fishermen village) is amazing. Wholeheartedly recommend!"
- **Maverick** (4/5): "We tried two bowls at Beach Coconut Bowls, which were really tasty and fresh. The two avocado toasts were also good and nicely prepared. The atmosphere has some room for improvement, as it feels a bit uninspired and could be more inviting. The service was okay, the staff was generally friendly, but you have to place your order yourself and only the food is brought to the table. There were no further check-ins or follow-up questions. Overall, great food with some potential to improve the ambiance and service."
- **irma alam** (5/5): "It’s a little hot in the upper floor and the drinks are underwhelming. The bowls were scrumptious. The waiter was very friendly and attentive. I loved that he adjusted the portable AC for my son. Love the hospitality of Thai people!"
