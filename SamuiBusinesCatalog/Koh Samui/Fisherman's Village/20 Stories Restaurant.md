### Top Reviews
- **Dtown** (3/5): "Beautiful location right on the beach front. I ordered a mojito which wasn't the best, there was hardly any sugar and not enough lime. I also ordered seafood with rice, the seafood was well overcooked and the squid was rubbery and really chewy. There are lots of wait staff who were very friendly, and service was very fast."
- **Luisa Behrens** (5/5): "We visited this restaurant twice during our stay and absolutely loved it. The tuna steak was fantastic — fresh, perfectly cooked, and honestly one of the best meals we had. What made it even more special was that it wasn’t even on the menu, but they still kindly prepared it for us when we asked. And for such great quality, the price was really excellent. That’s exactly why we came back a second time. Highly recommended!"
- **Steve Hampson** (4/5): "Stopped in for a bit to eat. I had the Ribs and the wife had Chi Ken cashew nut. Whilst the staff were exceptional the food was a little bland.. Maybe just an off day but wouldn't make it a priority to return. Sorry guys."
