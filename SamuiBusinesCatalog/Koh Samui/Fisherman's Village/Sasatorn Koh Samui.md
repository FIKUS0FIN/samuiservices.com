### Top Reviews
- **Rebecca Smith** (5/5): "This Cafe was recommended by our host for breakfast and we ate here most days. The coffee was excellent, we usually had an iced cappuccino. Every juice we tried was delicious and the meals were so full of flavour. There is a great choice of Thai foods with a bit of a Melbourne twist. The kids menu had lots of fun options, each kids drink came with marshmallows. The service was friendly and professional. The water and ice are filtered so you can drink without worry."
- **Marie Laurence Demeyer** (5/5): "Amazing coffee places with an extraordinary large menu, full of never-seen-before drinks and dishes. As i took about 20 minutes upon deciding what i wanted, i was in a bit of a rush to grab breakfast before an appointment nearby, but they were very friendly and made my breakfast super quick (without judging me for taking so long to order 😆). I had a Melbourne style sourdough toast with a folded chili omelet, salmon, fermented cauliflower and a goatcheese espuma and it's truly one of the best sourdough toast i've ever had! The coffee, as stated in lots of reviews, is fabulous indeed."
- **Lee Markham** (5/5): "Well they have every type of coffee you can imagine and it's bloody good!!!  Food - lovely, just a really. Nice relaxed place. Worth going to if you love coffee"
