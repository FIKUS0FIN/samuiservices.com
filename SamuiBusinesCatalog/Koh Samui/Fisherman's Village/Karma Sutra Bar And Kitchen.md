### Top Reviews
- **Virender Singh** (3/5): "I loved the vibe, music and atmosphere of this place. Cocktails were outstanding. I wish I had the capacity to try more of what they had to offer. But, the service was horrible. The lady on the top floor was eyeing my wife's phone since she was checking for vegan options nearby. This place didn't have good vegetarian/vegan options. We still waited for 20 mins for someone to come and take our order but no one did. By the time we walked downstairs, our bill was already ready. Sorry but that one lady kind of killed the vibe of the place for me."
- **Marie-Eve Parent** (1/5): "I ordered a glass of red Côtes du Rhône wine and the bottle clearly turned bad or was oxydized. The wine tasted like burned caramel and the color was brownish similar to à bad Porto. I told the waitress and she argued the wine was good. However she changed the glass but it was the same disgusting taste, really not normal for this type of wine and all the staff were just staring at me and I felt so unconfortable that I left. I do not recommand this place."
- **Collin Kinnear** (5/5): "Great food, great service. Live music every night. The perfect place to take in great cocktails, great view, and watch all the people walk by. Situated on the busiest corner of Fisherman's Village, with it's sister bistro across the street, it is the perfect location. All of the staff are friendly and attentive. The food is excellent, as are the cocktails. The beer, wine, and liquor selection are top notch. Not the lowest prices around, but not the priciest, either. It is the perfect range to be surrounded by a nice class of diners and drinkers."
