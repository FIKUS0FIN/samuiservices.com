### Top Reviews
- **JuMK** (2/5): "I had a disappointing experience at Link in Koh Samui. I ordered the Black Angus steak, but what was served was definitely not Black Angus. The meat had a strange taste and texture, and I honestly couldn’t even identify what cut or type of meat it was — but it certainly wasn’t what was advertised. I double checked and they still said it was a black angus…  My son ordered a pizza, which was also very poor in quality and flavor.  To be fair, the service was ok and the staff were polite and attentive. However, when it comes to food, this place is not something I would recommend. The quality simply doesn’t match the menu description or the prices"
- **Claire Grant** (1/5): "Great atmosphere with live singer…. However the food was pretty rubbish. Thai cashew chicken was crap breaded chicken, no flavour. Calamari chewy and inedible. Chips had a bizarre dip. The pizza should have been the star and taste was good… however last slice had massive long hair baked in which I ended up having to pull out of my mouth. So much great food in Samui, this was absolutely awful. Total let down on last night!"
- **Sop U** (4/5): "Nice ambience. They moved us to a table by the beach once there was space. They also made vegetarian food for us. It was quite tasty. The Pad Thai portion could have been a bit more."
