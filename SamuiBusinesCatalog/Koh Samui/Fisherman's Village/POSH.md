### Top Reviews
- **Martyn Symes** (5/5): "Amazing! We had such a lovely day!  So relaxing, chilled and nice. It was great to have a private boat experience, not having to visit the islands with loads of other people.  Being able to chill out and sunbath on deck, drink fresh coconut and eat fresh fruit in the sun, dive off the boat in the middle of nowhere, eat on a private jetty for lunch.... Yes it is relatively expensive, but it a extremely nice treat!  That said, there were 4 of us and if you paid for a individual trip on a tourist boat you would pay a fair bit and it would be nowhere near as nice! Well worth it!!"
