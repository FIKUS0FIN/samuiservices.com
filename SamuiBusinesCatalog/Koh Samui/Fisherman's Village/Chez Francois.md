### Top Reviews
- **Chaiyo Jiarachaikul** (4/5): "Chez Francois Koh Samui is truly one of the most impressive dining experiences in Koh Samui. The food is absolutely delicious — every dish is beautifully presented, rich in flavor, and crafted with clear attention to detail. It genuinely feels like a fine dining experience comparable to top French restaurants.  The restaurant itself is small and intimate, with only a limited number of tables, which makes the atmosphere cozy and exclusive. Because of this, it is highly recommended to make a reservation in advance to secure your spot.  Service is exceptional — the staff are attentive, professional, and very welcoming, making the entire evening even more enjoyable. In terms of value, it is definitely worth it considering the quality of food and overall experience.  One small drawback is that the wine list is relatively limited, which might not fully satisfy wine enthusiasts. However, this does not take away from the overall outstanding experience. If you are visiting Koh Samui, this restaurant is absolutely worth trying."
- **Olesia** (5/5): "We celebrated my birthday at this lovely French restaurant with my husband and our two children, and it turned out to be an absolutely exceptional evening. We chose the four-course tasting menu, and every single dish was beautifully presented and full of incredible flavor. The beef cheeks were especially unforgettable — we highly recommend trying them.  This is exactly the kind of place that may look modest at first glance but is truly a hidden gem. The wine pairing was outstanding and complemented each course perfectly.  Chef, we wish you good health and continued success in your craft. Thank you for such a memorable dinner and warm atmosphere."
- **Richard Griffin** (5/5): "Make sure to book in advance. There are limited spaces, and for a good reason.  This is a must go to if you like fine dining with excellent service.  The restaurant is very quaint, there are al a cart or a tasting menu, a great wine menu too.  You'll love it."
