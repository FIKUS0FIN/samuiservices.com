### Top Reviews
- **Hazel Huang** (5/5): "Stunning resort, I can’t fault any of it. We felt so lucky to be here! The entire place was beautiful and immaculately maintained, and we felt that the strip of beach outside the resort was some of the nicest along Chaweng. I don’t usually have high expectations for hotel food but the à la carte restaurant at Banana Fan Sea was actually good. Staff are thoughtful and kind. We’d often return to our room with a citronella candle lit in the bathroom which was such a lovely touch."
- **Vilmos Kovacs** (4/5): "In my opinion, this is one of the best hotels on the best part of Chaweng Beach. The resort has a beautiful boutique style — authentic yet modern — and the location right on the beach is fantastic.  The only downside is that such a great place sometimes attracts guests who unfortunately don’t know how to behave. But overall, it’s still a wonderful place to stay."
- **lynn Wild** (5/5): "Fantastic hotel on a beautiful beach. Great rooms. Stand out is great staff who are helpful and friendly. The swim up bar is lovely. Twice a day happy hour is good. Food and the Thai buffet were great. Lovely touch was managers drinks reception early on evening. Cannot recommend the hotel, staff and facilities highly enough"
