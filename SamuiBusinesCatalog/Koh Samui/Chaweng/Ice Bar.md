### Top Reviews
- **Ella Benchan** (5/5): "I visited an ice bar in Samui with my friends, and it was such a unique and fun experience! The bar is on the smaller side, but it has an incredibly cool and distinctive vibe inside. Upon arrival, they ask for an entrance fee, which includes one drink. The drink is served in a glass made entirely of ice, which is super cool and adds to the experience. They also provide you with warm clothes and accessories to help you get the best photos.  The overall experience was fantastic – we had a great time taking fun photos, enjoying the ice-cold drinks, and just soaking in the unique atmosphere. The staff were friendly and made sure we were comfortable throughout our visit.  The location is perfect, right in the heart of Chaweng's party area. It’s a great spot to check out before heading out for a big night out. Highly recommend it for anyone looking for something fun and different to do in Samui!"
- **City of Angels** (5/5): "Awesome icebar and experience in Samui. This place is seriously cold. They do give you jackets and hats to wear before going in. We bought entry for 280 baht which included a flavored vodka shot. Even the shot glasses are made of ice. You can chill in the bar and take photos and there’s even a polar bear head to wear. Friendly staff and great experience."
- **Xavier ARRUEGO** (5/5): "What a great experience ! Imagine enjoying 30 degrees celcius Samui night Life and all of the sudden being transfered into a minus 30 degrees all Ice bar environment enjoying a vodka flavored shot dressed in a glass made of Ice as well. This for Only 280bath. Definetely recommend. We had so much fun with my Friends !"
