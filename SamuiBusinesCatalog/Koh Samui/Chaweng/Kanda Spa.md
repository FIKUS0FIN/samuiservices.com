- **Marion Athané** (5/5): "Meow was my massage therapist and she was wonderful. Very professional, with good English, and she fully respected my request for a soft massage, with extra attention to the head and face.  The place is very clean and peaceful, and the massage room feels calm and relaxing. Soft background music plays during the treatment. Even if there is another person nearby, the space is well separated by curtains, and there is no noise or talking from the therapists, which I really appreciated.  The massage ends with a lovely herbal tea served outside, a nice finishing touch. I paid 400 baht for a one-hour oil massage, and it was excellent value for the quality of the experience."
