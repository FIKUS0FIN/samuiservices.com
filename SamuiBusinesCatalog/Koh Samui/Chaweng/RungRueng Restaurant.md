### Top Reviews
- **RJ** (5/5): "The food is always so delicious and the staff are friendly and welcoming. They’ve made recent updates/renovations that are quite nice also. There are plenty of shakes/smoothies and cold beer on the menu to cool you off on the hot days. There are good music videos (most always in English) always playing on the tv and there is also a pool table for entertainment. It’s worth a visit up on Chaweng Hill, it’s not too far from main road. Just 2-3 minutes after turning by Caltex station. ⛽️"
- **Taj Wortham** (5/5): "My group of 3 stayed at an Airbnb nearby and ended up eating here multiple times. It was just too convenient and too good to pass up. The staff were super friendly, the food came out quickly, and everything was both tasty and reasonably priced. Even if you’re not staying in the area, I’d definitely recommend making the trip for some authentic, family-run Thai food.  I even tried their pizza one night, and it was surprisingly delicious. A fun change of pace from the usual dishes. Honestly, you can’t go wrong with anything here. Highly recommend!"
- **Grace Chizawsky** (5/5): "I had the cashew chicken with rice for lunch and it was to die for. The chicken was cooked perfectly and the flavors were amazing. The staff were so sweet and friendly, which made the experience even better. Honestly, everything on the menu looked delicious and it was hard to choose. Highly recommend stopping here if you’re in the area!"
