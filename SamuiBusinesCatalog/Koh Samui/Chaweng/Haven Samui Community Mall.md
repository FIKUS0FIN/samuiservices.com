### Top Reviews
- **Peachy** (5/5): "Cozy mall in the heart of Chaweng with events almost every weekend. Good eateries in the mall including Thai, Italian, French, and Kosher. Plus a few clothing boutiques where you can shop too!  There's also Tops Supermarket and upcoming bowling lane + arcade.  Lots of parking space."
- **Gavin Asavapatthasakul** (5/5): "A spacious, open area with a relaxed atmosphere, free from crowds. The design embraces nature, featuring plenty of wood, glass, and greenery. There are cafés and restaurants offering food and drinks, with cozy corners perfect for unwinding. Unlike large shopping malls, it remains peaceful and ideal for those who appreciate tranquility. Convenient parking makes it a great spot to spend quality time with family."
- **The Humble World Trotter** (5/5): "Nicely located at Chaweng Beach Road, right across a very good hotel, Banana Fan Sea. It is very new and just had a soft opening on 11 Dec 2024. Not all the shops are open. On the day we went: Tops, Le Bistro a French restaurant, Quicksilver, Luxoptics, 69 Slam, an art gallery, money exchange booth, and a few more shops were open. The design of the mall is very nice and beautiful. I like the way they design the second floor and the 4 buildings weaving together nicely with a large open space in the middle. Very clever and inspiring design. Well done!"
