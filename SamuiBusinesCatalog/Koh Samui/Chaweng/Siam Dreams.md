### Top Reviews
- **Hays** (2/5): "Wouldn't recommend. We came here today and was very disappointed. I ordered the chicken ceasar salad and it was definitely not a ceasar salad. There was no dressing, no croutons and looked nothing like what was being advertised. I wouldnt have minded but it wasn't fresh at all, with browning lettuce. I ate the chicken only. My husband got the avocado and scrambled egg, was meant to be on toast but came on a roll and the avocado was sour, it didn't taste right so he couldn't eat it. The guy offered to take the avocado meal off the bill which was generous but overall would not return or recommend."
- **Marija Kraft** (5/5): "Christmas being far away from home, our family decided to hunt for some German food and stumbled onto this spot near Central Samui.  The Schnitzel was tender and perfectly seasoned, while the potato salad—hand‑made by the owner himself specifically for Christmas —was creamy, tangy, and oddly comforting in the tropical heat.  It just proves my theory: the most authentic Western food in Asia lives in these hidden‑away places where someone decides to bring a piece of home and blend it with the local vibe. If you’re craving real German comfort on Koh Samui, this is the spot."
- **Randolph Schäfer** (5/5): "Good German food, thai people like it too. The owner Patrick is the cook"
