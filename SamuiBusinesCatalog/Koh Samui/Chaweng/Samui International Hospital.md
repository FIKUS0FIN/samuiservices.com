### Top Reviews
- **Pankaj Parihar** (5/5): "We had to visit Samui International Hospital twice in one day, and I am truly grateful for the care we received.  In the morning, we brought our young son in due to significant swelling around his eye after what appeared to be a mosquito bite. The doctor examined him thoroughly, explained everything clearly, and reassured us calmly. The nursing staff was extremely gentle and kind with him, which meant a lot as parents. Later the same day, I unfortunately got stung by sea urchins in the ocean and had to return for treatment. Once again, the team was prompt, professional, and very attentive. The doctor handled the situation efficiently and the nurses were supportive throughout the procedure. What stood out most was their patience, clear communication, and genuine concern for patients. As travelers, that reassurance is priceless.  Thank you to the doctor and the entire nursing staff for your professionalism and compassion. Highly recommended for anyone visiting Koh Samui."
- **Karolina Krawczyk** (5/5): "I'm so thankful for the team at this hospital, they took such a good care of me!
