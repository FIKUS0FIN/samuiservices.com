### Top Reviews
- **Dariusz Witkowski** (5/5): "The best fish I've had on Koh Samui. Fantastic drinks. Very friendly and helpful staff. Wonderful atmosphere. A fantastic place for dinner and pre-party drinks. I highly recommend it."
- **Tony Marsden** (5/5): "A wonderful on the beach restaurant, part of the Chaweng Gardens Beach Resort. The menu covers Thai and European foods and there is a cook to order seafood bar for grilled, fried or baked fish. I thoroughly enjoyed a steak whilst my lady enjoyed squid from the seafood bar. The staff were all very friendly and added to the friendly, relaxed atmosphere. Prices were very competitive."
- **Gozzi Bråten** (5/5): "We made a pit stop here after a walk along the beach. We had a late lunch and drinks while looking out over the sea and enjoying the view beneath their amazing tree, with small birds and squirrels around us. A bit more expensive than the alternatives, but absolutely worth it. Great food and great drinks."
