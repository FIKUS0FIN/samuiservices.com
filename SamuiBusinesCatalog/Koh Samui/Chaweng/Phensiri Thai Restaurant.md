### Top Reviews
- **Natalia Percival** (5/5): "Im a big fan of Thai cuisine and this place has pleasantly surprised me. Their menu is large and will suite different tastes. Some dishes are completely not spicy. I love my spices so opted for a bit of heat. All ingredients are super fresh, cooked to perfection and served in a speedy manner with a smile. We were so impressed that came back 3 more times to dine there. It is my advice to book the table in advance. They will try to accommodate you without the booking but you may need to wait. There are 3/4 parking spaces at the rear which are almost always full. Parking is available 100 metres away on the corner towards the beach."
- **Gursheel Dhillon** (4/5): "Fantastic, authentic, soul food no doubt. Every dish was absolutely on point - the best meal we had in Samui. Can't say the same about the service sadly. We were made to wait unnecessarily (almost like a punishment) when we can clearly see several empty tables.  We had to repeatedly immediately asked for mosquito spray as we were getting bitten incessantly. The waitor can even make a joke that "oh the mozzies r crazy and so when they bite you, we become crazy".  We realised the manager who was seating everyone was really polite to Caucasians but not Indians.  Even when we were placing our orders, the waitor was like oh it's spicy you need extra large rice and we're like erm we have been coming to Thailand for the last 18 years, we know our Thai food.  They were shocked when we ordered more than they expected - I mean if the food is excellent, you will order more right?  Suddenly, they were a little nicer seeing how much we ordered. The worst service experience in all of Samui to be brutally honest - almost unwelcoming but the food is worth enduring a lil disrespect."
- **唐有建** (5/5): "The restaurant was very full, and the staff were quite friendly. The food did take a little while to arrive, but honestly, it was absolutely delicious.  This is the most refined Thai cuisine I’ve ever had. No wonder they won a Michelin award — if you ever get the chance, you definitely have to try it at least once."
