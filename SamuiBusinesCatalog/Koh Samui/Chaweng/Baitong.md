### Top Reviews
- **David Rashethie** (5/5): "We had a very good experience during our stay at Banana Fan sea resort Hotel, especially the breakfast at Baitong Restaurant. The restaurant offered a wide variety of breakfast items on the buffet line, with many choices available for everyone to enjoy.  We especially enjoyed the Pad Thai, which was very delicious. The staff were friendly and attentive, and the atmosphere in the restaurant was pleasant and relaxing.  Overall, we had a wonderful stay and would be happy to recommend the hotel to others visiting Koh Samui."
- **Abhishek Dubey** (5/5): "Excellent food and service. Lot of vegetarian and vegan options. Aom, Van & Teng do a great job with the guests. They have live music, sea views and fire shows.  However, they should arrange “to go breakfast bags” for guests who are leaving early for airport or for excursions. You have to tell them 12 hours in advance which is not great."
- **Thelover Cat** (5/5): "In the morning at Baitong Restaurant, there is a breakfast buffet with a wide variety of food all in one place. The highlight is the fresh juice corner, where juices are freshly squeezed every day, giving you a refreshing start to the morning."
