### Top Reviews
- **W A** (5/5): "Wonderful Stay – Highly Recommended!  I thoroughly enjoyed my month-long stay at this villa in Thailand. The staff were incredibly friendly, and any language barriers were easily managed using Google Translate or by speaking with Kittabrun, who speaks excellent English and was always happy to help.  I stayed in one of the bungalows with a private pool, which was a perfect size and ideal for relaxing. There’s also a shared full-length pool available if you fancy a proper swim – both pools were always clean and well maintained. The garden surrounding the bungalow was private, spacious, and secure – we even felt comfortable leaving items outside with no issues.  The groundsman and maids were fantastic – super friendly and very efficient. Any minor issues that popped up during the stay were quickly resolved without hassle.  I had read some reviews mentioning insects, but we had no real problems during our stay. In four weeks, we spotted just two insects inside and a few mosquitoes early on. As long as you avoid leaving doors open, you’ll be absolutely fine.  The complex itself has a very relaxed, peaceful vibe. Even in shared areas, it often felt like you had the place to yourself. The WiFi was strong and reliable throughout my stay, which made working remotely smooth and stress-free. Netflix was also available in the bungalow, perfect for unwinding in the evenings.  The location is great – just a 10-minute drive or a 30-minute walk to Chaweng Beach. Scooters are cheap and easy to rent, but if that’s not your thing, Grab taxis (like Uber) are available. I’d also recommend hopping on a local tuktuk from the main road – most places can be reached for just 100 baht per person, which is a real bargain.  A special mention goes to the cats living on the property – they became part of the experience. They’re no trouble at all unless you choose to befriend them with food!  Overall, the villa offers excellent value for money compared to similar properties on the island. I’m already looking forward to returning next year."
