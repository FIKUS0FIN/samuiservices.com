- **siddharth singh** (2/5): "Stayed from 25th to 30th March 2026.  The resort benefits from a prime location and a generously sized property, both of which create strong first impressions. However, beyond this, the experience struggles to consistently meet the expectations associated with its pricing and positioning.  Our initial stay in the Deluxe Garden View room (3 nights) revealed dated interiors and a rather basic overall finish. The bathroom, in particular, felt below par for a property of this category. Additionally, the persistent mosquito issue across the resort was hard to overlook and detracted from overall comfort.  The breakfast offering lacked both variety and consistency. Replenishment was irregular, and the service, while courteous, lacked the attentiveness and energy one would expect. The omelette station was a rare bright spot.  Evenings felt noticeably underwhelming, especially in comparison to nearby venues such as Ark Bar Beach Club and Elephant Beach Club, which offer a far more engaging atmosphere.  Upgrading to a higher room category for the final two nights did enhance the stay, though it also highlighted the disparity in quality within the room categories.  Overall, while the location and scale are compelling, the product, service refinement, and experiential depth fall short of what one would expect at this price point."
