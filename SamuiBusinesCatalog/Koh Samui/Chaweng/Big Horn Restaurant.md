### Top Reviews
- **jyoti soni** (5/5): "Big Horn Restaurant was honestly one of the best parts of our Koh Samui trip. We visited 4 times during our 5-day stay, which says everything about how much we loved it.  The food is absolutely amazing — delicious, fresh, beautifully presented, and consistently excellent every single visit. On top of that, the prices are extremely reasonable and truly value for money considering the quality and portion sizes.  What impressed us the most was the service. Every single staff member was warm, attentive, and genuinely cared about the experience. I can’t name just one person because the entire team was outstanding. Their attention to detail and hospitality were exceptional — honestly, even some 5-star hotels fail to provide service at this level.  They have won several awards, and after experiencing this place multiple times, I genuinely feel they deserve every one of them. Highly recommended to anyone visiting Koh Samui."
- **caroline doerksen** (5/5): "Delicious drinks and food. Highly recommend you visit this restaurant during your stay on Koh Samui island. The tuna steak was incredible and the deep fried bananas and honey were fantastic. All of the staff were so lovely and kind. We also received 2 plates for complimentary watermelon for dessert. :)"
- **Happy Happy** (5/5): "The food was some of the most flavoursome and well presented meals I’ve enjoyed in Thailand. For me the calamari is one of the best in the world prepared fresh by the chef. The restaurant is Thai owned, a refreshing change in Koh Samui where foreign ownership takes the lion share of the tourist market. I enjoyed Penang curry chicken, green papaya salad and the calamari. Fabulous experience, highly recommend"
