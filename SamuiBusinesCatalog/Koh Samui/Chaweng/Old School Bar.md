### Top Reviews
- **Lital Levitin** (5/5): "Real Thai vibes! One of the most authentic Thai bars I’ve ever been to! No fancy cocktails – just cold beer with ice, good vibes, and amazing live Thai music. Mostly locals, which makes the atmosphere even more special. I speak a bit of Thai, and they actually invited me on stage to sing a Thai song with the band – such an unforgettable moment! 🎤🇹🇭 The staff is super sweet and welcoming, and the energy is just pure joy. If you're looking for a real, fun Thai night out (and maybe a chance to sing on stage 😄) – this is the place."
- **Peej** (4/5): "Great little local hangout next to the lake. Excellent hip hop and rock music playing while band setting up. Once band set up the music was fantastic from both groups that played when we were there. Food overall was great with a few dishes missing the mark slightly. If you want to experience a typical local social hangout, this is one of the better ones."
- **Jessi Heifets** (5/5): "so much fun! Thai live music, good vibes, and a great local feel."
