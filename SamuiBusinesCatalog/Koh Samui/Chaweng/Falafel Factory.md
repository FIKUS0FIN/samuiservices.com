### Top Reviews
- **Mohamed Aslam** (1/5): "I decided to try their shawarma after seeing the positive reviews and because it was close to my hotel. Unfortunately, it was absolutely not worth it. It didn’t resemble any Middle Eastern shawarma I’ve ever had — from the bread to the veggies, meat, and sauces, nothing was even close. The taste was terrible, and I ended up throwing it away after just one bite."
- **Sonja Nova** (5/5): "I was seriously craving some comfort street food, and since I don’t just eat anything, a good kebab wrap came to mind. But where to get it? Of course, from a trusted vendor. And as always.. a hit. Thank you so much — just mmm! 🫶🏼 Great people behind the counter 🙏🏼"
- **Sandra Juhanson** (5/5): "Perfect falafel. I really enjoyed my wrap. I took the small one but it was totally perfect size for me. The ingredients were very fresh and I was pleasantly surprised that this place gets that a good wrap must have a good sauce!!! It is ESSENCE for a wrap to succeed. And this place offered various sauces which tasted really good, not cheap at all. I was pleasantly surprised, considering that the place looks very modest. Would order again and would recommend to others."
