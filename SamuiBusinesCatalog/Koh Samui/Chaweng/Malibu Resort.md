I then asked to leave and get a refund for the remaining nights but they refused and were very rude about it. I ended up leaving early anyway. I could barely sleep because there were lots of mosquitoes and bed bugs, as well as very thin walls so you can hear everything. Terrible sewer smell in the room too. Very run down room. The breakfast was horrible and very limited. The only good thing about this resort is it’s next to the beach. The hotel is very expensive for what you get - you can spend the same amount of money on a much better resort."
