### Top Reviews
- **Joshua Horn** (3/5): "I stayed at Avani Chaweng for several nights during my trip through Thailand. While the hotel has a great location and the staff was generally friendly, my overall experience was mixed.  Unfortunately, the cleanliness of the room did not meet the standard I would expect for a hotel in this price range. I received stained towels on multiple occasions, the shower area was not properly maintained, and there were visible signs of mold on the ceiling.  I was also disappointed that access to parts of the pool and beach club area required additional payment, which was not clearly communicated beforehand. Furthermore, there was confusion regarding the included minibar and the promised 500 THB resort credit during check-out.  To the hotel's credit, management responded professionally to my complaint and refunded the resort credit afterwards, which I appreciate.  Overall, the hotel has potential and a good location, but considering the price of over 4,000 THB per night, I expected a higher level of cleanliness and clearer communication."
- **Nora Page** (4/5): "This was definitely one of the best hotels I have stayed in, in Thailand. The service was amazing, super friendly staff. The receptionist Fern was very nice and helpful. The only to downfalls are that the music from the seen beach club is very loud and the placement of the hotel is in the bottom of Chaweng so it’s kind of a long walk to centrum. But besides that would definitely recommend, especially if you’re a group of young people."
- **יוסף מרגי** (5/5): "I would like to give a special compliment to the hotel staff member Neng. He was extremely kind, professional, and always willing to help with a big smile.
