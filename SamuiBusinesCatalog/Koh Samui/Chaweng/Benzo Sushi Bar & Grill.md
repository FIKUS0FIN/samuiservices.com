### Top Reviews
- **Oops i ate it** (3/5): "The shop is quite small and doesn’t have air conditioning, but surprisingly it wasn’t too hot. Also appreciated that there were no mosquitoes thanks to the mosquito trap machine — small detail but it made the experience more comfortable.  Food was okay overall. The rolls were reasonably priced and decent, but the sashimi wasn’t as fresh as expected, especially the tuna. Prices felt a little on the higher side, though I guess that’s expected for a tourist area."
- **Shahed Tako** (5/5): "I first came here 3 years ago, and I still remember how amazing the sushi was. Since then, I’ve tried sushi in many different countries, but honestly nothing compares to this place. The quality, freshness, flavors, and presentation are all on another level. Every bite tastes perfect. This is easily one of the best sushi restaurants I’ve ever tried, and I’ll definitely keep coming back whenever I can. Highly recommended!"
- **Goose** (5/5): "Three of us went for dinner last night and we really enjoyed ourselves. The service needed to be a little better specially as it’s not a big place but overall good. The food was superb and we will certainly go back, it’s in an easy location to get to and if you are staying on Chweng beach you can walk along the beach to get there. So give it a go"
