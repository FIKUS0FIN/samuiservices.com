### Top Reviews
- **Olivia Humphreys** (5/5): "We had the most amazing time at this hotel. The room was perfect and clean, the pool and view was beautiful, and the food at the restaurant was delicious every time. I am coeliac and every member of staff made sure I was looked after, catered to my allergy’s and made sure I had gluten free bread every morning at breakfast. If I ever come back to Ko Samui, I will be coming back here! Thank you so much!"
- **Tatiana Sergienia** (2/5): "I wad not impressed by the food: the starter (potato slices with truffle) was overfried in a too big amount of oil. The main course (risotto with baby octopus) was a bit overcooked, wine should have been evaporated better. Overall it looked better than tasted. However, the place is located in a beautiful spot where you can enjoy amazing sunset and ocean"
- **Patty** (5/5): "We stayed at the accommodation at the same premises so we frequented Olivio’s throughout our stay and we cannot fault anything. The staff were great, all meals including the two for one pizzas, the breakfast and the menu items were delicious. The restaurant was always immaculately clean and well presented and the background music added to the ambience of the restaurant. The service was prompt, professional and efficient and it was an added bonus which kept us from going elsewhere. I have no hesitation in recommending Olivio’s."
