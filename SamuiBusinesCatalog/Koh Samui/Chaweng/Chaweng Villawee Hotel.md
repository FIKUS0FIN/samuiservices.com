### Top Reviews
- **Rob Andrews** (4/5): "Booked in 4 nights ..and this hotel is lovely. Good location in Chaweng, not on beach side. Very clean, modern flair and very welcoming staff. After we checked in to our poolside room, we realised that this older pair had difficulty accessing the pool...no small steps just a large drop and no railing. Great for the younger folk with no joint problems lol... We advised the reception that we would be checking out after one night and that we were not looking for a refund. However, the manager and staff were brilliant and organised a 50% refund which was absolutely grand and very appreciated. The overnight stay was great, really lovely bed, great bathroom (we reported a leak in plumbing and that was fixed within 30 minutes ). We had brekkie the next morning, although limited choice the food was fresh, brekkie egg options available and strong coffee... what else do you need !!! The pool waterfall is lovely to hear and see in the brekkie room, with lovely large louvres which give lovely breezes. If our old bones were not so old ..perfect, quiet part of Chaweng to stay !!!"
- **Bohdana Kráľová** (5/5): "We were welcomed by very friendly staff, which made a great first impression. The room was clean, cozy, and well soundproofed, allowing for a comfortable stay. Breakfast was good, and the location was convenient. Due to the hotel’s proximity to the airport, airplane noise could be heard quite often. However, it didn’t bother us at all. We actually enjoyed watching the planes take off and land. The pool looked great as well, although unfortunately we didn’t get to use it due to bad weather. Overall, a pleasant and enjoyable stay."
- **ashyy** (4/5): "Really enjoyed our stay at Chaweng Villawee Hotel. Even though it’s in a busy area, we slept very well. You can walk to all the shops easily. You can hear airplanes occasionally, but it didn’t bother us. The pool area is very nice and perfect for relaxing. Great overall experience!"
