## Description
Koh Samui's largest and most prestigious residential project, offering an exceptional living experience. Spanning over 18,000 sqm, this expansive development features 14 modern buildings with 533 beautifully designed units. As the first EIA-approved condominium project on the island, it stands as a symbol of quality and innovation.

- **Penelopé** (2/5): "Honestly, this place is a bit of a shambles right now. It’s giving 'unfinished hotel' rather than a cozy condo. The units are tiny and way too cramped—even for one person, the kitchen and bathroom are basically a joke. Plus, the sink faucet is weirdly long? It literally blocks the whole basin, making it a pain just to wash your face.  Also, a major heads-up: the water smells like a straight-up swimming pool. The chlorine is insane. If you’re moving in, you 100% need a filtered shower head or a proper water purifier, otherwise your hair and skin are going to be absolutely trashed.  If you're in Building 6 or 7, good luck. You’re basically living on a construction site. Constant noise, workers singing at the top of their lungs, and dust everywhere. The walls/ceilings are paper-thin too; if you're on the first floor, you’ll hear every single footstep from upstairs.  Pros: It's new, has a washer/stove, and the ground floor garden is chill... until the smell of cigarettes and weed hits you.  The management needs to make up its mind. It functions okay for short-term hotel guests, but for anyone actually living here? It’s a mess. The hybrid 'hotel-meets-condo' setup just doesn't work for residents."
- **Digzz Digzz** (2/5): "I would like to raise several concerns regarding the current condition and management of the condominium facilities.  The public staircase appears incomplete, with paint stains visible throughout, and the windows in the stairway have not been properly cleaned. At the pool area, there are insufficient sun loungers, and music from the pool bar is played at an excessively loud volume in the mornings. Overall, many aspects of the property give the impression of being rushed or unfinished, including landscaping, where a number of plants and trees appear unhealthy or poorly maintained.  It also seems that the condominium units were delivered overdue and in a rushed condition. The gym facilities require attention: the treadmill machines are not adequate for running, the sauna is very small and unclean, and there are no lockers available. In addition, the gym equipment does not appear to be cleaned regularly. Finally, the metal gates near the pool are already showing signs of rust, which raises concerns about maintenance and durability.  I hope these issues can be addressed promptly to improve the overall standard of the property."
