### Top Reviews
- **Fabio Vincenti** (5/5): "Cracking little restaurant with what looks like has a boutique hotel attached. Lovely and clean inside with attentive staff. Nice menu with a mix of Italian dishes and Thai dishes. The kids were supper happy to find an authentic pizza after ten days of Thai food. The Thai green curry and cashew chicken were fantastic. Very well priced and the 99bhat happy hour cocktail was a nice surprise."
- **Wunda Stone** (5/5): "Had such a wonderful dinner at Rice on my 2nd night during my stay at Jalmin hotel. The staff made us feel right at home and they were super helpful, making sure we had plenty of space and never felt rushed.  The food was great and their homemade pizza was a highlight as well as their extensive cheese and cold cuts selections. We had tried a few different dishes like the rack of lamb, pesto spaghetti and tuna salad, and every bite was packed with flavour throughout the dishes.  The restaurant also houses extensive selections of wines and bar beverages.  The atmosphere and ambiance is thoughtfully decorated with Thai motifs and elements, featuring extensive teak furnishings and panels throughout the interiors. The setting is stylish and relaxed. If you're on Koh Samui want a memorable Italian and Thai cuisine with warm service, this place is absolutely one of them."
- **RE/MAX Island Real Estate** (5/5): "If you’re in Chaweng and craving amazing food, you have to try Rice Restaurant.  This place is not just about authentic Thai cuisine — it’s a perfect mix of Thai and Western favorites, making it ideal for every type of traveler.  From the moment you walk in, the vibe is warm, relaxed, and welcoming. But the real highlight? The food. Every dish is packed with flavor — from rich Thai curries and spicy Tom Yum to delicious pasta, spaghetti, and freshly made pizza. Whether you’re in the mood for Thai or something more familiar, they do both incredibly well."
