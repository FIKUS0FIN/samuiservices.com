### Top Reviews
- **Koray Cankara** (5/5): "I'm writing this review from the hotel right now. My wife, son, and I had a very comfortable 10-day holiday. The hotel is located at the beginning of the main street in Chaweng Beach, a famous area of ​​Koh Samui. It's situated in the lively atmosphere of Chaweng, but also offers endless peace and relaxation when you retreat to your room or hotel. The staff are generally very kind. I don't know about all the rooms, but the suites are quite spacious and provide all the comforts. The mini-bar is replenished daily. The breakfast has a wide variety. The internet speed is acceptable. The deposit they take upon arrival is a bit high. There are two beautiful swimming pools, one on the beach and one in the inner courtyard. And the inner courtyard pool bar is especially nice, and the staff there are very polite. The biggest problem at the pools is that even in the early morning hours, guests occupy the sunbeds by placing their towels there. The hotel management needs to be stricter about this. The beach is very beautiful and peaceful. During our visit (the second half of January), due to the tide, the beach disappeared until late afternoon, but in the evening it was incredibly peaceful. The gym is very clean, and although there isn't a lot of equipment, you can still keep up with your workout while on vacation. There were complaints about neglect on booking sites and in reviews here. While these comments are valid, I didn't see much neglect or wear and tear. Yes, there are minor things. And also, the resort is practically in the sea, so this kind of wear and tear is normal. But still, I don't think it's a 5-star hotel. However, I was quite satisfied with my stay and, as someone who has seen other parts of Koh Samui, I highly recommend it to anyone coming to Koh Samui."
- **Robin Innes** (5/5): "Love this hotel, location is perfect for beachlife and nightlife. Staff are friendly and attentive. Breakfast is excellent. Rooms are large with good air con. Pools are kept immaculately clean. Having stayed 5 times here my only complaint is that check in is always a little chaotic and could be smoother."
- **Darin Shipp** (5/5): "We have just had 14 nights at this resort and  have had a wonderful experience.  The staff are fantastic, the facilities are clean, well maintained and the location is ideal. We had the pleasure of meeting Joakim ( Hotel Manager) at one of the nightly events , who was a delight and very engaging. We will be booking in the future."
