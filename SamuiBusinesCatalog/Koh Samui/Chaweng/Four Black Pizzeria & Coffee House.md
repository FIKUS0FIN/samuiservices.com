### Top Reviews
- **Frederik Kiel** (5/5): "Best pizza I've had during my 3 weeks in Samui. Prices are also very fair for what you get. Paid around 330 baht for a pizza and an ice tea"
- **Simon** (5/5): "This is a really good italien restaurant. The Bruschetta with tomato and mozzarella was delicious and not too crunchy. The pizza was handmade and tasted amazing. The dough was perfect, not too crunchy and not too soft. I recommend going here if u want to eat italien food in thailand. The price was also really fair (~300 per pizza). The espresso was decent."
- **Andreas Flanco** (5/5): "Out of dosen pizzerias I visited on Samui this one is by far my favourite. Pizza is a matter of personal taste of course, but I was pleasantly surprised here: very tasty soft dough, stretchy cheese, top-notch ingredients. I really enjoyed every bite. Don't bother with the delivery: go there and eat it straight from the oven. If you going to be dissapointed with this one, hell, I don't know what kind of heavenly pizza you've been eating all your life, but I want a slice!"
