### Top Reviews

- **Angelo Palmitessa** (5/5): "We had a fantastic experience here. The food was outstanding and truly authentic – you can really taste the quality in every dish. The service was top-notch as well; the staff is incredibly friendly, attentive, and welcoming. A special shout-out goes to the Italian espresso – without a doubt the best I’ve had on Koh Samui! If you’re looking for genuine Italian cuisine and wonderful hospitality, this is the place to be. Highly recommended!"
- **Kevin H** (5/5): "Took my daughter out for an Italian today, she loves Spaghetti.  This restaurant is one of those unexpected little gems that just surprises you with the quality of the food.  If you are in Samui, and fancy an Italian, then don’t go anywhere else. This seriously was awesome.  I love pizza, this was hands down the best pizza I’ve had for a very long time.  Will be a regular treat for me now.  The only thing I would like to see, is maybe a small kids menu as the portions are far too big for kids, but they will box up any left and explain how best to reheat as well. 😉"
