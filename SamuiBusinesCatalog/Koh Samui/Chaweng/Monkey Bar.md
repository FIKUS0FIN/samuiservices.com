### Top Reviews
- **M Bean** (4/5): "Beachfront bar at Sheraton hotel. Fire show at 8pm, lasts 20-30 minutes. Smaller show than at bigger/busier parts of the island. The food selection is a mix of Thai and western food. Quality is fine, but not high. I’d search elsewhere for higher quality food and better atmosphere. Location is convenient if you like this beach or are staying nearby. Service was friendly and prompt."
- **Nicola Davies** (5/5): "Amazing location! We enjoyed delicious beach bar food, ice cold beers and happy hour cocktails. Staff were friendly and helpful. Highly recommended!"
- **Lydia Griffiths** (5/5): "We absolutely love this bar. We stayed at the Sheraton for two weeks and had the best time here. The food is great and the drinks are top class but I have to say that it’s the staff who make the bar extra special. Everyone is so welcoming and friendly. We will miss our evening cocktail looking at the sea and sharing a story or two with our new found friends at the Blue Monkey. A must visit if you’re in Samui."
