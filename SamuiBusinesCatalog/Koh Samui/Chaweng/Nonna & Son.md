### Top Reviews
- **Peacezy Wirinya** (5/5): "The pasta was absolutely delicious and full of flavor. The staff were very friendly and provided great service throughout the meal. We chose to sit on the outdoor balcony, which had an amazing view of airplanes taking off and landing — such a unique atmosphere. The restaurant is conveniently located at Chaweng Beach, easy to get to, and the food arrived very quickly without a long wait.  The only small downside was that we asked for extra sauce and received only a little portion, plus there was an additional charge for it. Other than that, it was a very enjoyable dining experience and definitely worth visiting."
- **Angelina** (5/5): "Really enjoyed my visit here. I had the Wagyu pasta, and I can definitely recommend it,  it was full of flavor and cooked perfectly. We also tried the tiramisu, which was delicious.  The service was great as well. Everyone was friendly, attentive, and made us feel welcome throughout the evening.  Overall, a very good dining experience with great food and a nice atmosphere. I would happily come back and definitely recommend this place to others."
- **Dagmara Samko** (5/5): "What a lovely place! The food is amazing and the staff is very attentive. We’ve eaten here 3 times already and the food has been delicious every time. Highly recommend!"
