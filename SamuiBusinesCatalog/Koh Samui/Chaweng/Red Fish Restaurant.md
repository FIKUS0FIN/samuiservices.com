### Top Reviews
- **Dirk L.** (5/5): "I can really recommend the food. It was prepared very nice and the tasting, especially of the fish, was fantastic. Yes, you can get yor dinner cheaper somewhere else, but for me it was it worth. Also the Mango sticky rice, yummy!"
- **Tatiana Sergienia** (3/5): "I think it is a bit overrated and overpriced. I went for seafood set A, and I can't say anything too much special about it. Fish and prawns were even slightly overcooked, to my mind. Seeing the rating I expected something better than average, but you can for sure find same options in quality for a lower price in other places. Besides, when ordering seafood in such a place I would at least expect lemon water or aroma tissues to be offered by default."
- **Timea Ivančová** (5/5): "A stylish Thai and seafood restaurant with quality ingredients, food, wine and professional service. We simply love the baked sea bass with vegetable fried rice, which goes perfectly with a dry Chardonnay. The service is excellent and the atmosphere is so beautiful and stylish that you feel like you are in any 3-star Michelin restaurant, the only difference is the lower prices."
