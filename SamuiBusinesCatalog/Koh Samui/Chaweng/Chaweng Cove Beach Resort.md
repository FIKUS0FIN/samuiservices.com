### Top Reviews
- **P-A Nordmann** (4/5): "Excellent location by the beach, maybe the best part of Chaweng beach for swimming, quiet aera. Many bars, restaurants around as well as 7/11. You can’t fault the location. The entrance is not the most welcoming but once you find you bungalow you will feel better. Our room was beachfront, with a private little pool on the terrace. We like to stay outside and the seaview. The room itself was okay nothing fancy. 2 beds, one fridge, simple bathroom but large shower aera. Only drawback is they provide only 1 room key which ignites the power and A/C. That means when you go out, it’s getting really hot inside. They argued it was to save energy… Other hotels don’t do that. The breakfast food was okay and we have tried the lunch once which was not the best we had. Paid about 7500 THB a night in April. I wouldn’t pay more here. Overall a good stay."
- **Nicolas Fuchs** (5/5): "The staff was very friendly. They surprised me with a little birthdaycake and helped me apply disinfectant after I slipped outside the hotel due to rain, i appreciate this alot! The cleaningstaff were also very friendly in explaining me some questions about the minibar and towels. The room and facilities and the friendly baarkeeper of their restaurant were all great. Totally recommend and would stay again :)"
- **Gary Lamb** (5/5): "What words can we use to describe this hotel. Amazing, fantastic, unbelievable, stunning Why this hotel only has a 3* rating is crazy, because everything about it is 5*. The hotel manager Ananya, goes out of her way to personally make sure everything is to your satisfaction. All my wife and I can say about Chaweng Cove is that it is simply amazing, and will make sure you have the best holiday of your life. There are 3 swimming pools of various depths to cater for all ages, and their own private section of the beach, has numerous free sunbeds and parasols for their guests. When they found out it was my wife's 70th birthday during our stay, they decorated the bed and left a birthday cake, which was delicious all at no extra cost.
