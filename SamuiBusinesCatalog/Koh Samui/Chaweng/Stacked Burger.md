### Top Reviews
- **Oren Tocker** (4/5): "Good burgers, comes with fries. They give you pop corn wike waiting. They have gluten free bun. For gluten free you have to take the classic burger and pay 125 extra for the gluten free bun. The gluten free bun us nice, a little bit heavy. I think the fries are made in a speciffic gluten free fryer, i am not sure.  FYI, you are not allowed to take away burger with gluten free buns, only burger with gluten buns."
- **Wa Sun** (5/5): "STACKed Burger is hands down one of the best spots in Samui for a proper, juicy burger. The patties are flavorful, the brioche buns are soft and buttery, and every bite feels satisfying without being overcomplicated. The loaded fries are a must-try, and the cold beers pair perfectly with the beach-town vibe.  What really makes it special is the atmosphere — relaxed, fun, and social. It’s the kind of place where you come for a burger and end up staying for the vibes. Friendly staff, generous portions, and great music make it easy to come back again and again."
- **Оксана Солдатова** (5/5): "We visited this burger place and overall we really enjoyed it. The burgers were genuinely tasty, fresh and filling, so we were happy with our order.  The only downside was that there were no gloves for eating the burgers — it would have been much more convenient. Also, the blue cheese sauce tasted store-bought and a bit too salty; we were expecting a more homemade flavor.  Apart from that, everything was great and we can definitely recommend this place."
