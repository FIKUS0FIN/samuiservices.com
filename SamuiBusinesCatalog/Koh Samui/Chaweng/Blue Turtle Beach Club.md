### Top Reviews
- **Ella L** (5/5): "We really enjoyed our stay at Blue Turtle Hotel. The hotel is located near a busy street, however, it was always peaceful and not too loud. The Restaurant/bar area is located right at a beautiful beach, the food and drinks are reasonably priced and good. The rooms are designed very nicely, super clean and we were never missing anything. There is a construction site on the front side of the hotel (they are expanding), but this was declared before arrival and did not affect us, especially because we were out doing activities all day. The receptionist was really friendly and kind. We would be happy to stay at Blue Turtle Hotel again."

- **Tegan Hurst** (5/5): "We had the best 4 night stay here! The staff are so kind and friendly. The hotel was immaculate, room was cleaned everyday, offering beach and body towels fresh everyday. They gave reusable water bottles and had a purified water fountain in the restaurant and kitchen which was fab. We also loved that the pool was open 24/7 for a late night dip!  Location: right on Chaweng beach, there are many loungers available (never struggled to get one at any time of the day). Right opposite a 7/11 which is helpful. It’s around a 10 min walk from the main “strip” and livelier part of Chaweng with lots of restaurants, nail salons, massage parlours, bars etc. Nextdoor is a restaurant which is open from breakfast-late, also offering cocktails under a really nice lantern setting.  Highly recommend, super reasonable price for the size of the villa and upkeep!!"
