### Top Reviews
- **Andrew Regos** (5/5): "Great clean resort, centrally located, very nice staff. The pool is good to cool down. Beach is on the cover side. Breakfast is included and plenty. Room is neat, well appointed with comfy bed. Safe and fridge in the room. Complimentary drinks and snacks in the lobby, also scooter rental and tour offerings are available."
- **Don Leibo** (4/5): "Overall, the hotel was good and the value for money is definitely worth it. However, there were two things that felt very outdated and unreasonable for 2026: Guests are charged a penalty if they lose the room key card — which is quite unbelievable nowadays. Guests are also charged if a glass is broken in the room, which feels a bit excessive. In addition, there is no proper table on the balcony for drinks or glasses, so the chances of accidentally breaking something are actually quite high. Aside from these issues, the stay was good and the price was fair for what you get."
- **Nicky Scarabello** (3/5): "The rooms are very small. Do not expect you can see ocean from your room. If you need just a room for sleep yhe hotel is good. As I say the rooms are very small. Also the beachfront of the hotel is super small. You find there beach bags for sitting but no sunbeds for this is no space. The pool is long so you can nice swimming if you not want go in the stunning ocean. Breakfast is nothing special just basic. The location is central chaweng so if you love the chaweng beach and the nightlife perfect."
