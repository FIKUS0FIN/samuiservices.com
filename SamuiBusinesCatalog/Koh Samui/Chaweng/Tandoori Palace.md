### Top Reviews
- **Hollie Anderson** (5/5): "Without a doubt THE BEST Indian restaurant you can find in all of Thailand!! We came back twice because it was that good. The onion bhajis are incredible, the crispiness and the flavour is so good!! Then the butter chicken… I’ve never had a butter chicken that tastes so beautiful, every mouthful the flavour got better, what a dish! Massive thank you to Rocky, a great restaurant, fantastic food and wonderful staff, would highly highly recommend!!"
- **Mohib Shams** (5/5): "I had the nicest Indian food in Koh Samui here! I got the butter chicken and fish pakora and they were both delicious, fresh and well seasoned. Prices were really good too. Overall I recommend this to anyone who wants nice and afforable Indian food"
- **Ilona Nurmela** (5/5): "Our family of 5 quite enjoyed our dinner at this place. The tikka masala chicken and lamb were great and they do ask how spicy you want it; the madras chicken was good & I loved my lamb korma - yum! We also ordered cheese naan & lamb naan & lamb pakoras - with raita, these were delicious. Fries were decent size. My GnT was make-it-yourself, but that allowed me to combine how I wanted it. They also have a selection of beer & cocktails. Basically, we cleaned the table - everything went and it was delicious! Recommend this place this side of the island - a good alternative if you love spicy but want something different. Tysm!"
