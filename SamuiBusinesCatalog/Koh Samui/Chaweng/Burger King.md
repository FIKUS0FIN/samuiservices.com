### Top Reviews
- **405backpacker** (5/5): "Fast food in other countries is a bit of an experience. It’s always much nicer than in the states!  The sliding doors open with a single tap. (I now know this because I got trapped in the McDonalds.) 🤣  You order at the self-service kiosks and you can pay up front with baht after ordering!  Employees are quick to come and help with ordering at the kiosk and answering questions!  BK offers free WiFi. Most establishments print the WiFi code on the receipt but BK will provide it upon request.  One thing I’ve learned about fast food chains in Thailand is that they don’t typically have a public restroom.  The meal was prepared quickly with fresh ingredients and very good!"
- **Ethan Pegg** (1/5): "There was us and one other customer in the restaurant and after 15 minutes of waiting they gave our order to the other guy waiting! Luckily he was decent enough to come over and ask if it was ours. I’m guessing our food had been waiting on the side for a few minutes as well as it was given to him immediately upon asking. The staff seemed more interested in looking out the windows at something on the street than working. After all this they then didn’t give us the sauce with the meal so I had to go back up and ask for it. No real apology either, pretty poor."
- **Scuba Tom** (1/5): "It was the worst Burger King experience. The burgers are definitely not flame grilled as advertised. Cardboard patties, and way over priced. I ask for the large size, they gave me small, then made me pay again for for a large drink."
