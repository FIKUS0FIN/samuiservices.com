### Top Reviews
- **Gee Keyz** (5/5): "Great place to spend an evening. Sat ringside, got blood on us, made faces with one of the judges! Saw a couple of 15 y/o beat much older opponents. Great night"
- **Peter Kovacs** (5/5): "Very nice. Authentic Muay Thai boxing place. Air conditioned stadium. Ringside VIP seats for 2000 BHT and normal tickets for 1000 BHT. Every Monday, Wednesday and Friday program with 6-6 matches. International fighters as well. Food, drinks are availavle before and during the program. Ticket booking via WhatsApp."
- **Cinthia Piedrahita** (5/5): "This was my second Muay Thai fight experience — the first one was in Bangkok — and this one felt much more traditional and authentic.  The atmosphere was very entertaining and exciting. There were many tourists, but it still felt very real. I sat in the first rows and it was absolutely worth it to see the fights up close. The focus and intensity of the fighters is really impressive.  It’s definitely worth visiting this stadium if you’re in Koh Samui. You can also order food and drinks directly from your seat, which makes the whole experience very easy and enjoyable."
