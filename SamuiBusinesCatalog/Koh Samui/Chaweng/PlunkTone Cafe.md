### Top Reviews
- **Shannon Daly** (5/5): "So glad I came here to eat and work! The place is a vibe. Tons of comfortable seating with outlets, cold AC, great WiFi, food and service. The lunch set with lentil cutlet was flavorful and a great value. The cottage cheese pancakes and coffees were also unique and delicious. After walking down the deserted street I was a little skeptical and even thought they were closed because the door was locked, but you use another door that looks like another place. I stayed for several hours, hardly anyone else was inside. Good peaceful environment!"
- **Srishti Narula** (5/5): "Had a wonderful breakfast at Plunktone Café. The place has such a beautiful ambience with stunning views that make the whole experience even more special.  We ordered an omelette and a crepe, and both were absolutely delicious, fresh, flavorful, and perfectly prepared. What truly stood out was the warm hospitality. The staff was polite, attentive, and very courteous."
- **Thitaphas K.** (5/5): "Overall, everything was excellent. From the first visit, I really appreciated the variety and unique styles of food and drinks. The staff were well-trained, attentive, and provided great service throughout.  One small suggestion would be to consider having a designated smoking area. It would be nice for guests who prefer to sit outside to enjoy the atmosphere without losing their appetite due to cigarette smoke."
