### Top Reviews
- **Asa Trujillo** (4/5): "Delightful offerings and views, staff experience was mixed, but overall was very enjoyable, could see going back to spend the afternoon in the resort space."
- **Roy Shandy** (3/5): "We had breakfast. It was decent, but outrageously expensive for Thailand. Pi paid like 24 bucks each person for breakfast.  Quality certainly did not match the price. But that's a resort for you. Place was gorgeous though. Great view, too."
- **Jess Cos** (1/5): "Gorgeous location, restaurant is part of 5 star hotel. We took a canoe to their private beach and we seen locals catching fresh mussels! When we ordered from the restaurant I was craving seafood! We got the calamari to start, and it was very rubbery! You couldn’t break them, even if you pulled them apart! I ordered a seafood red curry as a main; when the waitress served them to me I asked if the mussels were fresh and she responded saying they were frozen. All the seafood was rubber! Overcooked and very overly priced! They took them off our bill. But I would highly recommend you do not dine here. Beyond the sea? More like beyond the frozen sea."
