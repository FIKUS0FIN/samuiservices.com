### Top Reviews
- **Barb Sisson** (5/5): "We didn't want to leave!  Our absolute best accommodation thus far in our 6 weeks in Thailand.  The whole suite was so comfortable.  We felt at home with so much living space and a well equipped kitchen.  The beds were very comfortable. The free breakfast was tasty and varied.  The restaurant food was good and we took advantage of room service a couple of times which was very convenient.   The pools are lovely and the views from anywhere on the property are stunning. It's a little off the beaten path so we made use of the scooter rentals on-site or called a taxi for some exploration of the rest of Samui.  I will now be ruined for any other place we stay because Shasa was so incredible."
- **Bram Tibbs** (5/5): "We loved our stay here. Positives: Super friendly and helpful staff, especially Thanida and May on reception. Fantastic pool areas and gardens. Awesome location on the beach. Amazing huge apartment - felt like a palace. Wonderful breakfast and resteraunt area. Our family really enjoyed the breakfast each morning. Good gym. Everything super clean.  Things to keep in mind: They have been renovating, but this did not bother us significantly. The location is a bit out of town, but we loved being away from the hustle and bustle. I recommend to hire a car to make life easy. We hired one at the airport for US$31 per day. Scooters are also cheap to hire and you can hire them from reception for 400 baht per day.  We loved this place and definitely hope to return one day. Thanks for having us! 🙂👍"
- **katie sisson** (5/5): "The most beautiful view, amazing pools, and spacious rooms. The rooms came fully equipped with a kitchen and washer/dryer. Ordered room service from the restaurant a couple of times and everything was delicious. Location was great for relaxing but not great if you wanted to do anything else on the island as it’s a 20-40 minutes drive from any stores or activities. Worth the extra driving time though."
