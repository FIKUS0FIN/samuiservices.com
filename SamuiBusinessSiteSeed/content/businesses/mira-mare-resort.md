## Top Reviews
> "I highly recommend it. The service is excellent, the people are friendly and helpful. The rooms are clean and comfortable, but most of the time you're outside anyway. The food there is also great, especially the Thai food. As for the location, in my opinion it's simply perfect. The beach is beautiful and you're quite centrally located. Lamai Beach gets deep quickly, even after just a few meters, and you can sometimes find some nice high waves. However, the water can also be very calm quite often. If you're looking for a good meal, there are many excellent restaurants in both directions, some less than a minute away. For activities like clubs or parties, you only have to walk 2-7 minutes. The travel agency is right next to the resort at the entrance, where you can book even better activities, such as island tours. Jet skis and other water sports are also just a 2-minute walk from the resort. If you get injured or anything happens, there's a doctor directly across the street from the resort, and if you need to do some quick shopping, there's also a 7-Eleven right there. And for massages there are enough places around too. Overall, I can highly recommend it and had absolutely no complaints."
> **- Nino (5/5/5)**
