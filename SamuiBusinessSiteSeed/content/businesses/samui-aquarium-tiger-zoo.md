## Top Reviews
> "We visited Samui Aquarium & Tiger Zoo with mixed expectations after reading very different reviews.  On one hand, the place appears clean and there are many beautiful animals, including colorful fish, birds, raccoons, tigers, and otters. For children, it may seem like an exciting opportunity to see animals up close.  However, as someone who truly loves animals, this place broke my heart.  Seeing tigers kept in small cages, pacing in circles in the same spot, was deeply upsetting. The raccoon looked sad and lonely. The otters seemed desperately hungry, as if they would do anything for a small piece of food. At the end of the visit, tourists can take photos with tigers or tiger cubs. The cubs are placed in visitors' arms for just a few quick photos and then taken away immediately, which felt very unnatural and uncomfortable.  I understand that many families and children may enjoy seeing these animals, but for adults who care deeply about animal welfare, this experience can be heartbreaking.  I do not want to leave a harsh review, because the staff may be doing their best within the circumstances. But in my honest opinion, wild animals belong in nature, where they can live freely and with dignity.  This visit left me feeling very sad and reminded me how cruel humans can sometimes be toward animals."
> **- Jakub Zahradnicek (2/5/5)**
