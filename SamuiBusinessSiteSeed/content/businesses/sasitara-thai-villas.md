> "We stayed at Sasitara Thai Villas for a little over a month, and from the very first moment we arrived, it was clear that this place is something special. The atmosphere is warm and welcoming, largely thanks to the genuinely friendly and smiling staff. The hotel itself is beautifully designed in a traditional Thai style, with lots of wood, greenery, palm trees, and coconut trees, creating a calm and cozy environment that immediately helps you relax.  We stayed in several different rooms during our time there, and each one had its own unique and authentic character. This made our stay even more enjoyable, as every room felt like a slightly different experience while still maintaining the same level of comfort and care. It’s a great way to feel connected to Thai culture while enjoying all the conveniences needed for a comfortable stay.  One of the biggest advantages of this hotel is its quiet and secluded location. The area is very peaceful, and while airplanes do pass overhead during the day, it’s important to note that they do not fly at night. Since this is an island with a limited number of flights, you quickly get used to it, and it did not negatively affect the quality of our stay at all.  The hotel has a beautiful swimming pool and direct access to the beach. The beach itself is not very large and not ideal for long swimming sessions, but a big plus is the free access to kayaks, including double kayaks, which adds a lot of value and enjoyment.  The staff truly deserves special mention. Everyone is kind, attentive, and always willing to help. The administrator Ou is especially wonderful — incredibly caring, supportive, and professional. Many staff members speak very good English, which makes communication easy and ensures you can get all the information and help you might need.  After spending about a month and a half here, we can confidently recommend Sasitara Thai Villas both for short-term stays and for a longer winter stay. The hotel also offers the option to rent villas on a monthly basis at very reasonable prices compared to other parts of the island, which makes it an excellent choice for long-term living as well.  Overall, this is a place we would happily return to and sincerely recommend to others looking for a calm, authentic, and comfortable stay in Thailand."
