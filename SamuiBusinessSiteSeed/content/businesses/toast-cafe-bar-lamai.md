## Top Reviews
> "We came to Toast Cafe Lamai expecting a fresh and healthy breakfast, but unfortunately the experience was very disappointing. The food felt heavily processed and low quality rather than fresh or homemade. The only things we actually enjoyed were the spinach and the eggs.  Everything else tasted artificial and overly processed — especially the sausage, the hash browns, and the piece of meat that came with the meal. Instead of feeling like a healthy breakfast, it felt more like frozen or pre-made food heated up and served quickly. The presentation online and the atmosphere gave us completely different expectations.  For the prices they charge, we expected much better ingredients and much fresher food. Sadly, the meal left us feeling disappointed and unsatisfied. We would not come back and cannot recommend this place if you are looking for a genuinely fresh and healthy breakfast experience."
> **- The Guy 007 (1/5/5)**

> "Toast is a great spot for brunch with a wide and varied menu. There are plenty of choices, including healthy options and delicious vegetarian dishes, so everyone can find something they’ll enjoy.  The food is fresh, well-prepared, and very tasty, making it a perfect place for a relaxed brunch. The atmosphere is pleasant and welcoming, and the service is friendly and attentive.  A lovely place to enjoy a quality brunch, whether you’re looking for something indulgent or healthy. Highly recommended!"
> **- Kiymet Duran (5/5/5)**
