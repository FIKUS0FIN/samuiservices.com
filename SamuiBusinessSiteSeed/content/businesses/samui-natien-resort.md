> "The room (No.: 403) was dirty on arrival, but after we reported it to the reception, they cleaned it tomorrow morning and sincerely apologized for the inconvenience. After that, it was kept clean every day. The breakfast was delicious, with a great selection of fresh fruits and vegetables, and a nice mix of European and Thai dishes. We really enjoyed the variety and the beachside location of the restaurant. The staff was always helpful, kind and polite with us. The male receptionist spoke very good English and made check-in and check-out smooth and easy. The hotel team made us feel welcome throughout our stay. The à la carte food was also delicious, especially the Thai dishes we ordered. Presentation and portion size were both satisfying. The beach was clean and good, even though the water had a bit of algae. Compared to other beaches on the island, this one was still one of the better and calmer spots. We really appreciated being able to relax there. The happy hour cocktails were tasty and came with a good discount, a perfect way to enjoy the sunset by the sea. The only thing I didn’t like was the coffee at breakfast, it was quite bad, and there was no alternative milk (lactose-free or plant-based options). This would be easy to improve for guests with dietary needs. Altogether, it was a very good experience. A lovely hotel with a beautiful tropical garden, nice rooms, and friendly staff. I would definitely recommend it."
