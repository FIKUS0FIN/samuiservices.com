## Top Reviews
> "The drive up: saw some reviews talking about how terrifying it was and it almost put us off, but so glad we went. The 4x4 shuttle feels safe and the roads are smoothly paved - it’s just super steep up/down. Definitely don’t attempt the drive yourself.  Order immediately upon arrival if having food. Took 50 mins from we ordered and it wasn’t even busy. The food was mixed but I enjoyed my green curry dish. You can also go just for a drink, they have a huge selection.  Overall: The views are amazing and I recommend going for sunset to see it both in daylight and lit up. The restaurant itself is lovely and worth a visit if only for a drink.  Also: book in advance. Would be a shame to make it up there and have to turn around. We were a group of 11 (5 small kids under 5yo and they still gave us an amazing table with “proper” chairs - not bean bags)."
