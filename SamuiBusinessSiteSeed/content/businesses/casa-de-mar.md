## Top Reviews
> "Great quiet hotel walking distance from the city center (~20 min). My room was very comfortable, I had everything I needed. Water was provided every two days (2 bottles 0.5l). Beach towels and cool beach bag offered. Small private beach terrace apart from the pool area (you can always find a sunchair there) with good access to the ocean (shallow, but with high tide you can even swim). Service was just amazing, I would rate the staff six out of five, if I could! So helpful and always sincerely friendly! I just did not like the resto that much but there is plenty of choice for the meals nearby."
> **- Tatiana Sergienia (5/5/5)**

> "Lovely small beachfront hotel. The staff were all amazing! Jinny took daily care of the rooms in an excellent way. Location is good, on a quiet part of the beach. The busy town is about a 30 minute walk or 5 minutes by taxi/scooter. Ocean access is good and the beach chairs placed on a veranda near the pool. Pool has a perfect temperature and is swimmable. The ocean in front of the hotel has very low water for a big stretch, so safe for kids, but not ideal for swimming. Food and drinks quality is high standard. Breakfast has good variety and they make nice coffees."
