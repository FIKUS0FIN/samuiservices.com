## Top Reviews
> "We had a mixed experience at Cape Fahn Hotel.  The good: The room was beautiful, very well maintained, and the private pool was large and really enjoyable. The breakfast was nice, and the spa massage was excellent and reasonably priced.  The less good: Getting to and from the hotel is quite complicated. There are boat transfers only until 11 PM, and if you return later than that you need to inform the reception in advance. Some staff members made us feel uncomfortable about this instead of handling it pleasantly. There was also a woman working at the pool who was rude and unfriendly, which really affected the atmosphere.  In general, throughout the stay there was often a feeling that guests were expected to keep spending more money, which made the experience feel less relaxed and welcoming.  We also asked for a late check-out. Of course we understand that this is never guaranteed, but the hotel seemed half empty and it felt like there was no real effort to accommodate the request.  The pool and facilities are nice, but not as impressive as we expected from this level of hotel.  Overall, if the hotel itself felt truly exceptional in every aspect, the complicated access might have been worth it. For us, unfortunately, it wasn’t, and we would not return."
