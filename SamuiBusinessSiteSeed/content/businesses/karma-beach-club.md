## Top Reviews
> "Breakfast and food are typical hotel standard — nothing special. We stayed for three days without breakfast before, and that was a much better option, as there are many excellent breakfast spots in town. The hotel’s Pad Thai was worse than any street version, even those costing around 70 baht.  There are definitely too many outside guests, which makes the place very crowded. Jet skis operate constantly along the beach, making it difficult to relax, and we were even afraid to swim or float on an inflatable mattress because they ride very close to the shore.  We came to this hotel to relax, but instead we found crowds during the day and an atmosphere full of show-off type guests."
