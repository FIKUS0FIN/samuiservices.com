Equipment was prepared in advance based on sizing, was good quality and set up and changed over for you which was an unexpected bonus. Lunch was delicious, prepackaged local Thai dishes I had Pad-sew and I think it might have been the best one I had all trip. After only recently completing my Advance Course I felt safe and well looked after. They didn’t automatically supply a dive watch but when I requested one they happily accommodated with a spare. The dive itself was worth the trip. By far my favourite dive to date with exceptional amounts of life at the rock. Jo shot GoPro footage and supplied quickly after which was another appreciated value add. I also really valued that when a fellow diver chewed through his air quickly (approx 20mins) Jo managed the situation professionally and took the diver back to the boat without cutting our dive short. Thanks for a great experience."
