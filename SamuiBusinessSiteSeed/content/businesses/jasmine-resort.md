## Top Reviews
> "The room was extremely outdated, nothing like what is shown in the photos online. There were blood stains on the sheets. That was unacceptable. You’ll see it in my pictures. The pictures make the hotel look modern and stylish, but in reality they are very misleading. When I booked, the room was advertised as having a garden view, but the actual view was a wall, completely blocked. The air conditioning when set on low or medium, it made constant loud noise, making it impossible to sleep. I was forced to either turn it off, which made the room uncomfortably hot, or set it on the highest setting, which made the room freezing. There was no comfortable or quiet option. I also tried several items at the restaurant, and the food was barely average. Nothing stood out, especially considering the price point. Overall, there was no value for the money paid. I would definitely not stay here again and would not recommend this hotel to others."
