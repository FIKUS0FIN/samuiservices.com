## Top Reviews
> "This is a great stay, we enjoyed it, only rating 4 stars because setting your expectation is the most important thing to enjoying the stay and a few minor things. We stayed in the farm view bungalow which was lovely, rustic and so close to nature, exactly what we wanted. The experience is closer to camping (as per the place name) than a bungalow. The hut didn’t have space for our backpacking bags inside when we slept and the mosquito net had lots of holes in (and the replacement one we asked for) so we used our own. Minor things but important to know going into it.  If you want to camp this is 5 stars! Massive value for money. Location is great if you want to enjoy the peaceful quiet side of Samui away from the parties and main towns. You can rent a scooter on site which you’ll definitely need (although confirm the price before you do as there was a miscommunication with ours). The staff are so friendly and helpful, they even do transfers to the local points which is good value. Ice creams on site is a big bonus!  All in all not perfect, but who is, and the staff try very hard to make it a really good stay, so I’d stay there again!"
