## Top Reviews
> "Quiet place tucked away close to a few local food markets and many massage places. It consists of approx. 10 small detached bungalows, one per family. Our stay included breakfast (a choice of Thai, continental and English i think) served at the restaurant overlooking the sea. There is a small swimming pool which may be suitable for kids rather than adults. Beach beds with parasols are provided. Rooms are cleaned every day, all have ceiling fans, tv and air-conditioning, veranda. Bathrooms are modern. Make sure you close doors in the evening as there are some mosquitos especially towards evening hours. The service is great and accommodating.  There are 3/4 parking spaces which was plenty as we were the only guests with a car."
> **- Natalia Percival (5/5/5)**

> "What a wonderful resort. Great location, real standout is the amazing people. Special mention to King and Zaw who made me feel so welcome.
