> "Very unassuming and chilled out seaside bar and restaurant. Small but very friendly crowd of locals and expats and there was a lovely relaxed atmosphere. The owner and his wife both took the time to introduce themselves and chat with us which we appreciated. There is seating inside the restaurant or on the beach and they have a separate bar area showing sports. There were a lot of dogs lounging around which may put some customers off but as my wife and I are both dog lovers this just enhanced the homely family vibe.  The informal atmosphere did not prepare us for the food which was genuinely fantastic. I had the grilled white snapper with the mango salad and chips (nice and chunky) and it was delicious. Wifey ate the seafood salad and we both had the exact same dish on our second visit. They also do Sunday roasts here but we missed it this time round."
