## Top Reviews
> "The owner/chef greet us with a big smile at the entrance. He was welcoming and made us feel like home straight away. The food was amazing. He made me on request the beef caparcio burrata full of flavor even half portion was enough. My husband got the deal of the day and had green seafood curry and was also super rich and delicious. We got free dessert (fried ice cream) and some drink for free as it was our honeymoon. Definetly one of the best experience we got in Koh Samui. If you are French the guy will chat with you all night, being from Nice himself. Go check it out"
> **- Sabrina Roussel (5/5/5)**

> "Restaurant looked lively, good background music. We opted for the Thai 390 thb fixed menu with a starter and a main. Overall: Bland. Nice presentation. Spring rolls were deep fried and overly crispy, so much so that they were tough to bite and even tougher to cut with a knife. I don’t think we’ve ever had a spring roll wrapper like these. Chicken satay was four nice size skewers but on the dry side; the “peanut sauce” was not. Was very thick and had no peanut flavor even through it looked like it was filled with bumpy bits. Pad Thai was average, nothing special.  Chicken with cashew not very exciting. Staff spoke English. They accept credit cards, you have to go up to the bar to pay with a card. All in all, even tho the restaurant is across the road from us, we’ll probably walk over to the Night Market for more flavorful Thai dishes."
> **- Kele Coloma (3/5/5)**
