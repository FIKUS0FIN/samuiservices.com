## Top Reviews
> "Got 2 pizzas for 3 people, pepperoni, and seafood (around 350 and 450 baht respectively before taxes and service). Seafood was bland. Pepperoni was good. Pizza size is medium. Had to ask for plates (and even then we were given only 2). Prices are exclusive of vat and service charge with add up to a hefty 17%. Not good enough service for an asked price."
> **- Alex (3/5/5)**

> "We enjoyed a romantic dinner at the L’Ocean with the dishes served both fresh and flavoursome. Only downside was that the fish was rather small and the drinks were a little pricey when factoring in the near 20% tax and service charge to the cost. Two drinks cost over 700 baht so if you fancy a few it will be a relatively expensive evening by Thai standards. The table setting was beautiful with views out to the sea. The wait staff were attentive without being overbearing and all in all we had a very enjoyable evening. Highly recommended if you are looking for a romantic setting with a warm, laid-back ambience."
> **- Alfred Romeo (4/5/5)**

> "Average food with a nice view. The service is wonderful, however the quality and presentation of the food is lacklustre. I would suggest trying the pomelo salad."
