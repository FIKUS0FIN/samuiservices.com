## Top Reviews
> "Very solid Neapolitan-style pizza spot in Koh Samui. From the moment you walk in you can see they take pizza seriously. The wood-fired oven is visible in the kitchen and you can watch the team preparing the pizzas fresh, which is always a good sign.  The pizzas were huge, much larger than expected. The dough had a nice airy crust with good charring from the oven, and the ingredients felt authentic and good quality. We tried the Napoli and the Diavola and both were very enjoyable. The tomato sauce and overall flavor could be slightly more intense or balanced, but overall it’s still a very good pizza, especially considering you’re on a tropical island.  The place itself is cozy and nicely decorated, with Italian flour bags on the walls and a relaxed dining atmosphere. Service was friendly and efficient.  Price-wise it’s a bit on the higher side for Thailand (around 400–420 baht per pizza), but the size definitely helps justify it.  Overall I’d rate it 8.5/10. A great option if you’re craving proper Neapolitan pizza while in Koh Samui."
> **- Alexander Cillan (5/5/5)**

The restaurant features extensive beverage menus, as well as a comprehensive wine list.  Outdoor dining area also available.  The friendly and welcoming staff add a charming personal touch, as well as the hospitality from the owners who engage mostly in making the pizzas themselves.  A must try for all the pizza lovers when in Koh Samui. An establishment of true Neapolitan flavours proudly made by Thai hands."
