## Top Reviews
> "We made a reservation and were lucky enough to get a table outside right by the water — what a setting! The service was excellent, attentive and friendly throughout the evening. The food was outstanding. The steak was cooked to perfection and the burger was absolutely delicious — easily some of the best we’ve had. Yes, prices are on par with what you’d expect back in Europe, but given the quality of the food, the service, and that stunning waterfront atmosphere, it’s absolutely worth every penny. Highly recommend if you’re looking for a premium dining experience in Fisherman’s Village!"
> **- Yannick (5/5/5)**

> "We had a great spot at beachfront dining at El Gaucho.  We had a White Asparagus and Tuna Tartare for appetizers. Both were delicious. We were guided about out meats and fish selections . Everything was cooked to perfection and delicious! Pretty pricy dinner without alcohol."
> **- Nanda Sundri (5/5/5)**

> "Had dinner at El Gaucho which is a last minute plan as we did not have reservations anywhere. It’s a pleasant surprise, service is very good and attentive. Can really taste the freshness of the ingredients. Though is a steak restaurant, the lobster bisque is good. Can taste the umami freshness."
