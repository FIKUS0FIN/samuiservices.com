## Top Reviews
> "We had an amazing brunch here, everything was honestly top-tier. The place itself is beautiful: very clean, minimal, and tropical, with a super relaxing atmosphere. Sitting outside surrounded by greenery made it even better — calm, sunny, and perfect for a slow brunch.  The food was excellent. The eggs were perfectly cooked, creamy and full of flavor, served on great quality bread with fresh ingredients. The balance between the richness of the dish and the fresh salad on the side was spot on. You can really tell that attention is paid to both taste and presentation.  Drinks were also great, especially the iced coffee — refreshing, well-balanced, and ideal for a warm day. Everything came out well presented and appetizing.  Service was friendly and smooth, not rushed, which really adds to the experience. Overall, it was a very enjoyable brunch, great food, great vibe, and a place I’d definitely come back to and recommend without hesitation."
> **- Mister Benz (5/5/5)**

> "A hidden gem. The coffee was delicious and I loved that I got to choose which beans I could have for my espresso.  Naturally I went for the dark roast and that was divine. The pancakes with fruit and the the eggs Benedict were sumptuous.  I went keto with eggs, bacon and spinach — the surprise was the very well spiced spinach.  Don’t miss it.  P.s The service especially from the entire team was warm, welcoming and friendly. Traditional Thai hospitality (which isn’t always the case in the busy high season) mixed with modern charm.  P.s.s yes, almost empty cup photo - was so good 😊"
