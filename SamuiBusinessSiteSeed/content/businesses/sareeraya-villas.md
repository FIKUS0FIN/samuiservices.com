If we had known, we wouldn’t have booked them — at least not at that price. •	Once inside the room, it was immediately clear that the space was nowhere near 70 m². After raising the issue, the hotel sent two engineers to measure it. Their result confirmed my own quick measurement: 49 m². •	I was told that all rooms sold as 70 m² are in fact this size. So anyone booking these suites should expect roughly 50 m², not 70. •	I asked for a price adjustment to match the real size. Instead, I received a bottle of wine. Nice gesture, but it doesn’t fix the fact that I paid for a larger room than I got. •	To be clear: the staff is amazing. The ladies at the check-in were exceptionally kind, helpful, and welcoming. My issue is only with the misleading room description and the reluctance of management to correct the pricing.  Bottom line: Great people, but the advertised room sizes are simply not accurate. If you expect a true 70 m² suite, you won’t get it — and the rooms along the road are far from the quiet experience you might expect at this price point."
