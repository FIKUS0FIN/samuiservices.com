## Top Reviews
> "We had such a great experience at this beach bar and honestly everything about the day was just perfect.  First of all, the location is amazing. You’re literally right in front of the sea with a beautiful view, comfortable sunbeds, and a super relaxed atmosphere. The beach itself is clean, the water is clear and very pleasant to swim in, and there are also nice pools available which were really well maintained. Being able to switch between the sea and the pools made the whole experience even better.  The food was a very pleasant surprise. I ordered the chicken pad thai and it was excellent — perfectly cooked, flavorful, and very generous in portion. You can tell it’s made fresh, with good quality ingredients and great balance of flavors. Definitely one of the best pad thais I’ve had at a beach bar.  The drinks were also fantastic. We tried a couple of fresh juices and they were delicious — refreshing, well prepared, and perfect for a hot beach day. Exactly what you want when you’re relaxing by the sea.  Service was friendly and efficient, the staff spoke good English which made everything easy, and the overall vibe of the place is just chill and enjoyable. It’s the kind of spot where you can spend the whole day: swim, eat, relax, have drinks, and just enjoy the beach.  Overall, a really great beach bar with good food, great drinks, nice pools, and a beautiful sea view. Highly recommend spending a day here if you’re in the area — we’ll definitely come back."
