## Top Reviews
> "This Cafe was recommended by our host for breakfast and we ate here most days. The coffee was excellent, we usually had an iced cappuccino. Every juice we tried was delicious and the meals were so full of flavour. There is a great choice of Thai foods with a bit of a Melbourne twist. The kids menu had lots of fun options, each kids drink came with marshmallows. The service was friendly and professional. The water and ice are filtered so you can drink without worry."
> **- Rebecca Smith (5/5/5)**

> "Amazing coffee places with an extraordinary large menu, full of never-seen-before drinks and dishes. I had a Melbourne style sourdough toast with a folded chili omelet, salmon, fermented cauliflower and a goatcheese espuma and it's truly one of the best sourdough toast i've ever had! The coffee, as stated in lots of reviews, is fabulous indeed."
> **- Marie Laurence Demeyer (5/5/5)**
