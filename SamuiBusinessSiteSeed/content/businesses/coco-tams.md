## Top Reviews
> "Visited Coco Tam’s in Fisherman’s Village for dinner. By the time we arrived, most of the popular seats were already taken, so we could not get the exact spot we originally wanted. However, we ended up dining on the second floor, which actually turned out to be more comfortable and had an even better view.  The food quality was excellent. The menu seemed to lean toward Italian-style dishes, and everything we ordered, including the pasta and burrata cheese, was surprisingly good in both flavor and quality. The cocktails were also enjoyable, and they had the usual beer options as well. Overall, the food was definitely one of the highlights of the visit.  The atmosphere was another strong point. Arriving around sunset made the experience even better because the changing colors of the sky were beautiful. Later in the evening, there was also a fire show, which added a fun and memorable touch to the night. Since we were seated upstairs, the music was not overwhelmingly loud, which made the environment more relaxing.  One thing to note is that there is a minimum spending requirement of 2,000 baht. This could feel a bit expensive for lighter eaters, but for my wife and me, it was completely reasonable.  The only disappointing part was the service. Considering how large and busy the restaurant is, it is understandable that the staff are constantly occupied, but the overall attentiveness and quality of service felt a bit lacking at times."
> **- Dave (5/5/5)**

> "A place that’s absolutely worth visiting when you’re in Fisherman’s Village.  They offer incredible fire shows — honestly the best ones we saw in Thailand. The atmosphere is amazing, and it’s a great experience for all ages.  A few important things to know before you go:  1. If you don’t have a reservation, try to arrive at least 1–1.5 hours early to get a good spot. 2. They don’t always accept reservations for future dates — it seems to depend on availability and their own flexibility. 3. Fire shows don’t happen when it rains, so keep that in mind. 4. Smoking is allowed on the beach. 5. Payment is required immediately when ordering (not at the end). Credit cards are accepted. 6. Outside seating only offers the pizza menu.  Highly recommended — definitely one of those places you shouldn’t miss, and I think people of all ages will enjoy it. You won’t be disappointed!"
