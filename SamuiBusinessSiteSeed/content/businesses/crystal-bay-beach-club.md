We walked away disgusted by the sheer lack of professionalism and the shameless money-driven mentality. This is not a beach club, it’s a cash-grab machine.  Silver Beach is stunning, but this place in particular should be avoided at all costs if you’re expecting even the slightest bit of respect or hospitality."
