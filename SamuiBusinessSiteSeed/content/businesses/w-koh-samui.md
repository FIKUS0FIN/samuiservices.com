The W beach is standout with the ability to see both sunrise and sunset clearly. Given its on a small peninsula you can also get shelter from the wind regardless of which direction its blowing (predominantly was a mild easterly during our stay).  Food was excellent, and the breakfast buffet was the best Ive had. We also loved the Sip Bar on the beach - special thanks to Ting who works at the bar and was always friendly and up for a chat.  The layout of the resort is great, with a bit of elevation allowing for sweeping views from the restaurant and main resort pool. The onsite spa is of very high quality and has a large sauna which is free to use. I also think W is located well within Koh Samui - not too far away from the main tourist attractions (Fishermans Village just down the road).  If you are looking for a luxury escape to Koh Samui, I highly recommend the W!"
