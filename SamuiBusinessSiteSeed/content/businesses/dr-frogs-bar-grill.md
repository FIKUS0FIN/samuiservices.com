## Top Reviews
> "We came for a roast dinner on Christmas Eve and firstly, the views and design of the restaurant are beautiful. The food was good but we don’t think it was quite worth the price point (this was a Christmas set menu so appreciate there will be an uplift on the price). The staff were friendly but service was pretty slow. All in all a good place to come for a roast if you are willing to pay the price point."
> **- Kyah G (4/5/5)**

> "A perfect place to enjoy a meal with a view. We went for Sunday Roast. As it was our first visit, we both had the mix Roast, with every meat. Next time I think I will go for the porchetta. The plate was full and plenty of sauce on the side. The taste was really good, everything from the meat to the sauce and the vegetables was perfect! Tiramisu was also on point! Will definitely be back, both for Roast and to try the normal menu as well.  Recommend to book before as it was full when we were there."
> **- André Falkenhaug (5/5/5)**

> "One of the best meals we had in Koh Samui. Had to go back a second time to try their Roast Dinner which is one of the best I’ve ever had.  Only small gripe could possibly be the location as it is a bit isolated and hard to get a cab from.  Regardless, highly recommended!"
> **- Mark Gordon (5/5/5)**
