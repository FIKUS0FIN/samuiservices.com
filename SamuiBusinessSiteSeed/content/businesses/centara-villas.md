> "Nice quiet location, perfect to relax. Anu and her team were excellent, taking care of us for 7 days at the bar and in the restaurant. We stayed in the family suite which was very big so plenty of room for our family of 4 with a nice plunge pool. Perfect for when you want to kick back in the room to relax and the kids wanted to keep swimming.  Beautiful well kept gardens throughout the property and lots of stairs which didn’t bother us at all but something to consider if mobility is an issue.  Also an extra bonus was having an awesome little beach bar right next door “cool bar”, highly recommend that place too. Easy enough to get a Grab to/from Lamai and Chaweng."
