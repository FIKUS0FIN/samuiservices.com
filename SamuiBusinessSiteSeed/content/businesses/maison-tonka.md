## Top Reviews
> "Visited this café today with my husband and infant to escape the heat and enjoy something a little different.  The waitress was friendly and attentive. She brought over iced water while I waited for my husband to arrive and kindly accommodated my request for some plain bread for my infant. My smoothie was also very nice.  Unfortunately, the experience was overshadowed by the quality of the bread that was served. When it arrived, it was extremely dry and stale, to the point that I initially thought it had been toasted. Upon closer inspection, I noticed several blue-green specks within the bread that appeared consistent with mould.  When I raised my concerns with staff, I was told that the colouring was simply a characteristic of the “leavened bread.” However, the specks were blue-green in colour rather than the beige, white, or brown tones typically seen in sourdough or other artisan breads. This explanation did not address my concerns.  What was most disappointing was that the bread had been served for an infant. A simple replacement with a fresh slice would have gone a long way towards resolving the situation and reassuring us that food safety was being taken seriously.  My husband owns and operates two successful cafés, so we understand the importance of quality control and handling customer concerns appropriately. For that reason, we found this situation particularly disappointing and unacceptable.  I hope this feedback is taken constructively and helps prevent a similar experience for future customers."
