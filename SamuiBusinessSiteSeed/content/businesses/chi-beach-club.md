## Top Reviews
> "If you’re on the strip in Ko Samui and want somewhere that actually has a bit of life to it, this beach bar is easily the standout. Based on the time I spent in Samui, it was the only spot in the area that consistently has atmosphere — relaxed during the day, then gradually picking up energy into the evening without ever becoming chaotic.  The music is one of its strengths. Instead of blasting one genre, it is changed each day which adds variety. In the time I was there this included a local DJ in the day and then a singer who set the mood for dinner.  The service was great throughout the time we spent there. The staff are attentive and welcoming. The way in which they present themselves demonstrates a clear passion to cater to customer needs.  Food-wise, it’s better than you’d expect from a beach bar. The menu is broad — proper breakfasts, hearty English comfort food, solid Thai specials, pizzas, and more casual bites. Portions are reasonable (not oversized tourist traps), and the meals feel balanced rather than greasy, rushed or inconsistent. The quality of the food was noticeably high, and you can tell they care about presentation and ingredients.  That said, it is on the pricier side for the area. You’ll definitely notice you’re paying more than at some nearby local spots or the general average on the island. Whether it’s “worth it” depends on what you’re after — but in fairness, the quality of the food, the beachfront setting, and the overall experience do justify the premium.  If you want good food, decent music, and a proper beach atmosphere rather than just a quiet row of chairs and speakers, this is the place on Ko Samui that delivers and we will definitely return next time we are in the area."
> **- Louis Lamb (5/5/5)**
