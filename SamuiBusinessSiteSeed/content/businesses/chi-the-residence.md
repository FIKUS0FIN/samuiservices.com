> "We were in general ok staying here for a month. Service ladies and people in general, especially the gardener who sings outside, were cute and helpful. Managing team was ok. Water quality was almost all the time pretty bad though, and even if there are fancy baths you might not want to lie down in brown water.  Facilities in our villa (2nd floor) were good quality and we didn’t really lack anything. If something was broken they fixed it straight away. Cleaning was impeccable too.  What really left a bitter taste was a bit of nickel and diming. You pay 3k for a monthly apartment and then they charge 10 dollars for a chipped plate, and say they counted electricity extra for the last night in case we used a lot. We had to check out at 6:45 in the morning for our flight and we informed them in advance.  They also stood there counting all the pillows and each spoon in front of us to make sure we didn’t take anything. Pillows, umbrellas, beach bag too. That made us feel very uncomfortable. At 6:45 we did not expect someone knocking on our door, checking again and telling us it is time to check out and basically forcing us to leave immediately, so we ended up standing on the street with our luggage. Nice start of the day.  Location is in the middle of nowhere so you have to know you will have to drive everywhere. No one informed us that the beach bar would be closed this month, we only found out from the bartenders later. We were told we have 20 percent off there but it was closed anyway.  It is premium in terms of amenities, we were glad to have a pool for ourselves, and tenants living here were lovely people."
