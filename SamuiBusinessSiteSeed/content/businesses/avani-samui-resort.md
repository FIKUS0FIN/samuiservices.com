## Top Reviews
> "We had a pleasant stay at Avani+ Samui Resort, and the true highlight of this hotel was definitely the staff. Every single person we interacted with was kind, welcoming, caring, and genuinely amazing. They really elevated the entire experience and made us feel very comfortable throughout our stay.  The room itself was beautiful, modern, and comfortable, with great amenities around the property. Since we stayed for 5 nights and unfortunately had rain almost nonstop for 4 of those days, we ended up spending a lot of time enjoying the pool, gym, and the pool/ping pong tables, which helped keep us entertained.  That said, there are definitely areas that need improvement, especially for a hotel that markets itself as a 5-star resort. The shower pressure was inconsistent and quite weak, making it difficult to properly wash shampoo and conditioner out of our hair. The fixed shower head was also leaking and spraying water everywhere, so we mostly relied on the detachable shower head instead.  The biggest disappointment was the food. We would strongly recommend skipping the breakfast, as it was very underwhelming the few times we tried it. The hotel restaurant in general was overpriced and unfortunately the food quality did not match the prices ( a bad cold cheeseburger and club sandwich were 1000 THB). After our first few meals, we ended up eating elsewhere for the rest of the trip. Do yourself a favor and drive 10–20 minutes to enjoy amazing local restaurants with far better food and prices.  Overall, this felt more like a solid 4-star hotel rather than a true 5-star experience, but the exceptional staff really made the stay worthwhile."
