## Top Reviews
> "Really good gym and good price. Great selection of equipment, large airy building. No air con but I went in hot season and was fine as they have many fans. Definitely my favourite gym in Samui. Friendly staff too. I’m not sure why someone gave a bad review because of the state of equipment because it’s absolutely fine. The equipment is in good condition!"
> **- Tracey Wyatt (5/5/5)**

> "I regularly train here in badminton. During the hot season, it gets quite warm inside, but I’ve gotten used to it. I like the flooring, and the lights don’t glare into my eyes while playing. The court rental is 200 baht, and a personal training session with a coach costs between 700-1000 baht per hour. You can always buy drinks and snacks here, and there’s also a service for restringing racket strings.  Cons: The area in front of the building is full of potholes, and there’s no option to take a shower."
> **- Black Bird (4/5/5)**

> "Great gym. Very well equipped! You can do any type of training from functional to CrossFit to kettlebells. Dozens fans which is great for me but no AC, if that means something to you. Branded protein powder is very good and half the price of popular brands. Staff is pleasant. The lady “boss” not so much. But over all it’s one of the best gyms in Samui. No bro culture women feel comfortable here. Prices are reasonable."
> **- Caliph Herald (5/5/5)**
