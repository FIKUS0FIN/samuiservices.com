> "This was our first time coming to Koh Samui and we were looking forward to starting our trip with a resort stay. Unfortunately White Sand Samui Resort did not hit the mark, how they are able to qualify themselves as a 4 star hotel is beyond us, they were 2 star with a pool.  Pro - On Lamai beach. Airport transfer went smoothly.  Con - All staff were inadequate and lacked basic customer service. There appeared to be no management on site, the front desk staff are not capable of dealing with anything. Rooms seemed run down and needed to be refreshed/renovated. Pool and beach did not have enough seating, other guests would “reserve” their sun lounges with their towels and then never use the lounges, there were numerous times we baked in the sun waiting for someone to remove their items because they decided they didn’t want to use the lounges in the end. Or we would be waiting for people to leave so we could swoop in. There were times it was just us 2 in the pool and no one else around yet every sun lounge had someone else’s towels? We never dined in the restaurant purely because our interactions with bar staff were dull and slow, the menu didn’t look appealing and we ate elsewhere. When we came back after dinner around 9pm we asked the Front Desk if we could have some drinks and we will take them to the room, we were told no that everyone had gone home. There were 2 people at the Front Desk, what is the issue with getting 2 beers from the fridge and charging it to our room? I’ve never stayed anywhere that has denied a sale. The hotel is showing signs of aging and neglect.  Neutral - this is at a quiet end of Lamai, if you wish to be somewhere quiet then it is perfect. Note it’s about a 20+ minute walk to get to Lamai centre itself where the markets and shops are.  We are returning to Koh Samui in May 2026, and we have chosen to stay somewhere else. I wouldn’t recommend this resort to anyone."
