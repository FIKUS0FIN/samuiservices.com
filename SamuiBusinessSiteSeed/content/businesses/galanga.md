> **- M C (5/5/5)**

> "Great atmosphere with second-level seating that’s perfect for a bit of elevated people-watching. The space itself is beautiful — a thoughtful mix of old Thailand references woven into the interior design. It feels intentional, warm, and visually rich without being overdone.  The staff truly stands out here. Attentive, kind, and consistent with every interaction. You feel taken care of from the moment you walk in, and that kind of service makes a difference.  The starters were okay — perfectly fine, just missing that extra depth of flavor you hope for. The main dish landed similarly. Not bad by any means, just a bit underwhelming in terms of boldness and complexity. The presentation, though? Absolutely stunning. Every plate looked like it belonged in a feature spread.  There’s a decent wine selection and solid drink menu to round things out.  At the end of the day, the staff and location carry this place. If you walk in knowing the food is good but not groundbreaking, you’ll probably have a next-level experience simply because the ambiance and service elevate everything else.  A beautiful spot to spend an evening — just hoping for a bit more punch in the flavor next time."
> **- Ryan McGowan (3/5/5)**
