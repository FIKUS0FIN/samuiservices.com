> "Nice beach restaurant in a great location, but it’s definitely more on the touristic side rather than a local experience. The food was good, nothing bad at all, just nothing particularly incredible or memorable either.  The main highlight is the setting — right on the beach — and a big bonus is that the place next door does fire shows, which you can easily enjoy from the terrace while having a drink or meal.  It’s a convenient spot and a pleasant place to stop, especially if you’re already in the area, just don’t come expecting authentic local food or a hidden gem. One practical downside: the restaurant is quite large but there’s only one shared toilet, which can be a bit inconvenient when it’s busy.  Overall, a decent, easy option for beachfront dining with a nice view and entertainment nearby."
