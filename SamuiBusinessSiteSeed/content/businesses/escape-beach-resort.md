## Top Reviews
> "This hotel is absolutely fantastic. The bungalows are modern, well equipped with very comfortable beds. We didn’t have a beach front bungalow, but the complex is small so this isn’t an issue. Cleanliness is very good, with housekeeping everyday.  The pool area is great with a good, but little pricey bar with good cocktails. The breakfast is a small buffet, but the food quality is good. Staff service is very good and they are friendly and polite.  The highlight here is the pool and views right on the beach. Location isn’t amazing if you don’t have a scooter and there is a HUGE hill to get to the hotel."

The only positive aspects for us were the view of the sea and friendly ladies working at the cafe.  There are a lot of young families with kids who are very active and loud around the beach/pool area. So keep it in mind if this is not the environment you need.  The hotel is located quite far away from everything, so scooter is recommended as walking would take around 30 minutes to the main road."
