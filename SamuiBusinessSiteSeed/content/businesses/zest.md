## Top Reviews
> "Zest Restaurant at the Conrad Koh Samui serves up a view that’s absolutely five stars—perched high on a mountain with sweeping ocean vistas framed by lush green hills. It’s the kind of setting that makes you want to linger long after the meal, cocktail in hand, just soaking it all in. Service here fluctuates between solid 4 and standout 5 stars, depending on your server. I’ve had both immaculate, attentive service and also the kind that’s just “fine”—pleasant but not particularly memorable.  Now, this review is specifically for dinner, and let’s be honest: it leans more toward 3 stars in that department. For the price point, the food needs a lot more punch. I had to send dishes back asking for flavor (yes, flavor), and when they returned, they had more seasoning—but were now lukewarm, clearly just doctored up rather than freshly remade. Pro tip: if you like your food with Thai flair, be upfront about wanting bold flavors. The kitchen seems a bit shy about delivering them.  What bumps this review up to 4 stars is the breakfast. The buffet is extensive and genuinely impressive—with everything from fresh coconuts to flavorful Thai dishes that finally show what the kitchen is capable of. It’s a great way to start the day, and if dinner could catch up to breakfast in both flavor and consistency, Zest could truly live up to its spectacular setting"
