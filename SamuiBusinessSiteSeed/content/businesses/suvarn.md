## Top Reviews
> "Suvarn is easily the best cocktail experience I’ve had in Samui.  From the moment you walk in, the place feels beautifully elegant and refined, with an atmosphere that makes the whole evening feel a bit special. It’s stylish without being pretentious, and the setting really adds to the experience.  The staff are quick and professional, and the cocktails are honestly pure fantasy. The presentation, the balance of flavors, and the creativity behind each drink are on another level. You can really tell a lot of thought and skill goes into every glass.  What surprised me most is that the prices are more than reasonable considering the quality of the drinks and the fact that you’re in Southern Thailand at such a high level cocktail bar.  If you love great cocktails, this is a must visit spot in Koh Samui."
