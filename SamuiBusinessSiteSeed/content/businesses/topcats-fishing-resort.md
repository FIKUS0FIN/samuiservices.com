> "If you are looking to book an amazing fishing experience on Koh Samui look no further! This place is absolutely amazing. Simon, Larry and Sarah make you feel so welcome from the moment you walk in the door, nothing was too much trouble. The atmosphere is professional but fun at the same time, you quickly feel like part of the Top Cats family there. The guides know really their stuff, are enthusiastic and good fun to spend time with (thank you Doi, Tadpole and Mai Mai). The gear is good quality branded kit and stands up to the goliaths in the lake really well. The variety and size of the fish stocked is amazing and they are all in such great condition due to the care that the guides take with them. We all understand fish care is important, you only have to see the state of some of the fish in European commercial fisheries, but these guys take proper care of their stock and it shows in the quality of the fish you will catch. They have taught me a few lessons to take back to my own club in the UK for sure. Three of us fished over 3 days and had over 3,200lb of fish including a 180lb Arapaima, 100lb Mekong Cats, 90lb Siamese Carp, Asian and Amazonian Redtail Cats, Alligator Gar, Leopard Cats, Ripsaw Cats & Pacu. We stayed on site and our accommodation was lovely and clean, with everything we needed. The onsite catering is delicious, portion sizes are generous, great Thai and English options, the staff friendly and attentive and the bar is well stocked. The pool is a bonus to cool off in after a hard fight in the sun. What more could you ask for? As a family we have fished 4 continents and Top Cats stands out by far as the best place we have ever been. We can't wait to go back!"
