## Top Reviews
> "Absolutely incredible place. You can see how well taken care of these elephants are. You can feed them and delicately touch them if they allow you too, nothing is really forced. I saw the elephants playing with the staff and I could see how much they like them. You can learn so much being there. Price - 900 baht, that includes feeding and spending as much with animal as you wish to. I’ve visited other sanctuaries and they were also nice but liked this one the most. I highly recommend the visit, especially that you support the sanctuary in this way. Each elephants needs 200kg food daily so they are not that easy to take care of. They do an amazing job there 🤍"
