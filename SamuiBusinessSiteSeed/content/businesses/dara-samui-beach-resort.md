## Top Reviews
> "The Dara Samui Hotel is right at the entrance of the party area on Chaweng Beach, so you can be in the middle of the action in 5 to 10 minutes. The property is not the newest, but very well kept. Rooms are very clean, with big showers and large bathrooms. The staff is super friendly, always helpful, and cheerful. The beach is a bit small but still clean and with a nice view and invited you for long walks around the shore. The whole place is quiet, not noisy. The pool area is lovely, with a top  level ocean view from where you can look down at the beach area - extra plus for the nice wide view. One thing to know: it’s under a flight path, so you can hear planes maybe 6 to 12 times a day. But it starts in the morning and stops around 8 or 9 PM. Honestly, after a couple of days, we barely noticed, and it’s cool to watch them. Overall, we are super happy: great value for money, breakfast was good, had all we needed—we didn’t need 30 kinds of toast and Jogurts. Variety is on spot. It was perfect for us! We have been here from end april to first half of may 2026"
> **- Thorsten Lückemeier (4/5/5)**
