## Top Reviews
> "Authentic Thai Vibe, Amazing Food & Chill Atmosphere ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ If you're looking for a genuine, laid-back local experience rather than a fancy restaurant, this is the place! The atmosphere is unbeatable – sitting on bean bags right by the water, just soaking in the island vibe. The food is high quality and delicious. While they offer great Western options (like pizza and burgers) for those who prefer them, I highly recommend trying their Thai dishes to get the real authentic taste – the flavors are just on point! Another huge plus is their flexibility: the menu states that any order can have vegan substitutions, which was great for us. The staff is super friendly and the portions are generous. Highly recommended for a perfect, chill meal!"
