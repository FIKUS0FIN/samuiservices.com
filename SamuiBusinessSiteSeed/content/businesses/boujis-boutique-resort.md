## Top Reviews
> "We stayed at Boujis in February 2026 and had such a comfortable and genuinely hospitable experience.  The views from the rooms are absolutely extraordinary, vast stretches of the moody, ever-changing ocean that feel almost cinematic. We woke up to some of the most spectacular sunrises right from our room. Those quiet early morning moments overlooking the sea will stay with us for a long time.  The property is located in Chaweng Noi, right along the main road. I would definitely recommend renting a bike so you can easily explore nearby cafes, beaches, and spots around the island. The rooms are perched on a hill, so be prepared for a bit of climbing each time you step out , but honestly, the views make it worth the extra steps.  The rooms themselves aren’t ultra-modern or luxurious, but they are clean, comfortable, and perfectly functional for a relaxed island stay.  What truly makes this place special, though, is the people. Ice, Noom, and Nee at the front office were outstanding ,incredibly responsive, thoughtful about guest comfort, and always willing to help with very genuine care. The housekeeping team was also wonderful and attentive as well. It’s the kind of place where the warmth of the staff elevates the entire experience.  We left feeling well looked after  and very grateful. Would highly recommend this place."
