## Top Reviews
> "A very nice hotel, located in a quiet area. We had a room on a higher floor with a view of the bay. The restaurant is excellent. Finally, a place where you can get tasty sausages for lunch, as well as waffles and pancakes made right in front of you. The pool is very nice and clean. The only downside is that the jacuzzis are out of order, except for one. The pool bar has reasonable prices (290 baht). There's not much beach, but the sea slopes gently and is clean. A larger beach is just 100 meters to the left or right, so it's all good. The staff is very welcoming and attentive. They do everything they can to ensure that everything goes smoothly. Bicycles are not allowed on the premises, which is a shame."
> **- christian Kuppi (5/5/5)**

> "Great hotel situated between Nae Nam and Bo Phut so it was easy to walk to both, we tended to walk towards Bo Phut as there were plenty of restaurants on the way to Fisherman's Village.  The hotel has a selection of suites and villas set in nice gardens. We stayed in a pool villa which was marvellous with a large courtyard with sunbeds, sala with daybeds and pool. We have stayed in many pool villas and the pool is normally around 3m by 6m, the pool in our villa was apx 4m by 9m, exceptionally nice and it meant it was possible to have a proper swim, I only went to the main pool for Aquafit. Plenty of storage for clothes, so much that very unusually we didn't use it all.  Breakfast nice, as well as the buffet the cook would cook things to order (very nice and helpful lady). As well as the standard juices it was possible to order from 9 different cold teas and coffees.  The three young waitresses (Aern, Game and Praewa) were great as were all the staff.  Obviously our villa was cleaned daily and at the same time our pool was serviced and courtyard swept and plants watered. We had a few different maids as our normal maid had time off and they were all great however our normal maid (Fon?) looked after us really well and deserves a 🙏👍.  3 minutes walk to the nearest restaurant, 10 minutes to Tops supermarket, 18 minutes to the start of the restaurants towards Fisherman's Village,  20 minutes to Garden Restaurant (our favourite), 35 minutes to Fisherman's Village.  We tended to go out for meals to help keep other places going, however the meals we had at the hotel were fine, no problems.  Very nice hotel and holiday, nice cheerful helpful staff and the villa pool was exceptional 👌.  No problems at all in recommending it and we hope to return in the future."
> **- Carl Greenall (5/5/5)**
