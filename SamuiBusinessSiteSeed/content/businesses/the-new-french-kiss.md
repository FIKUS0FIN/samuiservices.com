## Top Reviews
> "The New French Kiss sets a high bar—and clears it comfortably.  Tony is an exceptional host. No theatrics, just sharp awareness, good timing, and genuine care. You feel looked after without it ever becoming intrusive. That’s professional hospitality.  The food is superb. Well-balanced, properly executed, and clearly built on quality ingredients rather than gimmicks. Each plate lands exactly where it should—confident and clean.  The setting does a lot of heavy lifting too. The view is outstanding, and it elevates the whole experience from “good meal” to something more memorable. At one point I was even fishing directly from the restaurant—caught a squid, returned it (we’d already eaten and were heading out), but it says everything about the place. It’s not just dinner, it’s an experience.  Bottom line: The New French Kiss isn’t trying too hard—it just gets it right."
> **- Anura Hotel (5/5/5)**
