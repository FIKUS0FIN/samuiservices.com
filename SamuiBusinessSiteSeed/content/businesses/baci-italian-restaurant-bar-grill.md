> "I had the pleasure of dining at Baci Italian Restaurant in Koh Samui and also meeting the owner, Tiziana, a truly wonderful lady with 33 years of culinary experience and life on the island. Listening to her story and passion for Italian cuisine made the evening even more special. We started with a seafood salad, and it was outstanding. Every component was cooked perfectly tender, fresh and full of flavor. You can immediately tell that she knows exactly where to source the best fresh seafood in Thailand, which makes all the difference. The seafood spaghetti al cartoccio was another exceptional dish. The sauce was beautifully balanced, rich but not heavy, allowing the flavors of the seafood to shine. When I opened the bread crust of the cartoccio the aroma was incredible. I even used the bread to finish every drop of the sauce simply delicious. To finish the dinner we had a chocolate mousse, which ended the meal perfectly. The restaurant itself is beautiful, elegant and welcoming, with a lovely wood-fired oven that immediately shows the passion and professionalism behind this place. You can feel that this restaurant is built with experience, dedication and love for authentic Italian food. If you are looking for one of the best Italian restaurants in Koh Samui, Baci is absolutely a must visit. Highly recommended."
> **- Luca Foschi (5/5/5)**
