> "My stay at Mantra Resort was definitely memorable, and what I liked most about the place was the excellent service. They treat you incredibly well and really take care of you - I even got a free room upgrade! The uphill infinity pool is stunning, offering incredible views over the gulf. They have two room types available: Studio Style: This is their signature room. They are really lovely, modern, and have a beautiful decor and vibe. Apartments: Located near the pool. While the apartments themselves are good, the location can be a bit sketchy. When it rains, it gets very slippery up there so be very careful. That being said, a few things bothered me during my stay, namely the constant AC issues and power cuts. To their credit, they get fixed very quickly. But there was a night where the issue lasted quite a while and I was even given a replacement room for the night when it rained heavily and the generator went out, which I really appreciated. However, I wish I didn't have to ask for the room change myself; I would have preferred if management recognized the issue quickly and offered it upfront. Overall, I’d give it a solid rating for the staff's attention, care, and quick fixes. But the power cuts and AC issues need a full audit and permanent fix so future guests don't have to deal with them."
> **- Nader Alqalaa (4/5/5)**

> "We had a great stay at the Mantra Samui Resort and would definitely recommend it.  The staff were without exception incredibly friendly, helpful, and welcoming.  The room very spacious, we had an ocean view with a private terrace, sun loungers, and a beautiful sea view.  The pool area is lovely and looks just like the pictures. The pool is very large, there are plenty of sun loungers, and we never had trouble finding a free one. Pool towels are provided.  The restaurant is also nice, with good food and drinks. Breakfast, however, has some room for improvement. It felt a bit uninspired at times. The quality was okay, partly à la carte and partly buffet. The coffee was very good, but overall breakfast does not quite reach a 5-star standard.  Regarding the location: the hotel is logically situated higher up the hill. With a scooter, everything is easily reachable, and for us the location worked very well. Scooters can be rented directly at the hotel. The road up to the hotel is quite steep, so you should already have experience riding a scooter.  Overall, a clear recommendation."
