## Top Reviews
> "The place is newly open (I think) all the facilities are very well maintained and clean. Staffs are mostly Myanma so they cannot speak Thai. So if you are Thai or locals you might need to speak English with them.  I tried their signature drink today and it’s very enjoyable and refreshing.  Pool is nice and clean.  The only downside in my opinion is that they have no parking lots but plenty of street parking available. :)"
> **- Setthaporn Junhavitthaya (5/5/5)**

> "Enjoyable lunch with a view at this chillaxing beach club in Bang Por. Coffee is good as well as their range of cocktails. The place is readily equipped with set of loungers and seating areas to relax and immerse in the beach side ambience.  There is also bathroom available for showers; although I must describe the shower room could be better equipped / designed for areas to stow bags and towels, and places to put personal amenities on, despite the spacious interior - for true convenience of things.  Otherwise, a chic beach club serving good menus and drinks; with friendly staff and beautiful seafront setting in Bang Por beach."
> **- Wunda Stone (4/5/5)**
