> "I recently had brunch here and overall it was good, but not quite excellent. The food was definitely enjoyable — I had the avocado toast with poached eggs and smoked salmon, and everything was fresh and nicely presented. The flavors were good, but for the price, I was expecting something a bit more memorable or flavorful. It was tasty, just not particularly exceptional.  The setting itself is really nice. The place has a beautiful atmosphere, open space, and a relaxed vibe that makes it pleasant to sit and have brunch. That part was definitely a highlight.  However, the service could be improved. When I arrived, no one explained where to wait or how the seating worked. There seemed to be a list or a queue, but it wasn’t clear who was waiting for a table or how long the wait would be. I ended up standing around on my own without any guidance, and no one came to tell me roughly how long it would take or what I should do. It felt a bit confusing and unorganized.  Once seated, I also noticed that everything took quite a while. I understand that brunch places can be busy, but there was waiting for almost everything. The meal itself took around 30 minutes to prepare, and overall it ended up taking close to an hour just to have brunch. Again, not terrible, but a bit long.  When it came time to pay, the interaction felt a bit awkward too. I went to the counter to settle the bill and it wasn’t very smooth — I almost had to confirm myself that I wanted to pay, which felt a little strange. Nothing dramatic, just not the most attentive customer service.  So overall: good food, nice location, pleasant atmosphere — but the service and organization could definitely be improved, and for the price I expected a bit more flavor and efficiency. Still a good experience, just not an exceptional one."
> **- Mister Benz (4/5/5)**
