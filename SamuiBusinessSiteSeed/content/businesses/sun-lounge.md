> "Sun Sea is a hotel, hostel and restaurant in one. It’s awesome. I came here for dinner at sunset for the very reason of the views and to watch the airplanes come into the airport nearby. This place is clean and a big area for people to dine and work if they need to. There are bean bag chair tables as well but they are first come first serve. There is great table seating to watch the water, sunset and planes. If you love this type of thing this place is amazing! We came a Friday night and there was live music too! The menu is quite large, offering salads, burgers, pizzas, pastas, fish and large pints (with a great cocktail menu too!) For 2 people with a few beers and 2 dinners it was approx $68 CAD. The view alone is worth this! I’d like to thank the staff who served us, they were so friendly and awesome. Thanks for making this an even more memorable experience!"
