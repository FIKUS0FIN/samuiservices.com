## Top Reviews
> "The Butcher is easily one of the best Western restaurants in Lamai. The steaks were cooked properly, the meat quality was noticeably better than most places on the island, and the portions were generous. The atmosphere feels busy and lively without being too loud or pretentious, and the staff were friendly and efficient even when the restaurant was packed. Prices are higher than average for Lamai, but for imported beef and the overall quality, it feels justified. Definitely a great spot if you want a proper steak, burger, or comfort food meal in Koh Samui."
> **- Kyrio Azure (5/5/5)**
